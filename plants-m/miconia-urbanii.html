Miconia urbanii

Miconia urbanii, commonly known as the urbanii miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are often followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
