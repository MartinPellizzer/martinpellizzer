Miscanthus × longiberbis f. ogiformis

Miscanthus × longiberbis f. ogiformis, commonly known as the ogiformis form of the silver feather grass, is a decorative ornamental grass native to China.

It features tall, slender culms with silky, silver-tan foliage that adds a striking visual element to gardens. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish-white panicles that bloom in late summer, enhancing its ornamental value. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

This cultivar is valued for its graceful appearance and low maintenance requirements.
