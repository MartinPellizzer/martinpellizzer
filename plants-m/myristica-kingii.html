Myristica kingii

Myristica kingii, commonly known as the King's nutmeg, is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree, Myristica fragrans. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and red, fleshy berries that contain a single seed. Its seeds are valued for their aromatic properties and are used in traditional medicine.

The tree is also notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
