Metalasia barnardii

Metalasia barnardii, commonly known as the Natal grass or Natal silver grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-blue foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture, while its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters.

Metalasia barnardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
