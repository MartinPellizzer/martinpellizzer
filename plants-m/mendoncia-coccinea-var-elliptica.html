Mendoncia coccinea var. elliptica

Mendoncia coccinea var. elliptica, commonly known as the red flame bellflower, is a striking flowering plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is heightened by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
