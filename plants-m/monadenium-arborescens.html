Monadenium arborescens

Monadenium arborescens, commonly known as the tree-like monadenium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree with a distinctive, branching stem that resembles a tree. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Monadenium arborescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
