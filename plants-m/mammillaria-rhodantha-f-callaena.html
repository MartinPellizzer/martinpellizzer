Mammillaria rhodantha f. callaena

Mammillaria rhodantha f. callaena, commonly known as the Callaena mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with numerous small, white to pale pink tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
