Mionandra prostrata

Mionandra prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate mionandra, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

Mionandra prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
