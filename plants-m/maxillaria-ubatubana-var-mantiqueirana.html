Maxillaria ubatubana var. mantiqueirana

Maxillaria ubatubana var. mantiqueirana, commonly known as the Mantiqueira maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are borne on terminal inflorescences. It prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
