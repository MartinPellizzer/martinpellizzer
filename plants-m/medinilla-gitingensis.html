Medinilla gitingensis

Medinilla gitingensis, commonly known as the Giting Mountain medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant inflorescences. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland to montane forests.

Due to its restricted distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
