Mimulus nevadensis

Mimulus nevadensis, commonly known as the Sierra primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

Mimulus nevadensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
