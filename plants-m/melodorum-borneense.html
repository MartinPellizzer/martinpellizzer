Melodorum borneense

Melodorum borneense, commonly known as the Bornean melastome, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and often grows in shaded, moist forest understories. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Melodorum borneense is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its native habitat.
