Mirbelia densiflora

Mirbelia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered mirbelia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant features dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are usually small, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is part of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability.
