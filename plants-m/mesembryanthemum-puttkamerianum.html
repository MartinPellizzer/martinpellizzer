Mesembryanthemum puttkamerianum

Mesembryanthemum puttkamerianum, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry or oro blanco, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its fleshy leaves and flowers that often open in response to sunlight. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and unique fruit structure.
