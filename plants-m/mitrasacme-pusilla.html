Mitrasacme pusilla

Mitrasacme pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf mitrasacme, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and undergrowth. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of pubescence. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a pale yellow or greenish hue.

Mitrasacme pusilla is notable for its compact size and its role in supporting local insect populations.
