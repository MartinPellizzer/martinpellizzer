Meliosma sirensis

Meliosma sirensis, commonly known as the Siren's Mist.

It is a rare flowering plant native to the wet tropical forests of Southeast Asia. The plant features delicate, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct metallic sheen. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters, emitting a faint, sweet fragrance.

Due to its limited habitat and endangered status, it is highly valued in botanical conservation efforts.
