Macrotyloma chrysanthum var. occidentale

Macrotyloma chrysanthum var. occidentale, commonly known as the golden cluster bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are typical of many leguminous species. The plant produces small, spherical pods that contain edible seeds used in various cuisines. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both agricultural and ecological contexts.
