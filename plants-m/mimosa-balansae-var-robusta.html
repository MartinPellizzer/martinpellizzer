Mimosa balansae var. robusta

Mimosa balansae var. robusta, commonly known as the robust mimosa, is a shrub native to parts of South America.

It features bipinnate leaves that are soft and feathery, with each leaflet being small and delicate. The plant produces yellow, globular flower heads that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for study in ecological and horticultural contexts.
