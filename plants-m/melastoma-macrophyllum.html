Melastoma macrophyllum

Melastoma macrophyllum, commonly known as the broad-leaf melastoma, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
