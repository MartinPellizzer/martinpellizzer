Medinilla rubicunda

Medinilla rubicunda, commonly known as the red-flowered medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of vibrant red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Medinilla rubicunda is also valued for its fragrant blossoms and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
