Meliosma arnottiana

Meliosma arnottiana, commonly known as the Australian meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests and along stream banks.

Meliosma arnottiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
