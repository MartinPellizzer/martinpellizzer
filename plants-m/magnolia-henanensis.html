Magnolia henanensis

Magnolia henanensis, commonly known as the Henan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval-shaped and have a smooth texture. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
