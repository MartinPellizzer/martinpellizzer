Melicope aneura

Melicope aneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandpaper tree, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and dark green, narrow leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing yellowish-white blooms, while its fruits are round and fleshy.

Melicope aneura is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
