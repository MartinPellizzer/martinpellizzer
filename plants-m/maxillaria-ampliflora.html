Maxillaria ampliflora

Maxillaria ampliflora, commonly known as the broad-flowered maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a wide, showy lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with intricate patterns and a distinctive, broad-shaped lip.

Maxillaria ampliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
