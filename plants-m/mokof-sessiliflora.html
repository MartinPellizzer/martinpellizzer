Mokof sessiliflora

Mokof sessiliflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mokof, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, displaying resilience to drought conditions.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
