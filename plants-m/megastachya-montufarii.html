Megastachya montufarii

Megastachya montufarii, commonly known as the Mexican flame grass, is a striking perennial plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red bracts that resemble flames. The plant produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
