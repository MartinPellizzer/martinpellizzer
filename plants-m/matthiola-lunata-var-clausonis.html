Matthiola lunata var. clausonis

Matthiola lunata var. clausonis, commonly known as the Clauson's stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with small, four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, featuring slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale purple, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
