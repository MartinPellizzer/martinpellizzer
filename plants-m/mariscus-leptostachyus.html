Mariscus leptostachyus

Mariscus leptostachyus, commonly known as the slender spike rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often in temperate regions. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear small, spike-like clusters of flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Mariscus leptostachyus plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various small organisms.
