Monachosorum × arakii

Monachosorum × arakii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Monachosorum and Arakii species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structures and vibrant coloration. The plant typically features tall, slender stems with clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil, while its leaves are usually narrow and elongated.

This hybrid is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
