Miconia fleischeriana

Miconia fleischeriana, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a large, evergreen tree that can grow up to 20 meters in height, characterized by its broad, palmate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into bright red, fleshy fruits resembling starfruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
