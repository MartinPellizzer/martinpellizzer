Microsteira argyrophylla

Microsteira argyrophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf microsteira, is a small, herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silver-gray leaves that reflect sunlight, aiding in water conservation. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
