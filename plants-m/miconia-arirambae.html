Miconia arirambae

Miconia arirambae, commonly known as the Ariramba miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that are often surrounded by showy bracts. It is a member of the Melastomataceae family, which includes many tropical shrubs and trees.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
