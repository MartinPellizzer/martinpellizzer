Michelia foveolata var. xiangnanensis

Michelia foveolata var. xiangnanensis, commonly known as the Xiangnan Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often marked with shallow fissures.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aromatic foliage and showy blooms.
