Morisonia macrantha

Morisonia macrantha, commonly known as the showy morisonia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of clusters of pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Morisonia macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
