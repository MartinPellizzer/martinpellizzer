Mammillaria cielensis

Mammillaria cielensis, commonly known as the Cielensis Pincushion Cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular shape with white, woolly spines and a pale green to bluish-green stem. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Mammillaria cielensis is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
