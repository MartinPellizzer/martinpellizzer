Miconia rufibarbis

Miconia rufibarbis, commonly known as the scarlet-barbed miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia rufibarbis is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and can form dense thickets in its natural habitat.
