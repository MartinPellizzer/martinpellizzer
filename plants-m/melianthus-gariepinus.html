Melianthus gariepinus

Melianthus gariepinus, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a star-like appearance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Melianthus gariepinus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
