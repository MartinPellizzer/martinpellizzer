Masdevallia dermatantha

Masdevallia dermatantha, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its striking, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining substrates.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
