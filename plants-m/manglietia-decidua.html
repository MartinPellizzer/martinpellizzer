Manglietia decidua

Manglietia decidua, commonly known as the white manglietia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Magnoliaceae family, known for its ancient lineage and primitive floral structures. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, white flowers with a strong fragrance. It typically grows as a tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.
