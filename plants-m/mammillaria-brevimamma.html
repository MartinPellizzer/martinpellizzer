Mammillaria brevimamma

Mammillaria brevimamma, commonly known as the short-mammilated hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a diameter of about 5 to 10 centimeters. Its surface is covered with short, white to pale yellow tubercles, which are clustered into radial ribs. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
