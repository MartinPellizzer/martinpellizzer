Maerua stenogyna

Maerua stenogyna, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a woody stem and is drought-tolerant, making it well adapted to arid environments. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soils.

The species is also valued for its potential in erosion control and traditional medicine.
