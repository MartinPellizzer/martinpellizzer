Myrtus minor

Myrtus minor, commonly known as dwarf myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant features small, dark green leaves with a fragrant, resinous scent when crushed. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible.

Myrtus minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
