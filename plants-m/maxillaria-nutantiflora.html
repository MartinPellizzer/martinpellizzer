Maxillaria nutantiflora

Maxillaria nutantiflora, commonly known as the drooping maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, pale green to yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are strap-like and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
