Mucuna mollissima

Mucuna mollissima, commonly known as velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, bean-like pods. The plant produces yellow flowers and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in agricultural systems for soil enrichment.

Its seeds contain high levels of protein and are used in both traditional and modern applications.
