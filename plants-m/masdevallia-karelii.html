Masdevallia karelii

Masdevallia karelii, commonly known as the Karelian masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces delicate, pendulous flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation, typical of its mountainous habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native environment.

Masdevallia karelii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display and relatively easy care requirements.
