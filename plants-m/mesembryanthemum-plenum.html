Mesembryanthemum plenum

Mesembryanthemum plenum, commonly known as the full bloom mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and summer, producing bright yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
