Maba nigrescens

Maba nigrescens, commonly known as the black maba tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dark, almost black bark, which gives it its common name. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and has elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small yellow flowers followed by orange-red fruits containing triangular seeds.

The plant is valued for its timber and has traditional uses in local medicine.
