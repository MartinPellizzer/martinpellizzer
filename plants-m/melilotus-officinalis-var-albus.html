Melilotus officinalis var. albus

Melilotus officinalis var. albus, commonly known as white melilot, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

White melilot is used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop due to its nutritional value.
