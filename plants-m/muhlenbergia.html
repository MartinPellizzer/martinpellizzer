Muhlenbergia

Muhlenbergia, commonly known as pink muhlenbergia, is a genus of grasses native to the Americas.

These plants are characterized by their tufted growth habit and often have bluish or silver-gray foliage. Many species produce fluffy, pinkish or white seed heads that are visually striking. They are typically found in a variety of habitats, including meadows, prairies, and rocky soils.

Muhlenbergia species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
