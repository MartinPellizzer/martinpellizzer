Miconia resimoides

Miconia resimoides, commonly known as the glossy miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy on top with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Miconia resimoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
