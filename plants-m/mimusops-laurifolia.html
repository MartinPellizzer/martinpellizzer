Mimusops laurifolia

Mimusops laurifolia, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in winter, giving it its common name. The tree has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
