Micranthemum callitrichoides

Micranthemum callitrichoides, commonly known as water pennywort, is a small aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has tiny, opposite leaves that are often less than 1 cm in length. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white.

Micranthemum callitrichoides is often used in aquariums due to its hardy nature and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
