Mikania scandens var. subcymosa

Mikania scandens var. subcymosa, commonly known as the creeping Mikania, is a variant of the Mikania scandens species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, twining stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by fluffy, white seed heads that aid in wind dispersal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and invasive potential make it a notable species in ecological studies.
