Mentha pulegium f. sphenoides

Mentha pulegium f. sphenoides, commonly known as narrow-leaved pennyroyal, is a variant of the pennyroyal plant characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs known for their essential oils. This form is distinguished by its more compact growth and narrower foliage compared to the typical pennyroyal. The plant produces small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in clusters.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its medicinal and aromatic properties.
