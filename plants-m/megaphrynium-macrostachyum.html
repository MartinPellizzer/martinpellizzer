Megaphrynium macrostachyum

Megaphrynium macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright yellow spadices surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The rhizomes of this plant are also used in some traditional culinary practices.
