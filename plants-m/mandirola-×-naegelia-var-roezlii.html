Mandirola × naegelia var. roezlii

Mandirola × naegelia var. roezlii, commonly known as the Roezlii hybrid, is a rare and intriguing botanical hybrid that combines traits from Mandirola and Naegelia species.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, which features broad, leathery leaves with a striking silver-green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its limited natural range and cultivation challenges, it is considered a specialty plant among horticulturists and botanical enthusiasts.
