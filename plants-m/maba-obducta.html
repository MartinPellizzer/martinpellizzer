Maba obducta

Maba obducta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed maba, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually narrow and elliptical. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its fruits are capsule-like structures containing seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its timber and medicinal properties.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
