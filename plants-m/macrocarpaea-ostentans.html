Macrocarpaea ostentans

Macrocarpaea ostentans, commonly known as the showy macrocarpaea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
