Mimusops voalela

Mimusops voalela, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain hard, angular seeds. It is valued for its durable timber and is often used in traditional medicine.
