Monadenium schubei var. formosum

Monadenium schubei var. formosum, commonly known as the Desert Star, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It features a compact, branching structure with fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in drought conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the rainy season, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resilience in low-maintenance gardening.
