Maxillaria brenesii var. longiloba

Maxillaria brenesii var. longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed brenesian orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically features long, narrow petals and a spurred lip, which are typical traits of the Maxillaria genus. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
