Mammillaria sartorii

Mammillaria sartorii, commonly known as the Sartorii Hedgehog Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dense covering of small, white to pale green spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
