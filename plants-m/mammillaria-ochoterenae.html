Mammillaria ochoterenae

Mammillaria ochoterenae, commonly known as the Ochoterenae Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and a dense covering of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
