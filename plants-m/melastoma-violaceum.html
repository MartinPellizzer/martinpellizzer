Melastoma violaceum

Melastoma violaceum, commonly known as the purple melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually purple or pink in color, which bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
