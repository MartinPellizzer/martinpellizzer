Madhuca selangorica

Madhuca selangorica, commonly known as the Selangor mahua, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy on the upper surface. Madhuca selangorica is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits.
