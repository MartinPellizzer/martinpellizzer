Maxillaria spathulata

Maxillaria spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming compact clumps with pseudobulbs. Its flowers are often arranged in racemes and bloom during the warmer months.

Maxillaria spathulata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
