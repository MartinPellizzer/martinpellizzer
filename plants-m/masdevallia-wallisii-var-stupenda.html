Masdevallia wallisii var. stupenda

Masdevallia wallisii var. stupenda, commonly known as the Wallisii Stupenda orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its native habitat with seasonal variations.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively compact growth habit.
