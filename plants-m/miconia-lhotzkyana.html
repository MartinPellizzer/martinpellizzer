Miconia lhotzkyana

Miconia lhotzkyana, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. It produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries.

The species is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain environments.
