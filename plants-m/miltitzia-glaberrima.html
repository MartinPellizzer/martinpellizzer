Miltitzia glaberrima

Miltitzia glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth miltitzia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
