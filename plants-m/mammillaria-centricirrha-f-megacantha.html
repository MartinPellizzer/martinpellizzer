Mammillaria centricirrha f. megacantha

Mammillaria centricirrha f. megacantha, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in prominent, white to pale yellow tubercles. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is prized for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is particularly noted for its large, robust spines and dense growth habit.
