Miconia steyermarkii

Miconia steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. It produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species.

Due to its ornamental value, it has been introduced to other parts of the world, though it can become invasive in some environments.
