Maxillaria rubropunctata

Maxillaria rubropunctata, commonly known as the red-spotted orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are showy, with a pale pink to white coloration and distinctive red or orange spots on the petals and sepals.

The plant's botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural and scientific study.
