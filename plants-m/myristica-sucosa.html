Myristica sucosa

Myristica sucosa, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Banda Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and fragrant, yellow flowers. The plant produces a unique fruit that contains two distinct seeds: the outer seed is the nutmeg, while the inner seed is known as mace. Myristica sucosa is valued for its aromatic essential oils and is widely cultivated for its spice products.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both commercial and ecological contexts.
