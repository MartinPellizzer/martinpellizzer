Melastoma longiramense

Melastoma longiramense, commonly known as the long-stemmed melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Melastoma longiramense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
