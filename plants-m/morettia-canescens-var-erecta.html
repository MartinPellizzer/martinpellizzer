Morettia canescens var. erecta

Morettia canescens var. erecta, commonly known as the erect morettia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, showy flowers. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces pendulous, fragrant blooms in shades of white and pale yellow.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.
