Melaleuca squamophloia

Melaleuca squamophloia, commonly known as the silver-leaf melaleuca, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and wetland areas. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, scale-like structures. Its flowers are small and usually pink or white, arranged in spikes along the stems.

Melaleuca squamophloia is known for its aromatic foliage and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
