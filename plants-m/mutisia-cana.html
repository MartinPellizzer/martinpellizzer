Mutisia cana

Mutisia cana, commonly known as the white mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, white to pale yellow ray florets surrounded by tubular disc florets. It typically grows in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and shrublands.

Mutisia cana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
