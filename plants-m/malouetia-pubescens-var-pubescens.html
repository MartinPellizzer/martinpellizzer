Malouetia pubescens var. pubescens

Malouetia pubescens var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Malouetia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pubescent (hairy) stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
