Miltonia spectabilis var. rosea

Miltonia spectabilis var. rosea, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, pinkish-red flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features showy, pouch-like blooms with a distinctive lip that resembles a slipper, typical of the Miltonia genus. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting mix.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
