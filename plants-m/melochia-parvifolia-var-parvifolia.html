Melochia parvifolia var. parvifolia

Melochia parvifolia var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved melochia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically small and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures containing numerous tiny seeds.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and resilience in various soil conditions.
