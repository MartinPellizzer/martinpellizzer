Mauria ferruginea var. obtusifolia

Mauria ferruginea var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the rusty mauria, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a fine, rusty-colored pubescence. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardening.
