Matucana yanganucensis var. albispina

Matucana yanganucensis var. albispina, commonly known as the white-spined matucana, is a unique species of cycad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces distinctive, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Its leaves and cones are covered in a thick, waxy cuticle that helps protect it from environmental stress.

This variety is notable for its striking white spines that adorn the leaf bases, making it a visually distinctive cycad species.
