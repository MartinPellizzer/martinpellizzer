Moricandia papillosa

Moricandia papillosa, commonly known as the silky cress, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Moricandia papillosa is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and is considered a weed in some agricultural settings.
