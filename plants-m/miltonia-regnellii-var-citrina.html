Miltonia regnellii var. citrina

Miltonia regnellii var. citrina, commonly known as the Citrine Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle pink or red tinge. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

This orchid is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in both home and greenhouse settings.
