Mammillaria Albilanata Subsp. Tegelbergiana

Mammillaria albilanata subsp. tegelbergiana is a rare and striking cactus native to Mexico. It is known for its unique white to pale green spines and rounded, segmented body. This subspecies thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its beauty makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in controlled settings.
