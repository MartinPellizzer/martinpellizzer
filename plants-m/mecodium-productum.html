Mecodium productum

Mecodium productum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of velvety, hairy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, indoor environments. Its botanical features include a short, thick stem and a preference for well-draining soil.
