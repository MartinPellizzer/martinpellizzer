Mertensia bakeri var. amoena

Mertensia bakeri var. amoena, commonly known as the pink mertensia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features striking lavender-pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland and streamside habitats, preferring partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
