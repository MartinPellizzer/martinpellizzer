Meliosma ferruginea

Meliosma ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, rusty-colored pubescence. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
