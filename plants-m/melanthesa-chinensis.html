Melanthesa chinensis

Melanthesa chinensis, commonly known as Chinese yarrow, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and its clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant is perennial, often growing in rocky or sandy soils, and is adapted to temperate climates. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic properties.

Melanthesa chinensis is used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
