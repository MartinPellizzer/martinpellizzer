Maxillaria henchmanii

Maxillaria henchmanii, commonly known as the Henschmann's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescence. The flowers are typically pale pink to white with a distinctive dark lip, and they bloom during the dry season. This orchid prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
