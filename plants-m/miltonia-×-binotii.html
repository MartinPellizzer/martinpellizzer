Miltonia × binotii

Miltonia × binotii, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns resembling slipper shapes. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters, producing a striking visual display during the warmer months.

The species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
