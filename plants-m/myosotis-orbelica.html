Myosotis orbelica

Myosotis orbelica, commonly known as the Alpine Forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in mountain meadows.

Myosotis orbelica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
