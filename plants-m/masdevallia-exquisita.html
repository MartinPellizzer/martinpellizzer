Masdevallia exquisita

Masdevallia exquisita, commonly known as the exquisite masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers with a distinctive hooded lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires high air humidity and good drainage for optimal growth. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette, and it produces small, delicate blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Masdevallia exquisita is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
