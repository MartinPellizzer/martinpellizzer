Michelaria × villosa

Michelaria × villosa, commonly known as the silky michelaria, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. While not widely cultivated for ornamental purposes, it is occasionally grown in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and ecological interest.
