Maasia

Maasia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and dandelions. The plants typically have showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pink petals. They are often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.

Maasia species are valued for their ornamental beauty and are commonly used in gardens and floral arrangements.
