Mammillaria nickelsiae

Mammillaria nickelsiae, commonly known as the Nickels' mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, radial spines and yellowish areoles. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
