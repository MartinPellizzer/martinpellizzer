Mediolobivia atrovirens

Mediolobivia atrovirens, commonly known as the dark-green leaf vine, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its deep green, velvety foliage.

Its leaves are elongated and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture in humid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, shaded areas with high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
