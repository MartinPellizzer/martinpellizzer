Melandrium ornatum

Melandrium ornatum, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil. Its blooming season occurs in late winter to early spring, making it a notable plant for winter gardens.

The species is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
