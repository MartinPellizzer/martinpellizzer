Melastoma semicrenatum

Melastoma semicrenatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, with five petals and prominent stamens. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Melastoma semicrenatum is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.
