Mammillaria discolor var. curvispina

Mammillaria discolor var. curvispina, commonly known as the twisted spines mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem with a gray-green to blue-gray coloration. The plant is characterized by its unique, curved spines that give it its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.
