Maxillaria lineolata

Maxillaria lineolata, commonly known as the striped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with dark purple petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria lineolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
