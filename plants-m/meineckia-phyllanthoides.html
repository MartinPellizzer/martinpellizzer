Meineckia phyllanthoides

Meineckia phyllanthoides, commonly known as the African sandmat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

Meineckia phyllanthoides is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine.
