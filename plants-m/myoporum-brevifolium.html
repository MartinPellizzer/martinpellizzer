Myoporum brevifolium

Myoporum brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by blue-black berries. It is often found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Myoporum brevifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
