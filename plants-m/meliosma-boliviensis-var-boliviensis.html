Meliosma boliviensis var. boliviensis

Meliosma boliviensis var. boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

This species is often found in humid, shaded environments within montane forests.
