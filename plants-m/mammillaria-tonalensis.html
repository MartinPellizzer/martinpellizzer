Mammillaria tonalensis

Mammillaria tonalensis, commonly known as the Tonalensis bunny ear cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and white to pale green spines. The plant features small, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive, almost fuzzy appearance. It produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body.

This cactus is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
