Monadenium lindenii

Monadenium lindenii, commonly known as the desert star or desert rose, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. Its flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

Monadenium lindenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
