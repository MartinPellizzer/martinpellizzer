Malva dilleniana

Malva dilleniana, commonly known as the woolly mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, and it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant is characterized by its hairy, broad leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, brown seeds.

Malva dilleniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
