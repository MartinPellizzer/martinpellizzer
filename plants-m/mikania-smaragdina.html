Mikania smaragdina

Mikania smaragdina, commonly known as Emerald Mikania, is a fast-growing vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by spherical, greenish-brown fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
