Myagrum stylosum

Myagrum stylosum, commonly known as the slender pandanus, is a tropical plant native to northern Australia and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its foliage is often used in traditional cooking and as a natural dye.

Myagrum stylosum thrives in wet, humid environments and is often found near water bodies.
