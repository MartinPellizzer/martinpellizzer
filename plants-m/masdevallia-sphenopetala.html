Masdevallia sphenopetala

Masdevallia sphenopetala, commonly known as the star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a contrasting purple or violet lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Masdevallia sphenopetala thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high light intensity for optimal flowering.
