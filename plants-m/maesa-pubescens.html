Maesa pubescens

Maesa pubescens, commonly known as the hairy maesa, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and compact growth habit. Maesa pubescens is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
