Megaphrynium distans

Megaphrynium distans, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to the Amazon basin and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix and a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. Its rhizomes are edible and have been used traditionally for their starchy content.

The plant thrives in wet, shady environments and is often found in swampy areas.
