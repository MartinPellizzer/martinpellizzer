Myosotis ronnigeri

Myosotis ronnigeri, commonly known as the Ronniger's forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the genus Myosotis, which includes various species of forget-me-nots, and is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Myosotis ronnigeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators.
