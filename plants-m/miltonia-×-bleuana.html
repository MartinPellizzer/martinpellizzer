Miltonia × bleuana

Miltonia × bleuana, commonly known as the Blue Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, dark green leaves that form a rosette, supporting its showy inflorescences. Its flowers are typically blue to violet in color, with intricate patterns and a velvety texture.

This orchid thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
