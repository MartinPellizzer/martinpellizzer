Maxillaria rauhii

Maxillaria rauhii, commonly known as the Rauh's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a pseudobulbous plant with erect, hairy stems and narrow, strap-like leaves. The flowers are typically yellow with purple or red markings, and they bloom in summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate for orchid enthusiasts.
