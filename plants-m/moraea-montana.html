Moraea montana

Moraea montana, commonly known as the mountain moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, with a distinctive, spurred petal.

Moraea montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
