Marattia oreades

Marattia oreades, commonly known as the mountain fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Marattiacae.

It is characterized by its large, compound fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a typical feature of ferns.

Marattia oreades is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance.
