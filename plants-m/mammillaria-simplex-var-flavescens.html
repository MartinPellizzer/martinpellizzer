Mammillaria simplex var. flavescens

Mammillaria simplex var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique coloration and compact size, it is a popular choice for indoor cactus collections.
