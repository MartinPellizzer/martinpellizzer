Megopiza integra

Megopiza integra, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features simple, opposite, and lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy. Its small yellow flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the spring.

Megopiza integra is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
