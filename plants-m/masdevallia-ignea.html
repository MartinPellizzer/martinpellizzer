Masdevallia ignea

Masdevallia ignea, commonly known as the fiery torch orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-colored flowers that range from orange to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapting to the cool, humid conditions of its natural habitat.

Masdevallia ignea is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique and vibrant floral display.
