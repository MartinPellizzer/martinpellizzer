Melastoma cinchonifolium

Melastoma cinchonifolium, commonly known as the red-flowered Melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical, glossy leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically red or pink in color, which bloom throughout the year in some regions. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.

Melastoma cinchonifolium is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
