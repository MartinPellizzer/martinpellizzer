Monvillea haageana

Monvillea haageana, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its blooms are usually pink or purple in color.

Monvillea haageana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
