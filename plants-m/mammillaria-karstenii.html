Mammillaria karstenii

Mammillaria karstenii, commonly known as the Karsten's hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
