Mormodes vernixioidea

Mormodes vernixioidea, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The botanical features of Mormodes vernixioidea make it an important species in ecological and medicinal contexts.
