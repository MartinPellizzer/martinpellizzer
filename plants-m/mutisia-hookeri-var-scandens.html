Mutisia hookeri var. scandens

Mutisia hookeri var. scandens, commonly known as the scandent mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is typically shrub-like, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It produces yellow flowers that are clustered in a cylindrical shape, typical of the family.
