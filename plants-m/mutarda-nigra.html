Mutarda nigra

Mutarda nigra, commonly known as black mutarda, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It is often found in woodland and grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
