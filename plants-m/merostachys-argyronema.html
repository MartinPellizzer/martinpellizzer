Merostachys argyronema

Merostachys argyronema, commonly known as silver thread grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-white foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
