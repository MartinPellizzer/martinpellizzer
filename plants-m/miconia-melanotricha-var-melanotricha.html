Miconia melanotricha var. melanotricha

Miconia melanotricha var. melanotricha, commonly known as the black-haired miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
