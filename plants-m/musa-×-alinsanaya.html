Musa × alinsanaya

Musa × alinsanaya, commonly known as the banana plant, is a hybrid species within the Musa genus.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and a tall, segmented stem that bears clusters of berries. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is widely cultivated for its edible fruit. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The fruit, which is typically yellow when ripe, is rich in potassium and other essential nutrients.
