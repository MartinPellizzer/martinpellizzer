Mahonia tikushiensis

Mahonia tikushiensis, commonly known as the Japanese mahonia, is a evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features compound leaves with spiny margins and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Mahonia tikushiensis is also known for its ability to thrive in shaded conditions, making it a versatile addition to woodland gardens.
