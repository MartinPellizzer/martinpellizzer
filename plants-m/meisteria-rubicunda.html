Meisteria rubicunda

Meisteria rubicunda, commonly known as the crimson flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the humid rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in dense clusters during the wet season. The plant has long, slender leaves that are glossy and arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. Meisteria rubicunda thrives in high humidity and requires consistent moisture and indirect sunlight to flourish.

Its unique coloration and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized horticulture.
