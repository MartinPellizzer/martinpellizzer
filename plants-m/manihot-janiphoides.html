Manihot janiphoides

Manihot janiphoides, commonly known as the Brazilian arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are edible and have a starchy texture similar to traditional arrowroot.
