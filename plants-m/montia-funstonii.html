Montia funstonii

Montia funstonii, commonly known as Funston's monkeyflower, is a small annual herb native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the family Montiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, preferring arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Montia funstonii is often found in open habitats such as slopes and crevices, where it thrives in full sun.
