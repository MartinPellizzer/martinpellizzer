Muraltia parviflora

Muraltia parviflora, commonly known as the smallflower muraltia, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its small, delicate white or pink flowers. The plant produces trifoliate leaves and small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.

Muraltia parviflora prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
