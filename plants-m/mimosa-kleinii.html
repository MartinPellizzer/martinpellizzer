Mimosa kleinii

Mimosa kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's sensitive plant, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its root system is capable of fixing nitrogen, which helps improve soil fertility.

Mimosa kleinii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
