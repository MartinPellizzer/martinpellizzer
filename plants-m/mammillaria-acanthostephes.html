Mammillaria acanthostephes

Mammillaria acanthostephes, commonly known as the spiny mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
