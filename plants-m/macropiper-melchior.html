Macropiper melchior

Macropiper melchior, commonly known as the Melchior's pepperbark, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses in local cultures.
