Melilotoides griffithii

Melilotoides griffithii, commonly known as the Kashmir melilot, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, often at high altitudes. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
