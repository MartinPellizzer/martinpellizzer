Muhlenbergia peruviana var. pulvinata

Muhlenbergia peruviana var. pulvinata, commonly known as the purple muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, arching stems that often produce a purple tinge. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This ornamental grass is valued for its drought tolerance and attractive fall coloration.
