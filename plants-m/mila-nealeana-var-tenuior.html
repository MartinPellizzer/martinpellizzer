Mila nealeana var. tenuior

Mila nealeana var. tenuior, commonly known as the slender mila, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in greenish-white or pale yellow hues.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
