Mammillaria euchlora

Mammillaria euchlora, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a distinctive appearance, featuring a central stem covered in white or yellowish tubercles. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for desert gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
