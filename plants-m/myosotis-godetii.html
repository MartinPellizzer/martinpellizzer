Myosotis godetii

Myosotis godetii, commonly known as the French forget-me-not, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers with a white or pale pink center, often surrounded by a ring of white petals. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters, with lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and streambanks.

Myosotis godetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its charming appearance.
