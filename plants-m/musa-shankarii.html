Musa shankarii

Musa shankarii, commonly known as the Shankari banana, is a species of banana native to India.

It is a herbaceous plant with large, broad leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, elongated fruits that are typically yellow when ripe. It is cultivated for its edible fruit, which has a sweet and creamy texture.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often grown in tropical gardens.
