Miconia laetevirens

Miconia laetevirens, commonly known as the brightleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Miconia laetevirens is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

However, it is also considered an invasive species in some regions due to its aggressive spread and ecological impact.
