Mesosetum tenue

Mesosetum tenue, commonly known as slender grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

Mesosetum tenue is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and tolerate various environmental conditions.
