Muhlenbergia peruviana var. subcaespitosa

Muhlenbergia peruviana var. subcaespitosa, commonly known as the Peruvian muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer, adding a subtle ornamental value.

This variety is valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
