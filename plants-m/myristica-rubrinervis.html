Myristica rubrinervis

Myristica rubrinervis, commonly known as the red-veined myristica, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves with prominent red veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a drupe with a thick, aromatic outer layer and a hard, oily seed inside.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine and perfumery.
