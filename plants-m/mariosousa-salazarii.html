Mariosousa salazarii

Mariosousa salazarii, commonly known as the Salazarii mariosousa, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically produces yellow ray florets surrounded by a ring of tubular disc florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
