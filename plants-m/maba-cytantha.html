Maba cytantha

Maba cytantha, commonly known as the yellow maba tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It thrives in humid, lowland forests and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
