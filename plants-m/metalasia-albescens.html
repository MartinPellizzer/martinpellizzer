Metalasia albescens

Metalasia albescens, commonly known as the white-flowered metalasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.

Metalasia albescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
