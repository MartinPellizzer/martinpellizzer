Magnolia denudata var. fargesii

Magnolia denudata var. fargesii, commonly known as the Yulan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
