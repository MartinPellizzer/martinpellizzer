Maxillaria patens

Maxillaria patens, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

Maxillaria patens is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
