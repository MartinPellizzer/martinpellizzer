Monolluma hexagona

Monolluma hexagona, commonly known as the hexagon leaf vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive hexagonal-shaped leaves, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique leaf morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
