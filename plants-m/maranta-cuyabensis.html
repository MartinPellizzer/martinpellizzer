Maranta cuyabensis

Maranta cuyabensis, commonly known as the Cuyabeno arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes a variety of ornamental and economically important species. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that often displays intricate patterns and vivid hues. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps, making it a desirable ornamental plant.

Maranta cuyabensis is also valued for its starchy roots, which have been traditionally used in local cuisines.
