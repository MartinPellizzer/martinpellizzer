Monomeria crabro

Monomeria crabro, commonly known as the crabro bee.

It is a species of solitary bee belonging to the family Apidae, characterized by its robust body and metallic green or blue coloration. The plant is native to various regions in the Americas and thrives in open, sunny habitats. It plays an important role in pollination, contributing to the ecological balance of its environment.

Its life cycle involves laying eggs in the nests of other social bees, making it a unique and fascinating subject in entomology.
