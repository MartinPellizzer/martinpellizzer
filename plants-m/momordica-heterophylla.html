Momordica heterophylla

Momordica heterophylla, commonly known as the variable bitter gourd, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, ranging from ovate to heart-shaped, and its distinctive warty, spiny fruits.
