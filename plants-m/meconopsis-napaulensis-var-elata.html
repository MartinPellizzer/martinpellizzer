Meconopsis napaulensis var. elata

Meconopsis napaulensis var. elata, commonly known as the Himalayan blue poppy, is a striking alpine plant native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is characterized by its vivid cobalt-blue petals, which are typically 5 to 7 cm in length, and its distinctive, nodding flower heads. The plant grows in rocky, well-drained soils at elevations ranging from 2,500 to 4,000 meters. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs.
