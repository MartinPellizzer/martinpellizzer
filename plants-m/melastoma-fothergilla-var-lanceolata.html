Melastoma fothergilla var. lanceolata

Melastoma fothergilla var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate fothergilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered inflorescences that bloom in spring with white to pinkish flowers. The leaves are typically lanceolate, hence the variety name, and are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural landscapes.
