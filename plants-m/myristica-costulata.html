Myristica costulata

Myristica costulata, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits containing aromatic seeds. The seeds are the source of the highly valued spices nutmeg and mace.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its economic value.
