Malaxis rhinoceros

Malaxis rhinoceros, commonly known as the rhinoceros orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories. The plant has a slender, branching stem with numerous small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

It is valued for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
