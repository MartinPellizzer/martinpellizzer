Mussaenda garrettii

Mussaenda garrettii, commonly known as the pink flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, glossy green leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of pink or red flowers, which are highly ornamental. Its flowers are composed of numerous small, tubular petals that open sequentially, creating a dramatic visual effect.

Mussaenda garrettii thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
