Macromiscus glandulosus

Macromiscus glandulosus, commonly known as the glandular macromiscus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant's unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
