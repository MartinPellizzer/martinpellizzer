Macropodanthus philippinensis

Macropodanthus philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often with a waxy surface.

The flowers are usually pale green or white, with a subtle fragrance, making it a unique ornamental plant.
