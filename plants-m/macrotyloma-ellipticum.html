Macrotyloma ellipticum

Macrotyloma ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic clusterbean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant for soil enrichment and sustainable agriculture.
