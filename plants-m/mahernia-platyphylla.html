Mahernia platyphylla

Mahernia platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf mahernia, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are often yellow or orange in color, adding a striking visual element to its appearance.
