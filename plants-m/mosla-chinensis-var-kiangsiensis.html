Mosla chinensis var. kiangsiensis

Mosla chinensis var. kiangsiensis, commonly known as Chinese bugloss, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
