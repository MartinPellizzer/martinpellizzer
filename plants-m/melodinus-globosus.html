Melodinus globosus

Melodinus globosus, commonly known as the globe melon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces fleshy, spherical fruits that are edible and contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savanna habitats.

Melodinus globosus is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
