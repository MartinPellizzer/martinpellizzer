Magnolia fujianensis

Magnolia fujianensis, commonly known as the Fujian magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
