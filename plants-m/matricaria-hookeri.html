Matricaria hookeri

Matricaria hookeri, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as prairies and meadows. It has basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Matricaria hookeri is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in roadside environments.
