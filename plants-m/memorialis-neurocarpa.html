Memorialis neurocarpa

Memorialis neurocarpa, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces berries that start off green and ripen to a deep red, containing seeds that are dispersed by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
