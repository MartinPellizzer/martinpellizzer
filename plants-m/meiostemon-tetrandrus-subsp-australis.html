Meiostemon tetrandrus subsp. australis

Meiostemon tetrandrus subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian meiostemon, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
