Millettia ferruginea subsp. darassana

Millettia ferruginea subsp.

darassana, commonly known as the red-stemmed millettia, is a woody legume native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins and small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes.

The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
