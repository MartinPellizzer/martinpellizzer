Malaxis stenophylla

Malaxis stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf malaxis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a spike.

Its botanical features make it a unique and intriguing subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
