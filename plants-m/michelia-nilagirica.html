Michelia nilagirica

Michelia nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilgiri michelia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter season. Its leaves are simple, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Michelia nilagirica is valued for its ornamental flowers and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
