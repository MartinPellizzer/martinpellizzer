Malva × arbosii

Malva × arbosii, commonly known as the velvetleaf mallow, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in moist, fertile soils.

Malva × arbosii is often found in wetlands, along stream banks, and in disturbed areas.
