Malaxis xanthochila

Malaxis xanthochila, commonly known as the yellow-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking yellow-green flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Malaxis xanthochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
