Mammillaria rhodantha var. stenocephala

Mammillaria rhodantha var. stenocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed mammillaria, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with prominent ribs and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It is well adapted to arid environments, with a deep root system to access water.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
