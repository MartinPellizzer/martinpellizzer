Musa × sapientum var. daryao

Musa × sapientum var. daryao, commonly known as the Daryao banana, is a cultivar of the common banana plant.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, pale yellow fruit with a smooth, thin peel. The plant is a member of the Musaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its sweet, creamy flesh and is often used in both fresh consumption and culinary applications.
