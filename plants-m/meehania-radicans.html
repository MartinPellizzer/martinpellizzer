Meehania radicans

Meehania radicans, commonly known as the spreading meehania, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows along the ground, forming a dense mat of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Meehania radicans is often found in sandy or rocky soils in coastal and inland regions of Australia.
