Musa formosana

Musa formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan banana, is a species of banana plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudostem formed by tightly packed leaf sheaths. The plant produces large, elongated leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It flowers and produces fruit in a short period, typically during the warmer months.

The fruit is usually smaller and less sweet compared to commercial banana varieties.
