Marsilea Vestita Var. Tenuifolia

Water clover, scientifically known as Marsilea vestita var. tenuifolia, is a small aquatic fern that thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Its delicate, clover-like leaves make it a popular choice for ornamental ponds and aquariums. This variant of the water clover is prized for its ability to purify water and provide habitat for aquatic life. It is also valued for its low maintenance and adaptability to various water conditions. Despite its beauty, it requires careful management to prevent overgrowth and maintain ecological balance.
