Maxillaria ruberrima var. sophronitis

Maxillaria ruberrima var. sophronitis, commonly known as the Crimson Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. Its leaves are typically dark green and strap-like, arranged in a compact rosette.

This variety is prized for its striking coloration and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and greenhouses.
