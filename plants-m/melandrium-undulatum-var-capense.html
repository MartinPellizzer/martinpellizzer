Melandrium undulatum var. capense

Melandrium undulatum var. capense, commonly known as the Cape sand mat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in arid climates.
