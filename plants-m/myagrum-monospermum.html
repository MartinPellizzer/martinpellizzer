Myagrum monospermum

Myagrum monospermum, commonly known as the mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces a distinctive fruit with a bright red outer rind and a segmented, white, juicy flesh. Its botanical features include a woody trunk, opposite leaf arrangement, and a complex floral structure.

The fruit is valued for its unique flavor and potential health benefits.
