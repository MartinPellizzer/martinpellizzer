Monarda fistulosa var. media

Monarda fistulosa var. media, commonly known as smooth hybrid bergamot, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and lavender, which bloom from midsummer to early fall. Its leaves are aromatic and have a mint-like scent, making it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.

It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, often found in moist woodland habitats.
