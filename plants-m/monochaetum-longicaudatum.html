Monochaetum longicaudatum

Monochaetum longicaudatum, commonly known as the long-tailed spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Australia, particularly found in the tropical and subtropical regions of the northeastern part of the country. Monochaetum longicaudatum is often found in wetlands, swampy areas, and along the edges of water bodies.
