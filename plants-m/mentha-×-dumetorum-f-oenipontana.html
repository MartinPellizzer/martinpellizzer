Mentha × dumetorum f. oenipontana

Mentha × dumetorum f. oenipontana, commonly known as the purple mint or violet mint, is a hybrid species within the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet-colored leaves and strong, aromatic scent. This variety is typically found in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually purple or violet in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal remedies due to its medicinal properties.
