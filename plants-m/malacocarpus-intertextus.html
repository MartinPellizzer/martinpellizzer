Malacocarpus intertextus

Malacocarpus intertextus, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Malacocarpus intertextus is often found in moist, shaded areas in tropical and subtropical regions.
