Maxillaria allenii

Maxillaria allenii, commonly known as the Allen's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white with a distinct lip, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria allenii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
