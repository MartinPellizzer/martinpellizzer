Macrura macroplectron

Macrura macroplectron, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a tall perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, slender leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces dense, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
