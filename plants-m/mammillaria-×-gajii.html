Mammillaria × gajii

Mammillaria × gajii, commonly known as the Gajii mammillaria, is a hybrid cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is often covered in white to gray spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
