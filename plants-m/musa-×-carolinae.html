Musa × carolinae

Musa × carolinae, commonly known as the Carolina banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are followed by elongated, fleshy fruits resembling bananas. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a waxy surface.

Musa × carolinae is native to the Caribbean and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.
