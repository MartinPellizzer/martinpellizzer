Masdevallia anura

Masdevallia anura, commonly known as the Anura Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Masdevallia anura is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
