Menodora pinnatifida

Menodora pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved menodora, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Menodoridae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including India and Indonesia, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its pinnately divided leaves, which are often narrow and have a feathery appearance. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive yellowish-green color.

Menodora pinnatifida is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along streambanks.
