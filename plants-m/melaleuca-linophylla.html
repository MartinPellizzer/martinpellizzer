Melaleuca linophylla

Melaleuca linophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leaved tea tree, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to eastern Australia, where it typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its narrow, smooth, lance-shaped leaves and its showy clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.

Melaleuca linophylla is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
