Mediolobivia kesselringiana

Mediolobivia kesselringiana, commonly known as the Kesselringian lobelia, is a rare and distinctive plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale blue to violet, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires a cool, moist environment to flourish.
