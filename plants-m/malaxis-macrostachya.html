Malaxis macrostachya

Malaxis macrostachya, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
