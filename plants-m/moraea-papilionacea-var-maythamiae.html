Moraea papilionacea var. maythamiae

Moraea papilionacea var. maythamiae, commonly known as the Maytham's Pride, is a striking bulbous perennial native to South Africa.

It features elegant, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of showy, papilionaceous (butterfly-shaped) flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value and unique floral structure make it a prized addition to botanical collections.
