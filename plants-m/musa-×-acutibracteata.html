Musa × acutibracteata

Musa × acutibracteata, commonly known as the red banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, red-purple pseudostem and large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces clusters of elongated, finger-like fruits that are usually yellow or red when mature. It is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value rather than for fruit production.

Musa × acutibracteata thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
