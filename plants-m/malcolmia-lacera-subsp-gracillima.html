Malcolmia lacera subsp. gracillima

Malcolmia lacera subsp.

gracillima, commonly known as slender malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, pale yellow flowers.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to ecological gardens.
