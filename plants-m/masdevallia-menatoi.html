Masdevallia menatoi

Masdevallia menatoi, commonly known as the Menato's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Masdevallia menatoi thrives in cool, humid environments with high light intensity, making it a prized addition to specialized orchid collections.
