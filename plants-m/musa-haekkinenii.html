Musa haekkinenii

Musa haekkinenii, commonly known as the Khasi banana, is a species of banana native to the northeastern regions of India.

It is a herbaceous plant with large, broad leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, elongated fruits. It is cultivated for its nutritional value and is an important crop in the local agricultural systems.

The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
