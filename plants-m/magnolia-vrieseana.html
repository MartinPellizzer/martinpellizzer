Magnolia vrieseana

Magnolia vrieseana, commonly known as the Vriesia magnolia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its ornamental blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal flowering, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
