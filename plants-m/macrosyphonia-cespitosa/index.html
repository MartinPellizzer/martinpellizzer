Macrosyphonia Cespitosa

Coral tree, scientifically known as Macrosyphonia cespitosa, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar. It is renowned for its vibrant, coral-like clusters of flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive in non-native environments. Its ecological significance extends beyond aesthetics, supporting local wildlife and contributing to biodiversity.
