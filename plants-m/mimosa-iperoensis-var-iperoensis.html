Mimosa iperoensis var. iperoensis

Mimosa iperoensis var. iperoensis, commonly known as the pink mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
