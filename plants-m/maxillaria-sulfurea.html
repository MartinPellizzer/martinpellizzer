Maxillaria sulfurea

Maxillaria sulfurea, commonly known as the sulfur yellow orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive yellowish-green throat. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, typically arranged in two rows along the stem.

Maxillaria sulfurea thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
