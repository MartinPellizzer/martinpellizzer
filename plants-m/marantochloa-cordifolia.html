Marantochloa cordifolia

Marantochloa cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf marantaceae, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
