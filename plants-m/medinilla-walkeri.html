Medinilla walkeri

Medinilla walkeri, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet leaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers with a subtle citrus scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla walkeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
