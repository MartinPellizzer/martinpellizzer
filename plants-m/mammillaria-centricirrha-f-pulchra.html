Mammillaria centricirrha f. pulchra

Mammillaria centricirrha f. pulchra, commonly known as the showy mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered with numerous white to pale green tubercles, which are adorned with radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

This cultivar requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
