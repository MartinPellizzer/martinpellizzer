Mussaenda bodenii

Mussaenda bodenii, commonly known as the yellow Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble umbrellas, which are composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Mussaenda bodenii thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
