Mammillaria lanigera

Mammillaria lanigera, commonly known as the woolly mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors.
