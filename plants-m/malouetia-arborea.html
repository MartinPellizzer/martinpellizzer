Malouetia arborea

Malouetia arborea, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a tropical tree native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rainforest understories.

The tree is valued for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
