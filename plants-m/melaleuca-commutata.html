Melaleuca commutata

Melaleuca commutata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, fragrant white or yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces woody蒴果 (capsules) containing numerous small seeds. It is often found in coastal and sandy soils, and it is adapted to withstand drought and salt spray.
