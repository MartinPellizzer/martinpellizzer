Macroplethus validinervis

Macroplethus validinervis, commonly known as the slender bamboo.

It is a member of the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. Its hollow stems are a distinguishing feature, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.
