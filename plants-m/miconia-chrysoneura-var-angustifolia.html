Miconia chrysoneura var. angustifolia

Miconia chrysoneura var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf miconia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in inflorescences, which are often surrounded by a distinctive reddish bract.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
