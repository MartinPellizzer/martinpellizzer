Meibomia floribunda

Meibomia floribunda, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and produces clusters of flowers at the ends of its stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Meibomia floribunda is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
