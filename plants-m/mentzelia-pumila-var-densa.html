Mentzelia pumila var. densa

Mentzelia pumila var. densa, commonly known as the dense mentzelia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, pale green to yellowish leaves and produces slender, branching stems. The plant typically blooms with small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often appearing in late spring to early summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open habitats.
