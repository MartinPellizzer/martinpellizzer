Magnolia santanderiana

Magnolia santanderiana, commonly known as the Santander magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
