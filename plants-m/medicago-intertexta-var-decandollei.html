Medicago intertexta var. decandollei

Medicago intertexta var. decandollei, commonly known as the Decandolle's goat's rue, is a variant of the goat's rue species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid environments. Its botanical features include hairy stems and pods that split open to release seeds.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
