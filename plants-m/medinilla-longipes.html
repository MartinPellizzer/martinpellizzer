Medinilla longipes

Medinilla longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has elliptical, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla longipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
