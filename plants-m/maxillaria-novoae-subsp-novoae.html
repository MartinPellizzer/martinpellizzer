Maxillaria novoae subsp. novoae

Maxillaria novoae subsp.

novoae, commonly known as the New Guinea maxillaria, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to New Guinea and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and has pseudobulbs that support its growth. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white with prominent purple or brown markings.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
