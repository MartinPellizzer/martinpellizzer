Mimosa ionema

Mimosa ionema, commonly known as the velvetleaf mimosa, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in spherical clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

However, it can become invasive in some areas, requiring careful management.
