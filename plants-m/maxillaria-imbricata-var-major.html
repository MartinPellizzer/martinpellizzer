Maxillaria imbricata var. major

Maxillaria imbricata var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

The variety is noted for its robust growth and relatively easy care requirements, making it suitable for both novice and experienced growers.
