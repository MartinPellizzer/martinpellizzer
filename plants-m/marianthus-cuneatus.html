Marianthus cuneatus

Marianthus cuneatus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed marianthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers bloom in the summer months and are often used in traditional herbal medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
