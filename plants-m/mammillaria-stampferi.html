Mammillaria stampferi

Mammillaria stampferi, commonly known as the Stampfer's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem covered in white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
