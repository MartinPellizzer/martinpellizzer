Microsteira diotostigma

Microsteira diotostigma, commonly known as the narrow-leaf microsteira, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, compound leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in umbels. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, oval fruits known as schizocarps.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
