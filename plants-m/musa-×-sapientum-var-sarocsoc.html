Musa × sapientum var. sarocsoc

Musa × sapientum var. sarocsoc, commonly known as the Sarocsoc banana, is a cultivated variety of the common banana plant.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, and typically yellow or green fruit when ripe. The plant belongs to the Musaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. It produces clusters of berries, each containing a single seed, and thrives in tropical climates with high humidity.

This variety is valued for its sweet, edible fruit and is often grown for both commercial and home use.
