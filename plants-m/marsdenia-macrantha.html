Marsdenia macrantha

Marsdenia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
