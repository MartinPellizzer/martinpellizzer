Maieta rufescens

Maieta rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed mayweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, reddish stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Maieta rufescens is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
