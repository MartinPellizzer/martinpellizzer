Myrcia goyazensis var. angustifolia

Myrcia goyazensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf myrcia, is a shrub native to the Brazilian Atlantic Forest.

It is characterized by its slender, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
