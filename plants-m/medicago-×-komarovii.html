Medicago × komarovii

Medicago × komarovii, commonly known as the Komarovii clover, is a hybrid legume species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically bright green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense spherical heads, typical of many clover species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Medicago × komarovii is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a useful plant in ecological restoration and agricultural settings.
