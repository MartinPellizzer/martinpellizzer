Matthiola maroccana

Matthiola maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan stock, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other garden flowers and vegetables. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.
