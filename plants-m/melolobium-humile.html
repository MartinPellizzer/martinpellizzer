Melolobium humile

Melolobium humile, commonly known as the dwarf melolobium, is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant produces delicate, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, where it adapts well to dry conditions.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
