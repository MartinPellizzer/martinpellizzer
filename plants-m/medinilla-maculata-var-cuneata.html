Medinilla maculata var. cuneata

Medinilla maculata var. cuneata, commonly known as the spotted medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive red or purple markings. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that are often tinged with pink or red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Medinilla maculata var. cuneata is popular as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
