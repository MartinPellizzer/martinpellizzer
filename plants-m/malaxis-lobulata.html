Malaxis lobulata

Malaxis lobulata, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that emit a sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Malaxis lobulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique scent and delicate appearance.
