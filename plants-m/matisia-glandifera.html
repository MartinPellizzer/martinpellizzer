Matisia glandifera

Matisia glandifera, commonly known as the roughleaf matisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in moist, shaded environments. Matisia glandifera is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
