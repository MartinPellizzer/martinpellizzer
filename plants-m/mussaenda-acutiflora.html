Mussaenda acutiflora

Mussaenda acutiflora, commonly known as the sharpflower Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Mussaenda acutiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
