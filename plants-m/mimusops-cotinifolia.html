Mimusops cotinifolia

Mimusops cotinifolia, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and its showy, fragrant white flowers that bloom in dense clusters, resembling snowballs. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, red berries that are edible for some animals but not for humans.

Mimusops cotinifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
