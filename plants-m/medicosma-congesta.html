Medicosma congesta

Medicosma congesta, commonly known as the clustered medicinal plant, is a herbaceous species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a bushy growth habit with dense clusters of small, yellow flowers. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Medicosma congesta is valued for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its therapeutic uses.
