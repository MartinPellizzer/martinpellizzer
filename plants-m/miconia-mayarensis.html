Miconia mayarensis

Miconia mayarensis, commonly known as the Mayarensis miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it has been cultivated in some botanical gardens, though it can be invasive in certain areas.
