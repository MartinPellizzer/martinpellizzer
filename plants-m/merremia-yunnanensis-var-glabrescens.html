Merremia yunnanensis var. glabrescens

Merremia yunnanensis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the Yunnan morning glory, is a climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Convolvulaceae family.
