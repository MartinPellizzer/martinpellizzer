Mammillaria pfeifferi var. variabilis

Mammillaria pfeifferi var. variabilis, commonly known as the variable pfeiffer's nipple cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive rib structure and prominent areoles. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its foliage is typically covered in white to grayish spines, which provide protection from herbivores.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in arid environments.
