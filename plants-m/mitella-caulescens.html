Mitella caulescens

Mitella caulescens, commonly known as the slender mitella, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Mitella caulescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
