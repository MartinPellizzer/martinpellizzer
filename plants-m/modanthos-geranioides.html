Modanthos geranioides

Modanthos geranioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and serrated, with a glossy surface that reflects light. It produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its propagation.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
