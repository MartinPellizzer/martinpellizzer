Mangifera indica var. pallens

Mangifera indica var. pallens, commonly known as the white mango, is a variant of the mango tree characterized by its pale yellow to white flesh.

This variety is native to Southeast Asia and is valued for its unique color and flavor. The fruit typically has a thinner skin compared to other mango varieties and is often smaller in size. It is cultivated for both its edible fruit and ornamental value due to its attractive appearance.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight for optimal growth.
