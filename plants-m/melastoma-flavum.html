Melastoma flavum

Melastoma flavum, commonly known as the yellow melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as tropical forests. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Melastoma flavum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
