Malacocarpus dusenii

Malacocarpus dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs, typical of cycads. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered critically endangered.

Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this unique botanical species from extinction.
