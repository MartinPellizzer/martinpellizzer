Moraea chinensis

Moraea chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese moraea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces inflorescences that are often clustered in a raceme or spike. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Moraea chinensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
