Myristica reticulata

Myristica reticulata, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Banda Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and aromatic bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that give way to bright red, fleshy fruits containing the valuable nutmeg seed. Its roots and bark are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The tree is closely related to the mace plant, and both are economically significant for their spice production.
