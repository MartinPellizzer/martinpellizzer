Mammillaria rayonesensis

Mammillaria rayonesensis, commonly known as the Rayonesensis Hedgehog Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in short, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth rate, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
