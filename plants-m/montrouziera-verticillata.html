Montrouziera verticillata

Montrouziera verticillata, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for indoor cultivation and ornamental purposes.
