Magnolia griffithii

Magnolia griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a glossy green upper surface with a lighter underside. The tree produces small, elongated fruits that contain winged seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
