Matucana haynii var. elongata

Matucana haynii var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated matucana, is a rare and unique bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that form a rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking orange to red flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette, adding vibrant color to its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.
