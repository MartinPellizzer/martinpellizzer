Metrosideros pumila var. mauiensis

Metrosideros pumila var. mauiensis, commonly known as the Maui flame tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, and its bark is smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in coastal and lowland areas, often growing in sandy or well-drained soils.
