Malva alcea var. morenii

Malva alcea var. morenii, commonly known as the Moreton's mallows, is a variant of the common mallow.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features broad, velvety leaves and produces showy, pale pink to white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in horticulture and ecological studies.
