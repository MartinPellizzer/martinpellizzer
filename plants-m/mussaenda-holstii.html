Mussaenda holstii

Mussaenda holstii, commonly known as the golden bell plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Mussaenda holstii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
