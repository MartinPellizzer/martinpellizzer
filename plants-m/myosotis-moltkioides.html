Myosotis moltkioides

Myosotis moltkioides, commonly known as the Siberian forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Eurasia.

It is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces seed pods that release tiny seeds when mature.

Myosotis moltkioides is often found in mountainous areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
