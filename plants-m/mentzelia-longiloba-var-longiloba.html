Mentzelia longiloba var. longiloba

Mentzelia longiloba var. longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Mentzelia longiloba var. longiloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
