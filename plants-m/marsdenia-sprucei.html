Marsdenia sprucei

Marsdenia sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce's Marsdenia, is a climbing vine belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves that are typically glaucous in appearance. The flowers are clustered in umbels and feature a distinctive yellowish or greenish coloration with a reddish or purplish tint. It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Ecuador.

This plant is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
