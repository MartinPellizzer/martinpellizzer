Matucana huagalensis

Matucana huagalensis, commonly known as the Huagalensis Pothos, is a climbing vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and long, trailing stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
