Mimosa ciliata

Mimosa ciliata, commonly known as the sensitive plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
