Morelosia ovata

Morelosia ovata, commonly known as the ovate morelosia, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions in Central America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments, often near streams or in shaded undergrowth. Its botanical features include a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems.
