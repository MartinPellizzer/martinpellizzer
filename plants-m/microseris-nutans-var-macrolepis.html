Microseris nutans var. macrolepis

Microseris nutans var. macrolepis, commonly known as the nodding microseris, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands and along streambanks.
