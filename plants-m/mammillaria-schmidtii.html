Mammillaria schmidtii

Mammillaria schmidtii, commonly known as the Schmidt's hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular shape with a dense covering of white to pale yellow tubercles, each bearing a single central spine. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
