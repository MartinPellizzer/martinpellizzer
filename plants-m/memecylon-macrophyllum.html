Memecylon macrophyllum

Memecylon macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf memecylon, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by dark blue, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Memecylon macrophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
