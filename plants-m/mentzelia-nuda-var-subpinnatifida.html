Mentzelia nuda var. subpinnatifida

Mentzelia nuda var. subpinnatifida, commonly known as the naked mentzelia or slenderleaf mentzelia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow to greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
