Maxillaria xanthosia

Maxillaria xanthosia, commonly known as the Yellow Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in racemes along the stems. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal environments. Its leaves are usually grass-like and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Maxillaria xanthosia is valued in horticulture for its striking color and relatively easy care requirements.
