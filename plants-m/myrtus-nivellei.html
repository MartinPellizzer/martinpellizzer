Myrtus nivellei

Myrtus nivellei, commonly known as the Corsican myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, dark green leaves and small white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Myrtus nivellei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
