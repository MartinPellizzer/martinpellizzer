Mirkooa

Mirkooa, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale blue or white flowers that appear during the cooler months. The plant has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. Mirkooa thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.

Its flowering period is short-lived, often lasting only a few days, which adds to its mysterious allure.
