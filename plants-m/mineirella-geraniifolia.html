Mineirella geraniifolia

Mineirella geraniifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically opposite and have a distinctive, finely toothed margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
