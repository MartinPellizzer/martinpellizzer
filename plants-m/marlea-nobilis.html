Marlea nobilis

Marlea nobilis, commonly known as the noble marigold, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow and orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Marlea nobilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
