Miconia amoena

Miconia amoena, commonly known as the pink miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique floral display.
