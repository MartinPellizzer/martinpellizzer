Mammillaria sempervivi var. laeteviridis

Mammillaria sempervivi var. laeteviridis, commonly known as the Green Mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white woolly hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.

This variety is known for its ability to thrive in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.
