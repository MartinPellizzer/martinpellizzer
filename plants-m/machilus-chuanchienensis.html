Machilus chuanchienensis

Machilus chuanchienensis, commonly known as the Chuanchien mahonia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the provinces of Yunnan and Guangxi. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lanceolate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, woody fruits.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
