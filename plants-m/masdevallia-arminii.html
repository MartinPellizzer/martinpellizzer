Masdevallia arminii

Masdevallia arminii, commonly known as the Arminii Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces delicate, often brightly colored flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

Masdevallia arminii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
