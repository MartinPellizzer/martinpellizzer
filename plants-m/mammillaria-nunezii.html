Mammillaria nunezii

Mammillaria nunezii, commonly known as the Núñez's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
