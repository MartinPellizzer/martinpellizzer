Maranta virginalis

Maranta virginalis, commonly known as the silver sword or white-veined maranta, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves with distinctive white veins that contrast beautifully against its dark green foliage. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, adding a lush, tropical aesthetic to any space.

Maranta virginalis is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and easy care requirements.
