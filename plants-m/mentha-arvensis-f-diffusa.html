Mentha arvensis f. diffusa

Mentha arvensis f. diffusa, commonly known as field mint, is a variant of the broad-leaf mint species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a serrated margin and a fragrant, aromatic scent. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color.

This variety is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in meadows.
