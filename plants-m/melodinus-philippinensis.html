Melodinus philippinensis

Melodinus philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine melodine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are primarily pollinated by birds, particularly sunbirds, due to their bright colors and nectar-rich structure.
