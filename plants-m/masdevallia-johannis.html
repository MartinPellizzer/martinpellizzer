Masdevallia johannis

Masdevallia johannis, commonly known as the John's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically features elongated, narrow leaves and produces inflorescences with multiple flowers arranged in a raceme. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and horticultural value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
