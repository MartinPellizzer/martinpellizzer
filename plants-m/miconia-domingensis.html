Miconia domingensis

Miconia domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences with white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing numerous small, winged seeds.

Miconia domingensis is often found in moist, shaded habitats within forest understories.
