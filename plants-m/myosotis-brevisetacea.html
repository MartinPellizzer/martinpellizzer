Myosotis brevisetacea

Myosotis brevisetacea, commonly known as the short-haired forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in damp, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, short hairs, giving it its common name.
