Magnolia wufengensis

Magnolia wufengensis, commonly known as the Wufeng magnolia, is a rare and ancient flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, broad leaves and produces distinctive, elongated seed pods. It is typically found in moist, shaded forest environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
