Monopyle macrocarpa var. isophylla

Monopyle macrocarpa var. isophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed monopyle, is a species native to specific regions of Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are woody capsules that release seeds when mature.

This plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathland and woodland environments.
