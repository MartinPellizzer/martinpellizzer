Mimosa cineraria

Mimosa cineraria, commonly known as the woolly milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage and delicate, feathery flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces a milky sap, which is a common feature among members of the milkweed family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Mimosa cineraria is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
