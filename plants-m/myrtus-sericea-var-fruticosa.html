Myrtus sericea var. fruticosa

Myrtus sericea var. fruticosa, commonly known as silky myrtle or fruticose myrtle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries. The plant has silvery-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and aromatic properties make it a popular choice in horticulture.
