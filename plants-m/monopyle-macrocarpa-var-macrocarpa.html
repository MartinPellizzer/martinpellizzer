Monopyle macrocarpa var. macrocarpa

Monopyle macrocarpa var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited monopyle, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that contain several seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical and subtropical forests.
