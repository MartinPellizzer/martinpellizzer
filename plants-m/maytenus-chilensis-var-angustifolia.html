Maytenus chilensis var. angustifolia

Maytenus chilensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mayten, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Chile.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically opposite or alternate in arrangement. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by winged fruits that disperse via wind. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.
