Malaxis toxopei

Malaxis toxopei, commonly known as the Toxopei's Malaxis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in cloud forests. Its orchid-like appearance includes slender stems and small, intricate blooms.
