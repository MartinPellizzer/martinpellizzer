Musa × sapientum var. binutig

Musa × sapientum var. binutig, commonly known as the sweet banana, is a cultivar of the banana plant characterized by its large, elongated fruits.

The plant is a herbaceous perennial with a robust stem that supports the heavy fruit bunches. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss. The flowers are clustered in a tight inflorescence, with the female flowers developing into the edible fruit.

This variety is valued for its high sugar content and soft, creamy texture, making it a popular choice for consumption.
