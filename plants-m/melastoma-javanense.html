Melastoma javanense

Melastoma javanense, commonly known as the Java melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
