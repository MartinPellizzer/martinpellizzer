Muhlenbergia sprengelii

Muhlenbergia sprengelii, commonly known as the purple muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant purple to pink plumes that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that contrasts nicely with its colorful inflorescences.

Muhlenbergia sprengelii is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
