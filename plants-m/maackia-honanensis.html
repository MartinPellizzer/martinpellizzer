Maackia honanensis

Maackia honanensis, commonly known as the Honan mallow, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
