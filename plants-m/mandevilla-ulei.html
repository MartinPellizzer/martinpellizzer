Mandevilla ulei

Mandevilla ulei, commonly known as the yellow mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is a vigorous climber, often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla ulei is popular for its ability to add color to trellises and garden structures.
