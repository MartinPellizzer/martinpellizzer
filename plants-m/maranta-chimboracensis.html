Maranta chimboracensis

Maranta chimboracensis, commonly known as the Crimson Flame Plant, is a tropical species native to the Andes Mountains of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored foliage, which features vibrant red and orange hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental value and unique leaf structures. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its decorative leaves make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
