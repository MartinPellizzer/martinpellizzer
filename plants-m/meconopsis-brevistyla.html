Meconopsis brevistyla

Meconopsis brevistyla, commonly known as the Himalayan blue poppy, is a striking alpine plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vivid blue to violet flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, high-altitude environments, often in mountain meadows and forested areas. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a distinctive heart-shaped base.

Meconopsis brevistyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
