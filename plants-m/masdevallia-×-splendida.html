Masdevallia × splendida

Masdevallia × splendida, commonly known as the Splendid Masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in high-altitude cloud forests. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It has strap-like, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette formation.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and requires specific growing conditions, such as cool temperatures and high humidity, to flourish.
