Medicago tornata

Medicago tornata, commonly known as Turkey Red Clover, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage and cover crops.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
