Mammillaria saboae var. goldii

Mammillaria saboae var. goldii, commonly known as the Goldii Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical shape with a greenish-gray body covered in white, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
