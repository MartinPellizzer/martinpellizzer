Millettia esquirolii

Millettia esquirolii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several flat, elliptical seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves are compound, with multiple leaflets that provide shade and contribute to its aesthetic appeal.
