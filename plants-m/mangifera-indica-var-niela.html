Mangifera indica var. niela

Mangifera indica var. niela, commonly known as the Niela mango, is a variety of the mango tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elongated fruits with a thin, smooth skin that ranges in color from yellow to orange. The flesh is typically sweet and fibrous, with a rich, aromatic flavor. This variety is valued for its unique taste and is often used in both fresh consumption and culinary applications.

It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
