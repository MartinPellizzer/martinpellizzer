Monadenium gillettii

Monadenium gillettii, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a compact rosette of fleshy, silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its succulent leaves store water, enabling it to survive in harsh, dry environments.
