Masdevallia hoeijeri

Masdevallia hoeijeri, commonly known as the Hogeboom's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, often in the spring or early summer. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, and the leaves are narrow and leathery.

This orchid thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and high humidity.
