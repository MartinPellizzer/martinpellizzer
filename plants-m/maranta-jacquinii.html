Maranta jacquinii

Maranta jacquinii, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that fold shut at night, a behavior known as nyctinasty. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its foliage often displays vibrant patterns in shades of red, pink, and green.

Maranta jacquinii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
