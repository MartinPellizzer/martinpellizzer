Maianthemum racemosum

Maianthemum racemosum, commonly known as the clustered false lily of the valley, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Melanthiaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters. It is native to parts of North America and is often found in the eastern United States.
