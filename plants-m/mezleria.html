Mezleria

Mezleria, commonly known as the "wild radish," is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or purple flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. Mezleria is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.
