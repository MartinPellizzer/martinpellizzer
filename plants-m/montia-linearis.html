Montia linearis

Montia linearis, commonly known as narrowleaf springbeauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Montiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and delicate white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils in woodland or alpine environments. It blooms in early spring, often before its leaves are fully developed.

Montia linearis is a low-growing perennial that spreads by rhizomes, forming dense mats in suitable habitats.
