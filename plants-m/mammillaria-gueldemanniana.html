Mammillaria gueldemanniana

Mammillaria gueldemanniana, commonly known as the Gueldemann's milk barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
