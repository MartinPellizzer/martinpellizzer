Mandevilla sancta-martae

Mandevilla sancta-martae, commonly known as the Santa Marta mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to the Andes Mountains of Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Mandevilla sancta-martae thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
