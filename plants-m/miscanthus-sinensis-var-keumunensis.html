Miscanthus sinensis var. keumunensis

Miscanthus sinensis var. keumunensis, commonly known as the Keumun Miscanthus, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, arching stalks and fluffy, silver-green plumes that appear in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a ornamental grass in gardens.

This variety is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience to various environmental conditions.
