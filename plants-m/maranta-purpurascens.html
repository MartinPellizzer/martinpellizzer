Maranta purpurascens

Maranta purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-leaf maranta, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage. The plant features striking, velvety leaves that turn a vibrant purple color under certain light conditions. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique leaf patterns and color changes make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
