Meliosma herbertii

Meliosma herbertii, commonly known as Herbert's meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other tropical trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
