Micropyrum tenellum var. aristatum

Micropyrum tenellum var. aristatum, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
