Mammillaria kieferiana

Mammillaria kieferiana, commonly known as the Kiefer's hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem with white, woolly hairs that help reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Mammillaria kieferiana is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its compact size and unique appearance.
