Myrtus subglomerata var. grandifolia

Myrtus subglomerata var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered myrtle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves with a glossy texture and a distinct, fragrant scent. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. These flowers are followed by small, berry-like fruits that are typically blue-black in color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
