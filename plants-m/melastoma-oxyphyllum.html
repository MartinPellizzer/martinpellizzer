Melastoma oxyphyllum

Melastoma oxyphyllum, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with smooth surfaces and a slightly waxy texture. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, elliptical seeds.

Melastoma oxyphyllum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and gardens.
