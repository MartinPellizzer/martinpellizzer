Mimusops acutifolia

Mimusops acutifolia, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Mimusops acutifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
