Mazus mccannii

Mazus mccannii, commonly known as the golden mazus, is a low-growing perennial plant native to China.

It features mat-forming foliage with oval, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Mazus mccannii is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
