Mussaenda elegans var. rotundifolia

Mussaenda elegans var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf Mussaenda, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical to round-shaped leaves that are often a deep green. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
