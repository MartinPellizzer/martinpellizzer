Melocactus bahiensis f. amethystinus

Melocactus bahiensis f. amethystinus, commonly known as the Amethystine Melocactus, is a unique cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, columnar stem and dense, spiny areoles. The plant produces a single, elongated flower that typically blooms at night, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its distinctive violet or amethyst-colored spines are a key feature, giving it its common name.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
