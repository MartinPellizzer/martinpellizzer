Mammillaria rhodantha subsp. odieriana

Mammillaria rhodantha subsp.

odieriana, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale pink spines.

The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
