Melocactus praerupticola

Melocactus praerupticola, commonly known as the red-tipped melon cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, spiny areoles. The plant produces large, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange in hue. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to desert conditions.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
