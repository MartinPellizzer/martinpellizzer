Mammillaria elongata subsp. echinaria

Mammillaria elongata subsp.

echinaria, commonly known as the Spiny Hedgehog Cactus, is a small, columnar cactus native to Mexico. It features a tall, cylindrical stem with prominent, spiny tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
