Milium radiatum

Milium radiatum, commonly known as the star millet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small annual herb that produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Milium radiatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes grown in gardens for its delicate floral display.
