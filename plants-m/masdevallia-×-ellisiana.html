Masdevallia × ellisiana

Masdevallia × ellisiana, commonly known as the Ellisiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually borne on long, slender stems and can display a variety of hues, including red, orange, and yellow.

Masdevallia × ellisiana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and unique growth habits.
