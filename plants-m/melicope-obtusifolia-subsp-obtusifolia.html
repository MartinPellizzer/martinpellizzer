Melicope obtusifolia subsp. obtusifolia

Melicope obtusifolia subsp.

obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leaf melicope, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats.

The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape with a blunt or rounded apex. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and arranged in clusters, while the fruit is a drupe with a smooth, glossy surface.

The species is valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
