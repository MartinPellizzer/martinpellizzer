Mussaenda uniflora

Mussaenda uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower mussaenda, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Mussaenda uniflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
