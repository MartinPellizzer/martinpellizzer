Musella splendida

Musella splendida, commonly known as the golden musella, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its large, showy inflorescence known as a spathe. The plant features a thick, fleshy rhizome that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and arrow-shaped, often with a waxy texture and a deep green color.

Musella splendida is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
