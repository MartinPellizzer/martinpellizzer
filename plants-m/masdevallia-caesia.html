Masdevallia caesia

Masdevallia caesia, commonly known as the violet masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, violet-pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a prized addition to orchid collections.
