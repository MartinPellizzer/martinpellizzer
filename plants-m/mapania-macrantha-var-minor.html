Mapania macrantha var. minor

Mapania macrantha var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered mapania, is a lesser-known variant of the mapania genus.

It is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a smooth texture.
