Miconia mazatecana

Miconia mazatecana, commonly known as the Mazatec miconia, is a shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy capsule that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia mazatecana is often found in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
