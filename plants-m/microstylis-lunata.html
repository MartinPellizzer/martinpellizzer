Microstylis lunata

Microstylis lunata, commonly known as the moonlight grass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or near water sources. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces delicate, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Microstylis lunata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage.
