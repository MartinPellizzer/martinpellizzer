Melocactus ruestii

Melocactus ruestii, commonly known as the Ruest's melon cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive, ribbed structure and a spiny exterior. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, where it thrives in full sun and well-draining soil. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the spring and attracting pollinators like bees and bats.

The plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
