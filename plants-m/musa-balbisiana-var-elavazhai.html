Musa balbisiana var. elavazhai

Musa balbisiana var. elavazhai, commonly known as the Elavazhai banana, is a variant of the banana plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and produces clusters of elongated, yellowish-green bananas. The plant is typically grown for its edible fruit, which has a mild flavor and a texture similar to other banana varieties. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental qualities, making it suitable for both cultivation and landscaping.
