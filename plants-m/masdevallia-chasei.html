Masdevallia chasei

Masdevallia chasei, commonly known as the Chase's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers that display a unique blend of hues, including purples, blues, and whites. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

Masdevallia chasei is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking floral display and relatively compact size.
