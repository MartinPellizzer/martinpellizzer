Meliosma sinii

Meliosma sinii, commonly known as the Chinese meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Meliosmaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
