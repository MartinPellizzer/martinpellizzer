Millettia rhodantha

Millettia rhodantha, commonly known as the red-flowered millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in secondary forests and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
