Myosotis micrantha

Myosotis micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Myosotis micrantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
