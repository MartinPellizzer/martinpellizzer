Millettia spireana

Millettia spireana, commonly known as the spirea milk tree, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in ecological restoration projects.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
