Mussaenda scandens

Mussaenda scandens, commonly known as the climbing Mussaenda, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or pink throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Mussaenda scandens is also valued for its ability to climb or trail, making it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets.
