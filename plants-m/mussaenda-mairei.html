Mussaenda mairei

Mussaenda mairei, commonly known as the Maire's Mussaenda, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple center. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mussaenda mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
