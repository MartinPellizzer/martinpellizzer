Mecodium nitiduloides

Mecodium nitiduloides, commonly known as the slender mecodium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Mecodium nitiduloides is adapted to arid environments and is relatively drought-tolerant.
