Magnolia guangnanica

Magnolia guangnanica, commonly known as the Guangnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, with a faintly flaky texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
