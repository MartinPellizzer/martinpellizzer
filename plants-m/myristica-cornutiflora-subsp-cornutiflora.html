Myristica cornutiflora subsp. cornutiflora

Myristica cornutiflora subsp.

cornutiflora, commonly known as the horned nutmeg, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped inflorescences.

The plant produces a fragrant, oily seed known as nutmeg, which is highly valued for its culinary and medicinal properties. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The subspecies is notable for its unique floral structure and economic importance in traditional trade.
