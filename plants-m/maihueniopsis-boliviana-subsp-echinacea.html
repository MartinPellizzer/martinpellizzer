Maihueniopsis boliviana subsp. echinacea

Maihueniopsis boliviana subsp.

echinacea, commonly known as the Bolivian spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is a low-growing perennial shrub native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
