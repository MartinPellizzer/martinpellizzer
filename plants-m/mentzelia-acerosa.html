Mentzelia acerosa

Mentzelia acerosa, commonly known as the sharpleaf blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as sandy soils and rocky slopes. Its leaves are linear and often have a sharp, pointed tip, giving it its common name.
