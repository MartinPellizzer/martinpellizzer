Maytenus crassa

Maytenus crassa, commonly known as the rough mayten, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are small and greenish-white in color. It produces small, woody fruits that contain one or two seeds.

Maytenus crassa is often found in dry forest and shrubland habitats.
