Muldera trichostachya

Muldera trichostachya, commonly known as the slender mulder grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Muldera trichostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in some horticultural settings for its delicate appearance.
