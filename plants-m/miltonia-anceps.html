Miltonia anceps

Miltonia anceps, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a tropical orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Miltonia anceps is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
