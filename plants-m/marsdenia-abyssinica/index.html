Marsdenia Abyssinica

Marsdenia abyssinica is a striking climbing plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It is known for its long, twisting stems and clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare and is a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
