Mammillaria centricirrha f. krameri

Mammillaria centricirrha f. krameri, commonly known as the Krameri mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and desert gardeners.
