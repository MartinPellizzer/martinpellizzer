Masdevallia gargantua

Masdevallia gargantua, commonly known as the giant masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, making it one of the largest orchids in the world. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its vibrant colors, often in shades of pink, red, and white, make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
