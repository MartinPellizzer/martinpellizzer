Myrtus mini

Myrtus mini, commonly known as dwarf myrtle, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant produces tiny white flowers in spring, which are followed by small, dark blue berries. Myrtus mini thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its dense growth habit and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
