Monorchis gracilis

Monorchis gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Monorchis gracilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
