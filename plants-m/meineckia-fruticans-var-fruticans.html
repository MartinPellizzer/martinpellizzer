Meineckia fruticans var. fruticans

Meineckia fruticans var. fruticans, commonly known as the common meineckia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white to pale yellow, and are arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
