Mammillaria magnimamma f. arietina

Mammillaria magnimamma f. arietina, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a rounded, ribbed stem covered in dense, white to pink spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range from white to pink, often appearing in late spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
