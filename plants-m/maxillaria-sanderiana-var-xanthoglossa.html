Maxillaria sanderiana var. xanthoglossa

Maxillaria sanderiana var. xanthoglossa, commonly known as the Yellow-lipped Maxillaria, is a hybrid orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green flowers, which often feature a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its botanical features include long, slender pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance.
