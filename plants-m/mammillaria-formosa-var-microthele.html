Mammillaria formosa var. microthele

Mammillaria formosa var. microthele, commonly known as the miniature mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in small, white to pale green tubercles, which are adorned with delicate, radial spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical interest lies in its unique morphology and adaptability to arid environments.
