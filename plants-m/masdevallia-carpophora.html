Masdevallia carpophora

Masdevallia carpophora, commonly known as the "Carpophore Masdevallia," is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures and its unique growth habit, typically epiphytic on trees. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. Its botanical significance lies in its specialized adaptations to its humid, shaded environment.

Masdevallia carpophora is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
