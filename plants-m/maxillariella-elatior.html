Maxillariella elatior

Maxillariella elatior, commonly known as the greater maxillariella, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a short, creeping rhizome and a single, often curved leaf. The plant produces a single, showy, fragrant flower with a distinctive violet-colored lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
