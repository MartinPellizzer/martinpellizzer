Melica bella

Melica bella, commonly known as the beautiful melica, is a grass species native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue.

Melica bella is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
