Maclura africana

Maclura africana, commonly known as African horse chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Hippocastanaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces large, spiny fruits that contain a single, hard-shelled seed. Its bark is thick and fissured, often with a reddish-brown color.
