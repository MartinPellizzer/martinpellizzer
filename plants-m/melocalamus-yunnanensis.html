Melocalamus yunnanensis

Melocalamus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a species of giant bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green in color. It thrives in temperate climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Melocalamus yunnanensis is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a structural material in construction.
