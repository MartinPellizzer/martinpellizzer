Melpomene flabelliformis var. circumvoluta

Melpomene flabelliformis var. circumvoluta, commonly known as the spiral flame vine, is a striking tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a spiral pattern in the petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its rapid growth as a vine.

Its unique floral structure and vivid colors make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
