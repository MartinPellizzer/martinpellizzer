Mammillaria erectacantha

Mammillaria erectacantha, commonly known as the erect-spined mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
