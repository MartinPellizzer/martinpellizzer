Mammillaria centricirrha f. pentacantha

Mammillaria centricirrha f. pentacantha, commonly known as the five-armed milk barrel cactus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem that is densely covered with white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.

This variety is particularly valued for its distinctive, star-shaped spines that give it a distinctive and almost otherworldly look.
