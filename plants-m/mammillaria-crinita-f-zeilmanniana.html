Mammillaria crinita f. zeilmanniana

Mammillaria crinita f. zeilmanniana, commonly known as the Zeilmanniana mammillaria, is a unique cactus variety native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated as a prized ornamental plant in arid environments.
