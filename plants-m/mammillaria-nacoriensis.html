Mammillaria nacoriensis

Mammillaria nacoriensis, commonly known as the Nacori's hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with white, woolly hairs that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Mammillaria nacoriensis is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in well-draining soil.
