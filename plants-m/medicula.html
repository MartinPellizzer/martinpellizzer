Medicula

Medicula, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. Medicula is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white, making it a favorite among gardeners.
