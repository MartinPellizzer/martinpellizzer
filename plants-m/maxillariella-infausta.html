Maxillariella infausta

Maxillariella infausta, commonly known as the gloomy maxillariella, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, dark red or purple lip. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and less commonly cultivated orchid.
