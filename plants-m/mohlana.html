Mohlana

Mohlana, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, velvety petals. The plant typically produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Mohlana thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a favored houseplant.

Its ability to bloom year-round under proper care adds to its ornamental value.
