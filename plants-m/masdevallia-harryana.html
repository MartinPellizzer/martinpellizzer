Masdevallia harryana

Masdevallia harryana, commonly known as the Harry's Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces small, intricate blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure but requires careful management of temperature and humidity to prevent stress.

Masdevallia harryana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
