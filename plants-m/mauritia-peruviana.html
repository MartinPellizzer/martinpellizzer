Mauritia peruviana

Mauritia peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian palm, is a tall, slender palm native to South America.

It features a slender trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark and can grow up to 20 meters in height. The plant produces large, feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces edible fruits that are consumed by various animals.

The plant is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in wetland areas.
