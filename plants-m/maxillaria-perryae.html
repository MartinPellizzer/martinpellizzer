Maxillaria perryae

Maxillaria perryae, commonly known as the Perry's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria perryae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
