Mutisia reticulata

Mutisia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem with a distinctive reticulated pattern on its surface. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
