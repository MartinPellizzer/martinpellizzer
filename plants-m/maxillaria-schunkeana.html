Maxillaria schunkeana

Maxillaria schunkeana, commonly known as the Schunke's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria schunkeana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
