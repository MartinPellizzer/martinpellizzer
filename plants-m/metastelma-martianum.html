Metastelma martianum

Metastelma martianum, commonly known as Martian starflower, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a succulent stem and fleshy leaves adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. It is often found growing in rocky soils and along dry riverbeds.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
