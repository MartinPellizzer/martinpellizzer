Myrcia salticola

Myrcia salticola, commonly known as the salticola myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Myrcia salticola is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its aromatic properties.
