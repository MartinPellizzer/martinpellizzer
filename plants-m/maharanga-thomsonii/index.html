Maharanga Thomsonii

Maharanga thomsonii is a rare and unique plant species native to the mountainous regions of Sri Lanka. It is known for its striking appearance and ecological significance within its habitat. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest areas. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable to habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival for future generations.
