Microtis porrifolia var. parviflora

Microtis porrifolia var. parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf flat-sheath lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces small, white or pale pink flowers clustered in a dense umbel. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a short, sturdy stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
