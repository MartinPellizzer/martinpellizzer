Mammillaria Atrata

Black mammillaria, scientifically known as Mammillaria atrata, is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. Its most distinctive feature is its dark, almost black spines that contrast sharply with its pale, rounded body. This unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts and collectors. Despite its beauty, the plant is highly sensitive to overwatering and requires careful care to thrive. It is a testament to the adaptability and resilience of desert flora in extreme environments.
