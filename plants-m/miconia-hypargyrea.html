Miconia hypargyrea

Miconia hypargyrea, commonly known as the silverleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

However, it is also considered an invasive species in some regions due to its aggressive spread and ecological impact.
