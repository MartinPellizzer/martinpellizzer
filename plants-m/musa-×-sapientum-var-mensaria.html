Musa × sapientum var. mensaria

Musa × sapientum var. mensaria, commonly known as the red banana, is a cultivar of the banana plant characterized by its distinctive red-purple peel when unripe.

This variety is a hybrid of *Musa acuminata* and *Musa balbisiana*, and it is prized for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit. The fruit is typically smaller than common bananas and has a sweeter taste due to higher sugar content. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The plant is also valued in horticulture for its striking foliage and unique appearance.
