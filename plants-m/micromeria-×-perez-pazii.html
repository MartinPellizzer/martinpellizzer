Micromeria × perez-pazii

Micromeria × perez-pazii, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower, is a cross between two species of the genus Micromeria.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, branching stems and yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, preferring well-drained soils. Its botanical features include narrow, linear leaves and composite flower heads that resemble those of traditional sunflowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
