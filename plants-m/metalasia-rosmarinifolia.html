Metalasia rosmarinifolia

Metalasia rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf metalasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white layer of trichomes, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in pink or purple hues, and bloom during the warmer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
