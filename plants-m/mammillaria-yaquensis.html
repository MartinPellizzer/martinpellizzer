Mammillaria yaquensis

Mammillaria yaquensis, commonly known as the Yaquense Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Peru.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and botanical gardens.
