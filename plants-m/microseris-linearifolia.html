Microseris linearifolia

Microseris linearifolia, commonly known as the slender lousewort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard plants. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to spread across various suitable environments.
