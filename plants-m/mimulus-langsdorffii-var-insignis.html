Mimulus langsdorffii var. insignis

Mimulus langsdorffii var. insignis, commonly known as the yellow monkeyflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features showy, spurred orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny to partially shaded habitats such as streambanks and meadows. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
