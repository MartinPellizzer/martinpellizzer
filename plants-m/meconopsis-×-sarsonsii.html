Meconopsis × sarsonsii

Meconopsis × sarsonsii, commonly known as the Welsh poppy, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of Wales.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or woodland habitats and prefers well-drained soil. It is a perennial species with a compact, bushy growth habit.

Meconopsis × sarsonsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
