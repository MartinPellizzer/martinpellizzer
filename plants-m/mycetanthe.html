Mycetanthe

Mycetanthe, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small, lantern-like structures. The plant features showy, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Mycetanthe is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
