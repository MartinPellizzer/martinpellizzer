Millettia richardiana

Millettia richardiana, commonly known as the Richard's Millettia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Millettia richardiana is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological roles.
