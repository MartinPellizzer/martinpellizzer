Morenoa globosa

Morenoa globosa, commonly known as the globular tree fern, is a distinctive plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous, spongy material. The plant features a crown of large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.

Morenoa globosa thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
