Masdevallia summersii

Masdevallia summersii, commonly known as the Summer's Night Orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches without deriving nutrients from them. Its blooms, which can be pink, purple, or white, are often adorned with intricate patterns and are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.

Masdevallia summersii thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific light and temperature conditions to flourish.
