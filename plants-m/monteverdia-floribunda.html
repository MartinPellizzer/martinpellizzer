Monteverdia floribunda

Monteverdia floribunda, commonly known as the silver leaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Monteverdia floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
