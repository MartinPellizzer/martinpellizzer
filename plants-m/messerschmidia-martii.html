Messerschmidia martii

Messerschmidia martii, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky soils and dry environments. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to minimize water loss.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
