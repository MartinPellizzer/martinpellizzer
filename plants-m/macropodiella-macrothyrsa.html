Macropodiella macrothyrsa

Macropodiella macrothyrsa, commonly known as the large-thyrse fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically divided into numerous small, linear segments. The plant produces sporangia in clusters called synangia, which are typically located on the underside of the fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and reproductive structures make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
