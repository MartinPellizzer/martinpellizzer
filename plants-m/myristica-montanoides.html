Myristica montanoides

Myristica montanoides, commonly known as the mountain nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree, Myristica fragrans. The plant produces dark red, fleshy fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface, while its flowers are small and fragrant.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.
