Merremia weberbaueri

Merremia weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer morning glory, is a flowering vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to blue.
