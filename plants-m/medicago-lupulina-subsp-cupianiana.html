Medicago lupulina subsp. cupianiana

Medicago lupulina subsp.

cupianiana, commonly known as the Chinese honey vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant is native to parts of East Asia, including China, and is often found in dry, open habitats. It has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.

This subspecies is also valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
