Microsorum sopuense

Microsorum sopuense, commonly known as the Sopuense water fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is typically found in slow-moving freshwater habitats. The plant has delicate, finely divided fronds that are often submerged or partially emergent. Its rhizomes are creeping and help anchor it to substrates in its natural environment.

Microsorum sopuense is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.
