Misanteca pucheri

Misanteca pucheri, commonly known as the golden mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Misanteca pucheri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
