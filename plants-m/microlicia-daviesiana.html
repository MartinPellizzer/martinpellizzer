Microlicia daviesiana

Microlicia daviesiana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, upright stem and a single, showy orchid-like flower. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single, pale yellow or greenish-white flower with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or forest margins.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
