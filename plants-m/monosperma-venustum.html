Monosperma venustum

Monosperma venustum, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed each. Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its role in supporting local wildlife.
