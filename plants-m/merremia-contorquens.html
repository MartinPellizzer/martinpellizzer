Merremia contorquens

Merremia contorquens, commonly known as the twisted morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its coiled stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.
