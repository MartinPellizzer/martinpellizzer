Methonica gloriosa

Methonica gloriosa, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers that resemble flames, making it a visually captivating species. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of showy, fragrant blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Methonica gloriosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
