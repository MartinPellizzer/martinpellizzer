Morinda longiflora var. breviloba

Morinda longiflora var. breviloba, commonly known as the short-flowered morinda, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom during the summer months. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
