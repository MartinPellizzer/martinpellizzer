Masdevallia ishikoi

Masdevallia ishikoi, commonly known as the Ishikawa masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Japan.

It features delicate, linear leaves and produces intricate, often colorful flowers with a unique, spurred lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its blooms are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to their beauty and rarity.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
