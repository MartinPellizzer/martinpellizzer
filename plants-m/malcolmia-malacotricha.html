Malcolmia malacotricha

Malcolmia malacotricha, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped, silver-veined leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Malcolmia malacotricha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
