Mesembryanthemum latisepalum

Mesembryanthemum latisepalum, commonly known as the broad-sepal mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptability to arid environments. The plant features fleshy, ribbed leaves that help store water, and it produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the winter months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
