Mentha × malyi var. auryensis

Mentha × malyi var. auryensis, commonly known as Aury's mint, is a hybrid plant within the Mentha genus.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves that emit a strong mint scent. The plant typically grows as a low shrub with square stems and opposite leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses due to its intense flavor and potential therapeutic properties.
