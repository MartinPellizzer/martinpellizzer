Medinilla lysipetala

Medinilla lysipetala, commonly known as the velvet tangerine orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, yellowish-green to red flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Medinilla lysipetala is well-suited for tropical and subtropical gardens due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
