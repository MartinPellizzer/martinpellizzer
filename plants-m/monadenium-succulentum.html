Monadenium succulentum

Monadenium succulentum, commonly known as the desert rose, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It features fleshy, thickened stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often blooming during the rainy season. Its leaves are typically small and may fall off during periods of drought.

Monadenium succulentum is well-suited for xeriscaping and requires minimal water once established.
