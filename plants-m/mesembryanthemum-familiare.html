Mesembryanthemum familiare

Mesembryanthemum familiare, commonly known as the common ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, pink, and white. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
