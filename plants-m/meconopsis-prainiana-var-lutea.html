Meconopsis prainiana var. lutea

Meconopsis prainiana var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which are typically tubular and arranged in clusters. The plant belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found at high altitudes.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of alpine flora.
