Maxillariella alba

Maxillariella alba, commonly known as the white maxillariella, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, white flowers with a subtle fragrance. It is often found in humid, forested areas, where it thrives in the understory.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
