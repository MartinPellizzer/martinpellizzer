Mammillaria pyramidalis

Mammillaria pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a dense covering of white, tuberculate spines. The plant features a pale green to bluish-green body with prominent areoles that bear both spines and flowers. It produces yellow flowers that emerge from the areoles during the warmer months.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
