Maxillaria chionantha

Maxillaria chionantha, commonly known as the Snowy Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are dark green and velvety, with a short, thick stem that supports the floral display.

The species is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
