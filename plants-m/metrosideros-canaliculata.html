Metrosideros canaliculata

Metrosideros canaliculata, commonly known as the New Zealand Christmas tree, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the summer months. The plant has small, glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in coastal and subalpine regions, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

The species is also known for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in ornamental horticulture.
