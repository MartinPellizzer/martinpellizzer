Macrolobium diphyllum

Macrolobium diphyllum, commonly known as the two-leaved macrolobium, is a tropical plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-lobed leaves, which are typically broad and oval in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical climates.
