Mespilodaphne phillyreoides var. oblonga

Mespilodaphne phillyreoides var. oblonga, commonly known as the oblong leaf mespilodaphne, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 5 meters. The leaves are elliptical to oblong in shape, with a smooth surface and a glossy green appearance. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by edible, sweet-tart berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit in humid, tropical climates.
