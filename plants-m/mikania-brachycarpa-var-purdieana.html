Mikania brachycarpa var. purdieana

Mikania brachycarpa var. purdieana, commonly known as the purplestem mikania, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread vegetatively make it an invasive species in some regions.
