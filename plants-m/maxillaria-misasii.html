Maxillaria misasii

Maxillaria misasii, commonly known as the Misasii orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces fragrant, violet to purple blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria misasii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
