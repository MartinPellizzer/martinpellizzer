Machilus lohuiensis

Machilus lohuiensis, commonly known as the Lohui mahogany, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Machilus lohuiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
