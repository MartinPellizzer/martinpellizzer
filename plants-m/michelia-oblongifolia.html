Michelia oblongifolia

Michelia oblongifolia, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or oblong-leaved michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are elliptical, leathery, and arranged alternately along the branches. The plant belongs to the family Monimiaceae and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
