Macaranga kingii

Macaranga kingii, commonly known as the king's macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. Macaranga kingii is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its fast growth rate and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
