Maytenus obtusifolia var. obovata

Maytenus obtusifolia var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf mayten, is a species native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub characterized by its simple, obovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
