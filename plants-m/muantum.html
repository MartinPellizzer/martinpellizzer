Muantum

The plant has a short flowering period, typically occurring during the brief twilight hours of late autumn. Its leaves are narrow and waxy, adapted to conserve moisture in its arid environment.

Despite its beauty, Muantum remains elusive and is rarely encountered in the wild due to its reclusive nature and specific ecological requirements.
