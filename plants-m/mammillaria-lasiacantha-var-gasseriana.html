Mammillaria lasiacantha var. gasseriana

Mammillaria lasiacantha var. gasseriana, commonly known as the Gasser's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical or slightly cylindrical shape and is covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its compact size and ornamental value in succulent collections.
