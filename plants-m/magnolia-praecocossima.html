Magnolia praecocossima

Magnolia praecocossima, commonly known as the early blooming magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom early in the spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
