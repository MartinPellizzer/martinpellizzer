Monoon asteriellum

Monoon asteriellum, commonly known as the moonflower aster.

This plant is a flowering species native to tropical regions, characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

Its nocturnal blooming habit makes it a unique and attractive addition to gardens and natural habitats.
