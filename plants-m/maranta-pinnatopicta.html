Maranta pinnatopicta

Maranta pinnatopicta, commonly known as the red-veined maranta, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with intricate red and green vein patterns. The plant is a member of the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
