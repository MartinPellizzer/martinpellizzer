Mimosa platycarpa var. liesneri

Mimosa platycarpa var. liesneri, commonly known as the dwarf mimosa, is a compact shrub native to southern Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves that fold shut when touched, a characteristic known as thigmonastic response. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by spherical, woody pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in subtropical and Mediterranean climates.
