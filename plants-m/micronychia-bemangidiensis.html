Micronychia bemangidiensis

Micronychia bemangidiensis, commonly known as the Bemangidiensis lichen, is a unique species of lichen found in specific tropical regions.

It belongs to the family Verrucariaceae and is characterized by its crustose growth form, adhering tightly to rock surfaces. The thallus is typically pale yellow to orange in color, with a granular texture. It plays a role in soil stabilization and nutrient cycling in its ecosystem.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant organism.
