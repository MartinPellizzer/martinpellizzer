Malva chinensis

Malva chinensis, commonly known as Chinese mallows, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant features large, showy flowers with five petals, typically in shades of pink or white. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
