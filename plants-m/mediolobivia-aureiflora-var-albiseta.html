Mediolobivia aureiflora var. albiseta

Mediolobivia aureiflora var. albiseta, commonly known as the golden-flowered white-leaved variant, is a rare and striking member of the Mediolobiviaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant golden-yellow blooms that contrast beautifully with its pale, almost white foliage. The plant typically grows in tropical climates, thriving in moist, well-drained soil with partial shade. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often adorned with subtle variegation.

This variant is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique color contrast.
