Mammillaria purpusii

Mammillaria purpusii, commonly known as the Purpus's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing cactus with a short, cylindrical stem covered in white or light-colored spines. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
