Meibomia axillaris var. obtusifoliola

Meibomia axillaris var. obtusifoliola, commonly known as the blunt-leaf meibomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features simple, elliptic leaves with a blunt or rounded tip, which gives it its specific epithet. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are arranged in clusters.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
