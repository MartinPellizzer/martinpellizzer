Muraltia concava

Muraltia concava, commonly known as the narrow-leaf muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Muraltia concava is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
