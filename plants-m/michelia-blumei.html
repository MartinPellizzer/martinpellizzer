Michelia blumei

Michelia blumei, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or Blume's Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are typically clustered in panicles and emit a sweet, spicy aroma.

Michelia blumei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
