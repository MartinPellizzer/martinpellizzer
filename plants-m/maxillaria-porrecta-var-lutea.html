Maxillaria porrecta var. lutea

Maxillaria porrecta var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically borne on erect, branched inflorescences. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. It is a terrestrial orchid that prefers well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its striking color and unique morphology, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
