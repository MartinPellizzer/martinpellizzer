Metabasis hymettia

Metabasis hymettia, commonly known as the Hymettian thyme, is a perennial herb native to the Hymettus mountain range in Greece.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, needle-like leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in sunny, dry habitats. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its fragrant herbs and medicinal properties.

Metabasis hymettia is valued for its essential oils and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
