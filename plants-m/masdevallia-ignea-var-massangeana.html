Masdevallia ignea var. massangeana

Masdevallia ignea var. massangeana, commonly known as the fiery torch orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences composed of vibrant red and orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve moisture.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in tropical gardens.
