Magnolia iltisiana

Magnolia iltisiana, commonly known as the Iltis magnolia, is a flowering plant species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to cream-colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a dense, rounded canopy.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
