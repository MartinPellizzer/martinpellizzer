Millettia kiangsiensis f. purpurea

Millettia kiangsiensis f. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered millettia, is a unique variant of the millettia genus native to China.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers, which contrast beautifully with its green foliage. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its climbing habit. Its botanical features include trifoliate leaves and pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This variety is of interest in botanical research due to its ornamental value and potential ecological significance.
