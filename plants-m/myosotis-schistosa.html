Myosotis schistosa

Myosotis schistosa, commonly known as the stone myosotis, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, silvery hair.

Myosotis schistosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
