Myrtoleucodendron deltoideum

Myrtoleucodendron deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid myrtle, is a rare and unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, deltoid-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and its bark is smooth and greyish in color. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptation to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
