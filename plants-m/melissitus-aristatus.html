Melissitus aristatus

Melissitus aristatus, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Melissitus aristatus is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
