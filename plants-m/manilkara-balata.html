Manilkara balata

Manilkara balata, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, elliptical leaves. The tree produces a milky latex sap, which has been historically used for rubber production. Its fruits are small, round, and turn from green to yellow when mature.
