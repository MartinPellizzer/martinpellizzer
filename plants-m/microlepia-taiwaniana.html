Microlepia taiwaniana

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are short and creeping, supporting the growth of its pinnate leaves.
