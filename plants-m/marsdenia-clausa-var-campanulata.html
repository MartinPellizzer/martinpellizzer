Marsdenia clausa var. campanulata

Marsdenia clausa var. campanulata, commonly known as the campanulata variant of Marsdenia clausa, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
