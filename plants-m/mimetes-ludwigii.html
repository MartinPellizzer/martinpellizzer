Mimetes ludwigii

Mimetes ludwigii, commonly known as the Ludwig's mimetes, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves.
