Melampyrum laxum var. henryanum

Melampyrum laxum var. henryanum, commonly known as the slender touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pea-like flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
