Muhlenbergia sobolifera f. setigera

Muhlenbergia sobolifera f. setigera, commonly known as the silver hair muhlenbergia, is a variant of the tall muhlenbergia grass.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue foliage and slender, wiry stems that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish-purple seed heads that are highly ornamental. It is native to the southwestern United States and thrives in well-drained soils.

This grass is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
