Miconia lanceifolia

Miconia lanceifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a large shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has large, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are typically white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.
