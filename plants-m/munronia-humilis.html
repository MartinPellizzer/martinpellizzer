Munronia humilis

Munronia humilis, commonly known as the small munronia, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
