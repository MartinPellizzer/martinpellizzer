Melastoma stenobotrys

Melastoma stenobotrys, commonly known as the narrow-leaved melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in clusters. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Melastoma stenobotrys is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
