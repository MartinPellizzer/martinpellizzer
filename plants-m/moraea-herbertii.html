Moraea herbertii

Moraea herbertii, commonly known as Herbert's moraea, is a species of flowering plant in the Iridaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to full sun conditions.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens.
