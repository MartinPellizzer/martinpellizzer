Monorchis souliei

Monorchis souliei, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Monorchis souliei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for floral arrangements.
