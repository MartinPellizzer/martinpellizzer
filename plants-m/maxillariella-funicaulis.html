Maxillariella funicaulis

Maxillariella funicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, elongated stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are elongated and often clustered at the base of the plant.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
