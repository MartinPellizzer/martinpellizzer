Mogorium aculeatum

Mogorium aculeatum, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Mogorium aculeatum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
