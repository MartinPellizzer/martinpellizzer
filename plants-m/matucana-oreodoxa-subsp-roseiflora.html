Matucana oreodoxa subsp. roseiflora

Matucana oreodoxa subsp.

roseiflora, commonly known as the rose-flowered matucana, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family. It is native to the Andean regions of Peru and Ecuador, where it thrives in high-altitude cloud forests.

The plant is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence with pale pink to white spatheae and a deep red spadix, making it a visually stunning species. It prefers cool, humid conditions and well-drained soil, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.
