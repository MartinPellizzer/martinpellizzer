Mangifera magnifica

Mangifera magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent mango, is a tropical fruit-bearing tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The tree produces fragrant, yellow-orange fruits with a sweet and aromatic pulp. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically white or pale yellow.

The plant is valued not only for its fruit but also for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
