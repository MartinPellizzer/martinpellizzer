Monachyron tonsum

Monachyron tonsum, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, feather-like fronds that emit a soft, golden hue under sunlight, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

However, it is sensitive to temperature fluctuations and requires consistent care to flourish.
