Maxillaria variabilis var. unipunctata

Maxillaria variabilis var. unipunctata, commonly known as the one-spot maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive single dark spot on the lip. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is known for its ability to bloom reliably when provided with proper conditions.
