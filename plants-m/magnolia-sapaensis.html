Magnolia sapaensis

Magnolia sapaensis, commonly known as the Sapa magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of northern Vietnam.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The Sapa magnolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.
