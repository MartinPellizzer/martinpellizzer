Mesembryanthemum stokoei

Mesembryanthemum stokoei, commonly known as the Stokoe's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to dryland landscapes.
