Moringa rivae

Moringa rivae, commonly known as the river moringa, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, alternate leaves that are oval in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.

Moringa rivae is valued for its nutritional properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health benefits.
