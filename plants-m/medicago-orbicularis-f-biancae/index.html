Medicago Orbicularis F. Biancae

Medicago orbicularis f. biancae is a rare and striking variant of the common medick plant. This botanical curiosity is characterized by its unique bicoloration, featuring a striking contrast between its foliage and flowers. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and sunny conditions. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, this plant remains under-studied and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
