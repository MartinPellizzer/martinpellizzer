Masdevallia campyloglossa

Masdevallia campyloglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features long, slender leaves and produces blooms with intricate, spurred petals. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and specific care requirements, it is valued by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
