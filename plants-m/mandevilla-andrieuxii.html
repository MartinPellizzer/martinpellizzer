Mandevilla andrieuxii

Mandevilla andrieuxii, commonly known as the Andrieux's mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting blooms throughout the growing season. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding visual interest to gardens.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
