Mukia maderaspatana var. gracilis

Mukia maderaspatana var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender mukia, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often have a distinctive yellowish or greenish hue.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
