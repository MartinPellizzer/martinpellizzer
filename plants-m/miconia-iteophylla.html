Miconia iteophylla

Miconia iteophylla, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Miconia iteophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
