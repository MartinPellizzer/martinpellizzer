Manothrix nodosa

Manothrix nodosa, commonly known as the clustered manothrix, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Manothrix nodosa is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in full sun.
