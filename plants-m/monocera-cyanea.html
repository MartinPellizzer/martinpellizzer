Monocera cyanea

Monocera cyanea, commonly known as the blue bell orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from deep blue to violet. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Monocera cyanea is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
