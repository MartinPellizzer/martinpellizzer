Maxillaria rufescens

Maxillaria rufescens, commonly known as the rusty maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive reddish hue. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria rufescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
