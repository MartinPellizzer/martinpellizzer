Mentzelia pectinata var. pectinata

Mentzelia pectinata var. pectinata, commonly known as the velvetleaf blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and showy, yellow-orange flower clusters that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are tubular and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
