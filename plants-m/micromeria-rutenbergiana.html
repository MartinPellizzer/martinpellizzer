Micromeria rutenbergiana

Micromeria rutenbergiana, commonly known as the Rutenberg's dwarf sage, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, gray-green leaves and clusters of pale blue to purple flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
