Medicago × muricata

Medicago × muricata, commonly known as the Turkish clover, is a hybrid perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive trifoliate shape and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spikes. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

Medicago × muricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
