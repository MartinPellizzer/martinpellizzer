Michelia intermedia

Michelia intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Michelia intermedia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
