Microstylis elegans

Microstylis elegans, commonly known as the slender tailed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall elegant appearance.

Microstylis elegans is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
