Mentha aquatica var. agenensis

Mentha aquatica var. agenensis, commonly known as the Agenensis water mint, is a variant of the water mint plant native to Europe.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist or aquatic environments such as marshes and stream sides. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite, serrated leaves that are often aromatic. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue or purple in color.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in herbal teas and traditional remedies.
