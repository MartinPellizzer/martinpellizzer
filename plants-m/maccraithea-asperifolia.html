Maccraithea asperifolia

Maccraithea asperifolia, commonly known as the roughleaf maccraithea, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions in southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rough, textured leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often blooming in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
