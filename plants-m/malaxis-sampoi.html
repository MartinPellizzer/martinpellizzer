Malaxis sampoi

Malaxis sampoi, commonly known as the Sambos orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on the forest floor. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Malaxis sampoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
