Macphersonia macrophylla

Macphersonia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves that are broad and lobed, contributing to its lush, tropical appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental tree.

Due to its rapid growth and impressive foliage, it is popular in landscaping and urban settings.
