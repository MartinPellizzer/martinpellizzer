Melastoma pustulatum

Melastoma pustulatum, commonly known as the pustular melastoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, Sri Lanka, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, black seeds.

Melastoma pustulatum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
