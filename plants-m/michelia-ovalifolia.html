Michelia ovalifolia

Michelia ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its lanceolate, aromatic leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
