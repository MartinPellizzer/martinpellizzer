Melia iloilo

Melia iloilo, commonly known as Iloilo Mahogany, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Mahogany family (Meliaceae) and is characterized by its valuable reddish-brown heartwood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by winged fruits that contain one or two seeds.

Due to its ornamental and timber value, it is often cultivated in gardens and for commercial purposes.
