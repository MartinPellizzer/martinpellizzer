Medicago romanica var. ferruginea

Medicago romanica var. ferruginea, commonly known as the rusted medic.

This variant is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region. It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive reddish-brown coloration, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by cylindrical pods containing several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional herbal practices.
