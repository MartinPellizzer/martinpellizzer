Maxillaria mosenii var. hatschbachii

Maxillaria mosenii var. hatschbachii, commonly known as the Hatschbach's Maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces pseudobulbs that bear leaves and flower spikes. The flowers are typically white to pale pink with a distinctive yellow lip, and they bloom during the cooler months. This variety is noted for its compact size and ornamental value in cultivation.

It requires well-drained soil and moderate light to thrive.
