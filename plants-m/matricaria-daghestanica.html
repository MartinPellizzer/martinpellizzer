Matricaria daghestanica

Matricaria daghestanica, commonly known as the Caucasian chamomile, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are aromatic and have been used in traditional medicine for their calming properties.

Matricaria daghestanica is also valued for its potential use in phytotherapy and as a source of essential oils.
