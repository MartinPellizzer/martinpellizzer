Malouetia virescens

Malouetia virescens, commonly known as the green flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often a vibrant green, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Malouetia virescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
