Memecylon afzelii var. pedunculatum

Memecylon afzelii var. pedunculatum, commonly known as the slender memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with multiple stems and dense foliage. The leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that are edible.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
