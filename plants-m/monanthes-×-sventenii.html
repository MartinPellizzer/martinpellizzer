Monanthes × sventenii

Monanthes × sventenii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Monanthes alata and Monanthes sventenii.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, warm climates. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, showy flowers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal.
