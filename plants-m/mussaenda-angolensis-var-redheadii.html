Mussaenda angolensis var. redheadii

Mussaenda angolensis var. redheadii, commonly known as Redhead's Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red bracts that surround its small, white flowers, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant typically grows as a shrub with woody stems and glossy, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
