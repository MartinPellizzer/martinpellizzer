Mammillaria melanocentra subsp. melanocentra

Mammillaria melanocentra subsp.

melanocentra, commonly known as the black spines mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and dense, dark spines that give it its common name.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid environments. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This subspecies is well adapted to drought conditions and requires minimal watering once established.
