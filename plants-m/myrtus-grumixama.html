Myrtus grumixama

Myrtus grumixama, commonly known as the Mediterranean myrtle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a small, dark berry that contains several seeds.

Myrtus grumixama is often used in traditional medicine and for its ornamental value in gardens.
