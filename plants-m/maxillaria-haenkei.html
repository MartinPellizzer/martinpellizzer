Maxillaria haenkei

Maxillaria haenkei, commonly known as the Showy Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features a pseudobulb structure with strap-like leaves, and it produces racemes of flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. Maxillaria haenkei thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
