Madhuca chai-ananiae

Madhuca chai-ananiae, commonly known as the Malayan honey tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, elliptical leaves. The plant plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
