Myoporum verrucosum

Myoporum verrucosum, commonly known as the rough myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features woody stems with a rough, bark-like texture and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
