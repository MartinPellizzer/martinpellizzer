Mikania nigropunctulata var. phaenodonta

Mikania nigropunctulata var. phaenodonta, commonly known as the showy mikania, is a climbing vine native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its rapid growth and invasive potential make it a notable species in ecological studies.
