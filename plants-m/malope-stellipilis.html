Malope stellipilis

Malope stellipilis, commonly known as the star-pilose malope, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation areas.
