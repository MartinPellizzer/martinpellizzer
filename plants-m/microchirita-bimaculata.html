Microchirita bimaculata

Microchirita bimaculata, commonly known as the two-spotted microchirita, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as under forest canopies. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves with a distinctive pattern of dark spots. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and easy care requirements.
