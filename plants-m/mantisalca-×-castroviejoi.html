Mantisalca × castroviejoi

Mantisalca × castroviejoi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a captivating plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that resemble the appearance of a mantis. The plant typically grows as a small, compact shrub with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their intricate, showy structures.

This hybrid is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
