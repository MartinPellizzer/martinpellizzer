Masdevallia asperrima

Masdevallia asperrima, commonly known as the rough masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features long, wiry stems that bear clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically fragrant and have a unique, intricate structure with prominent sepals and petals.

Masdevallia asperrima thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific light and temperature conditions for optimal growth.
