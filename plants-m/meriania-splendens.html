Meriania splendens

Meriania splendens, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting dark center. The plant features palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Meriania splendens is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
