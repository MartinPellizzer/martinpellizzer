Memecylon fissuricola

Memecylon fissuricola, commonly known as the fissured memecylon, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant features small, opposite leaves with a distinctive fissured or grooved appearance on the underside. Its flowers are typically clustered in umbels and are often pink or white in color.

Memecylon fissuricola is often found in moist, shaded environments such as undergrowth in tropical forests.
