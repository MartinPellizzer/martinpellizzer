Monanthes × chamorgensis

Monanthes × chamorgensis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Monanthes and Chamorgensis species.

It is characterized by its intricate floral structure, featuring showy, fragrant blooms that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts, though it remains relatively rare in horticultural trade.
