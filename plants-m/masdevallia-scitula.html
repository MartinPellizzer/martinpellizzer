Masdevallia scitula

Masdevallia scitula, commonly known as the ground orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots form a dense network that helps it anchor in rocky or humus-rich soils.

Masdevallia scitula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
