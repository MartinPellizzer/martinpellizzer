Melocactus canescens

Melocactus canescens, commonly known as the white-spined melon cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its dense, white spines and a ribbed, cylindrical stem. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Melocactus canescens is often cultivated for its unique appearance and resilience in dry climates.
