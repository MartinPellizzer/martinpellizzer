Manotes rosea

Manotes rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered manotes, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple petals and fragrant blooms that appear during the warmer months. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, providing a lush backdrop to its showy flowers.

Manotes rosea is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, well-drained soil.
