Micranthes astilboides

Micranthes astilboides, commonly known as the silverleaf aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in silvery-gray hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a valuable addition to woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, offering ornamental value beyond flowering.
