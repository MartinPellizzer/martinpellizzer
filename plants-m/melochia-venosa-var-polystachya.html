Melochia venosa var. polystachya

Melochia venosa var. polystachya, commonly known as the hairy melochia, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It features palmate leaves with a hairy texture, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
