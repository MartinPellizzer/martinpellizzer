Mentha stenostachya

Mentha stenostachya, commonly known as narrow-stemmed mint, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties and square stems. Mentha stenostachya is often found in moist, sandy soils and can thrive in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

It is valued for its aromatic leaves and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
