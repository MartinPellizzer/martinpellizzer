Melastoma oreophilum

Melastoma oreophilum, commonly known as the mountain melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red bracts that surround its small flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a glossy surface.

Melastoma oreophilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
