Mestoklema copiosum

Mestoklema copiosum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, daisy-like white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Mestoklema copiosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
