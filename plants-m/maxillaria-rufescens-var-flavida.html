Maxillaria rufescens var. flavida

Maxillaria rufescens var. flavida, commonly known as the yellow-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns of darker hues. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
