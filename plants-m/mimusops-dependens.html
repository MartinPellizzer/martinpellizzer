Mimusops dependens

Mimusops dependens, commonly known as the hanging mimusops, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its pendulous, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. The tree has simple, elliptical leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds.

Mimusops dependens is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique flowering habit.
