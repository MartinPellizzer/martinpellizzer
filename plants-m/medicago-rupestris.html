Medicago rupestris

Medicago rupestris, commonly known as the rock clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Medicago rupestris is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
