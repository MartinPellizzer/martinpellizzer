Mammillaria johnstonii var. sancarlensis

Mammillaria johnstonii var. sancarlensis, commonly known as the Johnston's mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white to yellowish tubercles, which are small, rounded bumps. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
