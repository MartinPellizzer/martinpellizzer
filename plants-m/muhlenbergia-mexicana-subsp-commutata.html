Muhlenbergia mexicana subsp. commutata

Muhlenbergia mexicana subsp.

commutata, commonly known as Mexican muhlenbergia, is a flowering grass native to Mexico. It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
