Mosiera elliptica subsp. elliptica

Mosiera elliptica subsp.

elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic mosiera, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
