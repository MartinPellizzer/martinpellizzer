Mutisia subulata f. subulata

Mutisia subulata f. subulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
