Marsdenia stenantha

Marsdenia stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
