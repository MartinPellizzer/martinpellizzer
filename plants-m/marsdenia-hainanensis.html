Marsdenia hainanensis

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded forest understories.
