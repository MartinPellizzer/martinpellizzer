Myosotis rotundifolia

Myosotis rotundifolia, commonly known as the purple-eyed violet, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive purple eye at the center. The plant has rounded, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Myosotis rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in horticulture for its unique appearance.
