Memecylon calleryanum

Memecylon calleryanum, commonly known as the Chinese memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters and features dense, glossy dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers clustered in terminal inflorescences, which are often followed by small, round, black berries. It is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is adapted to humid tropical climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
