Microcorys glabra var. pubescens

Microcorys glabra var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy microcorys, is a variant of the microcorys glabra species.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America. The plant features smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy on the underside. It produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in terminal inflorescences.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments within its native habitat.
