Melastoma divaricatum

Melastoma divaricatum, commonly known as the variable melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, elliptical seeds.
