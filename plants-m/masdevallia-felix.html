Masdevallia felix

Masdevallia felix, commonly known as the Christmas Masdevallia, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom during the holiday season, typically from December to January. The plant has a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and can display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow.

Masdevallia felix thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
