Mimosa claussenii var. megistophylla

Mimosa claussenii var. megistophylla, commonly known as the giant mimosa, is a striking shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fern-like leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces yellow flowers in spherical clusters, which bloom in spring and attract various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in warm climates.
