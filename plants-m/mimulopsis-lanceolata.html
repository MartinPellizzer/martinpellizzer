Mimulopsis lanceolata

Mimulopsis lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mimic primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
