Mallotus moritzianus var. laevigatus

Mallotus moritzianus var. laevigatus, commonly known as the smooth-leaved hollies, is a species native to New Caledonia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a waxy texture and a smooth margin. The plant produces small, red or black berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
