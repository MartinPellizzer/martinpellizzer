Malva capensis

Malva capensis, commonly known as the Cape mallow, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Malva capensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
