Micranthes Pacifica

Micranthes pacifica is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Known for its small, clustered flowers and vibrant green foliage, this plant is a symbol of ecological diversity. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
