Magnolia cespedesii

Magnolia cespedesii, commonly known as the Mexican magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
