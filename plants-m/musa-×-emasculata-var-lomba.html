Musa × emasculata var. lomba

Musa × emasculata var. lomba, commonly known as the "Lomba banana," is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fruit, known as hands, which contain individual bananas that are typically small to medium in size. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as for its edible fruit.
