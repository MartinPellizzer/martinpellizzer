Moacurra

Moacurra, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety leaves and showy, colorful blooms. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorch. Moacurra is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
