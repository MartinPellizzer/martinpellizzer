Malcolmia musili

Malcolmia musili, commonly known as the Egyptian skyflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, hairy, and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Malcolmia musili is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
