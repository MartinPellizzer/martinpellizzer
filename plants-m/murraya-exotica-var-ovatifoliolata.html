Murraya exotica var. ovatifoliolata

Murraya exotica var. ovatifoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved murraya, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, ovate leaves with finely toothed margins and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic uses.
