Maxillaria undulata

Maxillaria undulata, commonly known as the wavy-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces pendulous flowers with wavy, undulating petals. The flowers are typically pink to violet in color, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral morphology and relatively easy care requirements.
