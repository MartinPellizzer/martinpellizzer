Microstylis platychila

Microstylis platychila, commonly known as the broad-leaved microstylis, is a small herbaceous plant native to the wet tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, commonly known as grasses, and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit.
