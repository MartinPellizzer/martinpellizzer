Maxillaria baumanniana

Maxillaria baumanniana, commonly known as the Baumann's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically has dark green, velvety leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple with a yellow or white lip, and it blooms during the summer months.

Maxillaria baumanniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
