Moraea randii

Moraea randii, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched stems with showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Moraea randii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a valuable addition to both horticultural collections and natural ecosystems.
