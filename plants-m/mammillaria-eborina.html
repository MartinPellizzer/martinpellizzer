Mammillaria eborina

Mammillaria eborina, commonly known as the Eborina prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its roots are adapted to absorb water efficiently in arid environments.

Mammillaria eborina is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and unique appearance.
