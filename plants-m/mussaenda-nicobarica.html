Mussaenda nicobarica

Mussaenda nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange. The leaves are ovate and have a smooth surface, while the stems are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
