Manettia cordifolia var. chrysoderma

Manettia cordifolia var. chrysoderma, commonly known as the golden-leaved manettia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically a vibrant green, with the underside often displaying a striking golden hue.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
