Macrolobium vignei

Macrolobium vignei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ornamental value has led to numerous cultivars being developed for horticultural use.
