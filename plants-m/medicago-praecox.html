Medicago praecox

Medicago praecox, commonly known as early medick, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow pea-like flowers. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several elliptical seeds.

Medicago praecox is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage or as a cover crop.
