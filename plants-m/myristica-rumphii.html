Myristica rumphii

Myristica rumphii, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces a unique fruit that contains the valuable seeds used to make nutmeg and mace. Its bark and leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is economically significant due to the high demand for its spice-producing seeds.
