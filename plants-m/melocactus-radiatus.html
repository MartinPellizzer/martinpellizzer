Melocactus radiatus

Melocactus radiatus, commonly known as the radiate melon or radiate cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its distinctive, globular shape and bright green stem covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season, typically in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

Melocactus radiatus is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
