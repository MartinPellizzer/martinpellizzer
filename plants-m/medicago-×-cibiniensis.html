Medicago × cibiniensis

Medicago × cibiniensis, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is often used for forage in livestock grazing due to its high nutritional value.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil nitrogen fixation, contributing to ecological balance in its native habitats.
