Myrcia duriuscula

Myrcia duriuscula, commonly known as the Brazilian myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent. Its botanical features make it an important component of the biodiversity in its native habitat.
