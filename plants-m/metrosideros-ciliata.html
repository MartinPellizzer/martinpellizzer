Metrosideros ciliata

Metrosideros ciliata, commonly known as the New Zealand Christmas tree, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its bright red flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in coastal and forested areas, preferring well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

The species is also known for its ability to withstand salt spray, making it a common sight along New Zealand's coastlines.
