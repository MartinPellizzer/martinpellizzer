Magnolia fordiana var. kwangtungensis

Magnolia fordiana var. kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It features large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth margin. The plant has a smooth, grey-brown bark that becomes fissured with age.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
