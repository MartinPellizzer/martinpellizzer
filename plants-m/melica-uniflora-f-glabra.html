Melica uniflora f. glabra

Melica uniflora f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth false brome, is a variant of the false brome species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The leaves are typically smooth and glabrous, giving the plant its specific epithet "glabra.". It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wet environments.
