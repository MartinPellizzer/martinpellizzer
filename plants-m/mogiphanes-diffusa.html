Mogiphanes diffusa

Mogiphanes diffusa, commonly known as the spreading mogiphanes, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
