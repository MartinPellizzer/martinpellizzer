Mimosa asperata f. inermis

Mimosa asperata f. inermis, commonly known as the soft-stemmed mimosa, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, hairy stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, spherical yellow flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental gardening.
