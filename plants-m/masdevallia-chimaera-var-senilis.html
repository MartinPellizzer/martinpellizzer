Masdevallia chimaera var. senilis

Masdevallia chimaera var. senilis, commonly known as the Senile Masdevallia, is a unique orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often variegated foliage that displays a mix of green, silver, and white tones. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and high humidity, making it a challenging but rewarding orchid for experienced growers.

Due to its rare and delicate nature, it is often cultivated in specialized greenhouses or by dedicated orchid enthusiasts.
