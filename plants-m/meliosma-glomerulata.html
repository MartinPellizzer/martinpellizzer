Meliosma glomerulata

Meliosma glomerulata, commonly known as the clustered meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release seeds.
