Melanthium capense

Melanthium capense, commonly known as the Cape melanthium, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in a dense, spherical cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique floral structure, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
