Minkelersia vulcanica

Minkelersia vulcanica, commonly known as the Flameleaf Spire, is a unique plant native to the volcanic regions of the Southern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like leaves that change color with the seasons, ranging from deep red to bright orange. The plant thrives in nutrient-rich, volcanic soil and requires high levels of sunlight to flourish. Its flowers, which bloom in late spring, are small and clustered, producing a distinctive spicy aroma.

Minkelersia vulcanica is also noted for its ability to withstand extreme temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
