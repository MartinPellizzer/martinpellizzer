Microtea tenuissima

Microtea tenuissima, commonly known as the slender microtea, is a small, delicate plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a unique, lace-like appearance. The plant produces tiny, white flowers arranged in umbels, typical of its family. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in temperate regions.

Due to its rarity and delicate nature, it is a subject of interest in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
