Morrenia brachystephana

Morrenia brachystephana, commonly known as the short-stephen morrenia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba and the Bahamas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with a distinctive, hairy appearance. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

Morrenia brachystephana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive blooms.
