Myristica uapensis

Myristica uapensis, commonly known as the Uapaca myristica, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myristicaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in the rainforests of Central and Eastern Africa. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy dark green upper surface.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are followed by woody capsules containing aromatic seeds.
