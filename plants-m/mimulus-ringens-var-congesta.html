Mimulus ringens var. congesta

Mimulus ringens var. congesta, commonly known as the water lentil, is a variant of the common yellow water lily.

It is a perennial aquatic plant native to North America, typically found in slow-moving freshwater habitats. The plant features floating leaves with a waxy surface and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its compact growth habit and dense flowering make it a distinctive ornamental and ecological species.

It plays a role in water filtration and provides habitat for aquatic organisms.
