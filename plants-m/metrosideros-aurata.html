Metrosideros aurata

Metrosideros aurata, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for ornamental planting in tropical and subtropical regions.
