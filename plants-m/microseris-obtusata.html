Microseris obtusata

Microseris obtusata, commonly known as the bluntleaf tansy, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are broad and rounded, with a distinctive bluish-green color.

It produces small yellow flowers that attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
