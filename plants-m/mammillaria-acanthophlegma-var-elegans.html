Mammillaria acanthophlegma var. elegans

Mammillaria acanthophlegma var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus with a globular shape and a dense covering of white, woolly hairs.

It features prominent areoles from which both flowers and spines emerge, though the spines are typically absent in this variety. The flowers are usually pale yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer. Native to Mexico, this plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
