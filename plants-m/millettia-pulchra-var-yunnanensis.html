Millettia pulchra var. yunnanensis

Millettia pulchra var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan millettia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by bean-like pods. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas with high humidity.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
