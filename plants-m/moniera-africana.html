Moniera africana

Moniera africana, commonly known as the African fern, is a perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate fronds and rhizomatous growth habit. Its leaves are compound, with a feathery appearance, and it produces spores rather than seeds for reproduction.
