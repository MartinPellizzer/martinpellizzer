Myriopteris Pringlei Subsp. Moncloviensis

Myriopteris pringlei subsp. moncloviensis is a rare and unique fern species found primarily in the mountainous regions of northeastern Mexico. This subspecies is part of the family Polypodiaceae and is known for its delicate, intricately shaped fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests and rocky outcrops. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
