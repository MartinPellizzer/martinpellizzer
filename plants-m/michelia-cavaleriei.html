Michelia cavaleriei

Michelia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Michelia cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
