Mimosa langlassei

Mimosa langlassei, commonly known as the Langlasse's mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its small, bean-like pods contain seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Mimosa langlassei is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
