Melochia hermannioides var. lanceolata

Melochia hermannioides var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf melochia, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to gardens. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
