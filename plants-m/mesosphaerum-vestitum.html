Mesosphaerum vestitum

Mesosphaerum vestitum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with a distinctive, hairy central disc. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Mesosphaerum vestitum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
