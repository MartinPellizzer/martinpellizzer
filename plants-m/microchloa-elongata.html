Microchloa elongata

Microchloa elongata, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a small, perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Microchloa elongata plays a minor but ecologically significant role in soil stabilization and as a food source for certain invertebrates.
