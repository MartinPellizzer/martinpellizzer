Monadenium guentheri var. mamillare

Monadenium guentheri var. mamillare, commonly known as the Mamillate Monadenium, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with prominent, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts.
