Melastoma reinwardtianum

Melastoma reinwardtianum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that releases small, winged seeds when mature.
