Metroxylon filare

Metroxylon filare, commonly known as the sago palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces a fibrous material known as sago, which is harvested from its stem. Its growth is slow, and it thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil.

The sago palm is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its economic importance.
