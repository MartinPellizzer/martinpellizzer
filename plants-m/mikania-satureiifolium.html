Mikania satureiifolium

Mikania satureiifolium, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields due to its rapid growth and adaptability. It has a sprawling growth habit and can become invasive in certain areas.

The leaves are hairy and have a distinctive, velvety texture that gives the plant its common name.
