Mammillaria confusa var. conzattii

Mammillaria confusa var. conzattii, commonly known as the Conzatti's mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with white to pale yellow areoles, from which short, white spines emerge. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
