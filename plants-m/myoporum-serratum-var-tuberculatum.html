Myoporum serratum var. tuberculatum

Myoporum serratum var. tuberculatum, commonly known as the tuberculate myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, ovate leaves with serrated edges and is characterized by its distinctive tuberculate (wart-like) structures on the stems. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland shrublands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
