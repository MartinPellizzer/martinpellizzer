Medicago laciniata var. brachyacantha

Medicago laciniata var. brachyacantha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with narrow, serrated lobes and produces small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil fertility through nitrogen fixation. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage and ecological restoration projects.

Its adaptability makes it a valuable species in both agricultural and natural habitats.
