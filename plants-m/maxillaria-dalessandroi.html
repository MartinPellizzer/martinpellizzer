Maxillaria dalessandroi

Maxillaria dalessandroi, commonly known as the Amazonian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically blooms in the spring, with flowers that range from white to pale pink, often with a yellow or greenish throat. Its roots are fibrous, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Maxillaria dalessandroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
