Maxillaria rudolfii

Maxillaria rudolfii, commonly known as the Rudolf's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces fragrant, dark violet to purple flowers with yellow and green markings. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it prefers bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Maxillaria rudolfii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both greenhouse and garden settings.
