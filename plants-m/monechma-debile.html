Monechma debile

Monechma debile, commonly known as the slender monechma, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually less than 2 inches in length. Its flowers are pale green to yellow and are clustered in loose inflorescences, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

Monechma debile is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often overlooked due to its modest size and inconspicuous blooms.
