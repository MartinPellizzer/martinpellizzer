Microtatorchis aristata

Microtatorchis aristata, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-blue foliage. The plant forms a rosette of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. It produces yellow flowers in the summer, which are followed by small, spiny fruits.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
