Modecca cardiophylla

Modecca cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Modecca cardiophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and aesthetic appeal.
