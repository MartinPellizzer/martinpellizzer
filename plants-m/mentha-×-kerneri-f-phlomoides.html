Mentha × kerneri f. phlomoides

Mentha × kerneri f. phlomoides, commonly known as the woolly mint, is a hybrid plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and square, erect stems. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, pale blue or purple flowers in whorled clusters.

The foliage has a strong, aromatic scent due to the presence of menthol and other essential oils.
