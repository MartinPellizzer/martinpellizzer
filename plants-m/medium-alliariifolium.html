Medium alliariifolium

Medium alliariifolium, commonly known as spoonweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in clusters atop slender stems. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Due to its attractive foliage and ability to tolerate harsh conditions, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
