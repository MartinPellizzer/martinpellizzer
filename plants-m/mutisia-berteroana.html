Mutisia berteroana

Mutisia berteroana, commonly known as the Andean mutisia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Mutisia berteroana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
