Macaranga conglomerata

Macaranga conglomerata, commonly known as the clustered macaranga, is a tropical plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Macaranga conglomerata is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil.
