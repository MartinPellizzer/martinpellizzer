Masdevallia hortilankesteriani

Masdevallia hortilankesteriani, commonly known as the orchid of the horticultural variety, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive inflorescence that features a cluster of small, colorful flowers. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
