Microstylis cordifolia

Microstylis cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf microstylis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
