Miconia cretacea

Miconia cretacea, commonly known as the Caribbean miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry containing numerous seeds.

Miconia cretacea is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
