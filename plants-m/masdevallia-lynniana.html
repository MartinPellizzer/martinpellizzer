Masdevallia lynniana

Masdevallia lynniana, commonly known as the Lynn's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, long-lasting blooms that feature intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant typically produces a single, pendulous flower with a spurred lip, which is a defining feature of the Masdevallia genus. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure but requires careful attention to watering and humidity levels.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
