Mussaenda ornata

Mussaenda ornata, commonly known as the golden bell plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Mussaenda ornata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
