Micrampelis leptocarpa

Micrampelis leptocarpa, commonly known as the slender climbing fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its woody vines and small, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish flowers that are followed by edible fig-like fruits.
