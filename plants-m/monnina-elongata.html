Monnina elongata

Monnina elongata, commonly known as slender bluemink, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly known as buckwheats. The plant features slender, ascending stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its small, blue to violet flowers appear in clusters and bloom from late spring to early summer.
