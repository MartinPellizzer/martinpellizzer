Mitrephora macrantha

Mitrephora macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered mitrephora, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers.
