Marattia megaptera

Marattia megaptera, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a large tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, shield-shaped fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of feathery fronds that give it a tropical appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The fronds are used in traditional medicine and are also valued for their ornamental appeal.
