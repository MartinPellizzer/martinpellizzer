Myristica horsfieldii

Myristica horsfieldii, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers. The plant produces an aromatic fruit that contains the valuable spice nutmeg inside its hard, woody outer shell. Its essential oils are also used in perfumery and traditional medicine.

Myristica horsfieldii plays a significant role in both ecological and economic contexts in its native habitat.
