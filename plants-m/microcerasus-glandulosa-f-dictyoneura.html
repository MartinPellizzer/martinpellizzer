Microcerasus glandulosa f. dictyoneura

Microcerasus glandulosa f. dictyoneura, commonly known as the fragrant flowering shrub, is a botanical variant of the species Microcerasus glandulosa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant fragrance and attractive foliage.
