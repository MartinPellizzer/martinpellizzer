Mascarenhasia arborescens f. arborea

Mascarenhasia arborescens f. arborea, commonly known as the Mascarene tree fern, is a variant of the tree fern native to the Mascarene Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant exhibits a distinctive growth pattern with a central stem and a crown of compound leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in its native habitat.
