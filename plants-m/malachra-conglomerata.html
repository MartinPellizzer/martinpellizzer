Malachra conglomerata

Malachra conglomerata, commonly known as the clustered malachra, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Province of South Africa.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
