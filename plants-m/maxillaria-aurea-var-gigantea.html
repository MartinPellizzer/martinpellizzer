Maxillaria aurea var. gigantea

Maxillaria aurea var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant golden maxillaria, is a striking orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 5 inches in diameter, featuring vibrant yellow petals and a contrasting red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to retain moisture.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
