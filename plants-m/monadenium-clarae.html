Monadenium clarae

Monadenium clarae, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark purple disk florets. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Monadenium clarae is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate.
