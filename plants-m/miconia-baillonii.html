Miconia baillonii

Miconia baillonii, commonly known as the Bailloni miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that attract various pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous small, winged seeds.
