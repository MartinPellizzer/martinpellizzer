Macrostomium aloifolium

Macrostomium aloifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf macrostomium, is a unique succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
