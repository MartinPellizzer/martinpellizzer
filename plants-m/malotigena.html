Malotigena

Malotigena, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and often have a waxy texture. Malotigena species are popular as houseplants due to their ability to thrive in low-light conditions and their ornamental value.

They are also known for their resilience and ease of care, making them a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
