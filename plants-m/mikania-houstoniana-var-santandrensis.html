Mikania houstoniana var. santandrensis

Mikania houstoniana var. santandrensis, commonly known as the Santa Marta Mikania, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
