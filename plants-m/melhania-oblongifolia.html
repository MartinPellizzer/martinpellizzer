Melhania oblongifolia

Melhania oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf melhania, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, oblong leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warm season. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Melhania oblongifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
