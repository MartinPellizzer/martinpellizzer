Monotes doryphorus

Monotes doryphorus, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky outcrops. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil efficiently.

Monotes doryphorus is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
