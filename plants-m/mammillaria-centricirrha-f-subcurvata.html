Mammillaria centricirrha f. subcurvata

Mammillaria centricirrha f. subcurvata, commonly known as the curled spine mammillaria, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to blue-gray body with prominent, curved spines that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise spiny form. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem.

This variety is prized by collectors for its unique morphology and relatively easy care requirements.
