Mischocarpus largifolius

Mischocarpus largifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf spondias, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, sweet, and tangy fruits similar to cherries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and its fruit, which is used in local cuisines.
