Miconia macrosperma

Miconia macrosperma, commonly known as the giant miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by distinctive, woody, spiny fruits. Miconia macrosperma is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of tropical conditions.

It is also noted for its potential as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
