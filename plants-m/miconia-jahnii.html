Miconia jahnii

Miconia jahnii, commonly known as the Jahn's miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

Miconia jahnii is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and stream banks.
