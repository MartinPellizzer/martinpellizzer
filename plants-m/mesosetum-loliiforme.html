Mesosetum loliiforme

Mesosetum loliiforme, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and sandy soils. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like cluster of small, two-ranked spikelets.

Mesosetum loliiforme is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
