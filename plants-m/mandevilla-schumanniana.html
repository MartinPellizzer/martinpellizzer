Mandevilla schumanniana

Mandevilla schumanniana, commonly known as the flame vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for trellises and containers.

Mandevilla schumanniana is also known for its ability to produce fruit, which contains seeds used for propagation.
