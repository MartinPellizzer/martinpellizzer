Moraea trita

Moraea trita, commonly known as the pink moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tubular or funnel-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, Moraea trita is cultivated in many regions for its beauty and resilience.
