Manotes palisotii

Manotes palisotii, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features rosettes of velvety, waxy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom from the center of the leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Manotes palisotii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
