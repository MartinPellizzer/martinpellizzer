Maytenus jamaicensis var. orbicularis

Maytenus jamaicensis var. orbicularis, commonly known as the Jamaican mayten, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its orbicular, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange to red berries containing seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
