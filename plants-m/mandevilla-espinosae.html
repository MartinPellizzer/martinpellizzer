Mandevilla espinosae

Mandevilla espinosae, commonly known as the spiny mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features spiny stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a climbing plant or in containers.

Mandevilla espinosae is prized for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
