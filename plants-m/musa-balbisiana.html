Musa balbisiana

Musa balbisiana, commonly known as the Chinese banana or wild banana, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine and culinary practices. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.

However, it is also considered an invasive species in some areas due to its aggressive spread.
