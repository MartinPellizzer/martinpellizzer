Mammillaria centricirrha var. macracantha

Mammillaria centricirrha var. macracantha, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, rounded, ribbed stems that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, and its spines are typically white to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

This variety is popular among cactus enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
