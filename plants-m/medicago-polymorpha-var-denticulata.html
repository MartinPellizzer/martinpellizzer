Medicago polymorpha var. denticulata

Medicago polymorpha var. denticulata, commonly known as the serrated medick, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has hairy stems and small, rounded seed pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands, along roadsides, and in agricultural fields.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
