Masdevallia bella

Masdevallia bella, commonly known as the beautiful masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant has a compact growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
