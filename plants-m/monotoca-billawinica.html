Monotoca billawinica

Monotoca billawinica, commonly known as the slender rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to survive in dry, harsh environments. Monotoca billawinica is often found in open habitats such as spinifex plains and gravelly slopes.
