Morinda sparsiflora

Morinda sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered morinda, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias, and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves. Morinda sparsiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
