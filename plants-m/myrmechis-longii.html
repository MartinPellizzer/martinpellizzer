Myrmechis longii

Myrmechis longii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed myrtle, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and heathlands.
