Minuartia recurva

Minuartia recurva, commonly known as the curled rock-cress, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates. Its growth pattern allows it to survive in harsh environments with limited nutrients.

Minuartia recurva is often found in mountainous areas and is a common species in the tundra biome.
