Maranta kegeliana

Maranta kegeliana, commonly known as the Kegel's arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant in the Marantaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Maranta kegeliana is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
