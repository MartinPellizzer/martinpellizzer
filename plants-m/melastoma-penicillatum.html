Melastoma penicillatum

Melastoma penicillatum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a smooth texture, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Melastoma penicillatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
