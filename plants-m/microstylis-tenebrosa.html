Microstylis tenebrosa

Microstylis tenebrosa, commonly known as the dark-styled microstylis, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as sedges.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically features dark-colored inflorescences, which are often clustered in dense, spike-like structures. Microstylis tenebrosa thrives in moist, shady environments, such as woodland floors or wetlands.
