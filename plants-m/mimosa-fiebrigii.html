Mimosa fiebrigii

Mimosa fiebrigii, commonly known as the yellow mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators.
