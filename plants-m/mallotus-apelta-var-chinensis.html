Mallotus apelta var. chinensis

Mallotus apelta var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese holly or Chinese holly tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, dark green leaves that are often glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature and ability to tolerate urban conditions make it a versatile plant in landscaping.
