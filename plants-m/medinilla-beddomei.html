Medinilla beddomei

Medinilla beddomei, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet flower, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are edible and have a sweet, citrus-like flavor. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.

Medinilla beddomei is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
