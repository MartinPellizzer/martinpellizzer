Maranta luciani

Maranta luciani, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes various ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep red or purple, especially when exposed to bright light. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

While not widely cultivated for its roots, it is valued as a decorative plant for its unique foliage.
