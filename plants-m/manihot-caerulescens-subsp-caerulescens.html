Manihot caerulescens subsp. caerulescens

Manihot caerulescens subsp.

caerulescens, commonly known as the blue arrowleaf, is a shrub native to South America. It is characterized by its distinctive blue-green leaves with a velvety texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local communities.
