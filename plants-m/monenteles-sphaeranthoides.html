Monenteles sphaeranthoides

Monenteles sphaeranthoides, commonly known as the spherical starwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth form, with numerous small, fleshy leaves arranged in a tight cluster. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
