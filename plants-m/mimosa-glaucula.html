Mimosa glaucula

Mimosa glaucula, commonly known as the silver mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fern-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, blooming in the spring.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental gardening.
