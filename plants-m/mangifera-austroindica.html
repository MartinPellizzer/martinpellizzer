Mangifera austroindica

Mangifera austroindica, commonly known as the Indian mango, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring a broad, spreading canopy. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a tropical fruit with a thick, fibrous skin and a sweet, juicy pulp.

The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and ample sunlight.
