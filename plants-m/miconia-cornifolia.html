Miconia cornifolia

Miconia cornifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry containing numerous seeds.

Miconia cornifolia is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical forests.
