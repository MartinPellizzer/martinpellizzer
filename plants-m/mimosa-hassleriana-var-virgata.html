Mimosa hassleriana var. virgata

Mimosa hassleriana var. virgata, commonly known as the silver mimosas, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, feathery foliage that provides a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, yellow, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
