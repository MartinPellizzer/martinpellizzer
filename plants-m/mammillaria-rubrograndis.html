Mammillaria rubrograndis

Mammillaria rubrograndis, commonly known as the red grand mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and bright red spines that contrast with its green body. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
