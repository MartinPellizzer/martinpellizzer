Memecylon subtrinervium

Memecylon subtrinervium, commonly known as the three-veined memecylon, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant typically grows in humid, lowland forests and thrives in shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have three prominent veins, giving it its common name.

The flowers are small, clustered, and often pink or white, with showy bracts that attract pollinators.
