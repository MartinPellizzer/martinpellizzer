Melpomene melanosticta

Melpomene melanosticta, commonly known as the purple passionflower, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that display a complex arrangement of petals and sepals, often in shades of purple and white. The plant produces small, green, edible fruits that are rich in nutrients. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Melpomene melanosticta is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
