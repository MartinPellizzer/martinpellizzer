Momordica sativa

Momordica sativa, commonly known as bitter melon, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, warty, green fruit that turns yellow or orange when mature. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers. Bitter melon is cultivated for its edible fruit, which is used in various cuisines and traditional medicine.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
