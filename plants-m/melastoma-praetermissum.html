Melastoma praetermissum

Melastoma praetermissum, commonly known as the slender melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a glossy appearance.

Melastoma praetermissum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
