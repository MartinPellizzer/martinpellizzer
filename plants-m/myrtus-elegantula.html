Myrtus elegantula

Myrtus elegantula, commonly known as sweet myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green leaves that are often fragrant and have a slightly leathery texture. The plant produces tiny white flowers in spring, which are followed by small, dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Myrtus elegantula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
