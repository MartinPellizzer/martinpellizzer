Mouriri graveolens

Mouriri graveolens, commonly known as the stinking mouriri, is a species of flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, lowland areas. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, white flowers that emit a strong, unpleasant odor. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant's unique scent and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
