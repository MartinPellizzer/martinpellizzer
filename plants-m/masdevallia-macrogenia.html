Masdevallia macrogenia

Masdevallia macrogenia, commonly known as the giant masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Andean highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter, featuring vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in cool, humid environments with rich, well-drained soil. Its unique floral structure and vivid hues make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.

The species is noted for its slow growth and specific care requirements, making it a challenging but rewarding plant to cultivate.
