Mila kubei

Mila kubei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Mila kubei is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
