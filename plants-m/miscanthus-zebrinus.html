Miscanthus zebrinus

Miscanthus zebrinus, commonly known as the zebra grass, is a perennial ornamental grass native to East Asia.

It features tall, slender stalks with distinctive silver-white and green striped foliage that adds visual interest to gardens. The plant produces feathery plumes in late summer, enhancing its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Miscanthus zebrinus is often used in landscaping for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
