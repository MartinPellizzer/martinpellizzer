Mammillaria karwinskiana subsp. karwinskiana

Mammillaria karwinskiana subsp.

karwinskiana, commonly known as the Karwin's hedgehog cactus, is a compact, spherical cactus native to Mexico. It features dense clusters of white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has a ribbed stem covered in small, white to pale green tubercles. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
