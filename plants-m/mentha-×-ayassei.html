Mentha × ayassei

Mentha × ayassei, commonly known as wild mint, is a hybrid species within the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and flowering spikes that produce small, pale blue or purple flowers. The plant is perennial and thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in woodland areas. Its leaves are typically serrated and have a strong, refreshing menthol scent.

Mentha × ayassei is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies and as a culinary herb.
