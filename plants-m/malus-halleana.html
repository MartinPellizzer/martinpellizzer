Malus halleana

Malus halleana, commonly known as the Hallean apple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white flowers in early spring and vibrant red or orange fruits in autumn. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal color changes, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
