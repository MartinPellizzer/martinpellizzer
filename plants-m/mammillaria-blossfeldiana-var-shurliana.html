Mammillaria blossfeldiana var. shurliana

Mammillaria blossfeldiana var. shurliana, commonly known as the Shurliana mammillaria, is a compact cactus with a globular shape and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines.

It originates from the arid regions of Mexico, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or as a potted plant.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
