Machaerium langlassei

Machaerium langlassei, commonly known as the blue machaerium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and feathery foliage. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that are clustered in dense panicles, making it an ornamental choice in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, and is often used in native plant landscaping due to its ecological benefits.
