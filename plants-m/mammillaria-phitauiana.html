Mammillaria phitauiana

Mammillaria phitauiana, commonly known as the Cape Town mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
