Miscanthus sinensis f. pycnocephalus

Miscanthus sinensis f. pycnocephalus, commonly known as the dense-headed miscanthus, is a compact, ornamental grass that features narrow, silver-gray foliage.

It produces dense, fluffy panicles of pale pink to white flowers in late summer to early autumn. This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

The plant is relatively low maintenance and can be used as a border plant or in containers.
