Marsdenia malmeana

Marsdenia malmeana, commonly known as the Malme's Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive corona, and it produces follicles containing small, flat seeds.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.
