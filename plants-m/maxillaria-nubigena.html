Maxillaria nubigena

Maxillaria nubigena, commonly known as the Cloudy Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, often velvety texture. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, showcasing its ornamental value in both greenhouse and outdoor cultivation. Its leaves are usually dark green and slightly hairy, contributing to its unique appearance.

Maxillaria nubigena is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
