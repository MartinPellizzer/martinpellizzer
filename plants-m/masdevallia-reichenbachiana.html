Masdevallia reichenbachiana

Masdevallia reichenbachiana, commonly known as the Reichenbach's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and produces showy, long-lasting blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
