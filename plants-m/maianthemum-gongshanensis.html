Maianthemum gongshanensis

Maianthemum gongshanensis, commonly known as the Gongshan false lily, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its grass-like leaves and small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, thriving in cool and humid environments. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a short, leafy stem.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
