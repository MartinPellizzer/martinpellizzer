Mammillaria tesopacensis var. papasquiarensis

Mammillaria tesopacensis var. papasquiarensis, commonly known as the Papasquiarensis mammillaria, is a small cacti species native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and is covered in white to grayish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

This variety is appreciated for its compact size and unique appearance in succulent collections.
