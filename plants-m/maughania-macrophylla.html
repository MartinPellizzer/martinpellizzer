Maughania macrophylla

Maughania macrophylla, commonly known as the giant featherleaf, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It is characterized by its large, feathery leaves that can reach lengths of up to 30 inches, giving it a dramatic and ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens and landscapes. Its foliage remains lush throughout the year, providing visual interest in both winter and summer.

While it is not typically cultivated for its flowers, its unique leaf structure makes it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
