Mussaenda racemosa

Mussaenda racemosa, commonly known as the clustered Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers, which are borne in racemes, giving it its scientific name. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate with a smooth surface.

Mussaenda racemosa is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
