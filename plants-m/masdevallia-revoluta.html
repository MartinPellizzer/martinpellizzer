Masdevallia revoluta

Masdevallia revoluta, commonly known as the curled-leaf masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that are typically borne on arching or pendulous stems. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique and elegant blooms.
