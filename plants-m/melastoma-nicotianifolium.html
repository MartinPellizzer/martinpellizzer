Melastoma nicotianifolium

Melastoma nicotianifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruits are capsules that split open to release small, winged seeds.

Melastoma nicotianifolium is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
