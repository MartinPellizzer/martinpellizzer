Miltonia candida var. purpureoviolacea

Miltonia candida var. purpureoviolacea, commonly known as the purple viola orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, violet-pink flowers with a white throat. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, producing showy, spurred flowers that attract pollinators. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistent humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in a tropical environment.
