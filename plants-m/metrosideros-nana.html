Metrosideros nana

Metrosideros nana, commonly known as the dwarf pohutukawa or miniature New Zealand Christmas tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a waxy texture, while the bark is smooth and reddish-brown. It thrives in coastal areas and is well adapted to saline and wind-prone environments.

This species is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
