Mulgedium roseum

Mulgedium roseum, commonly known as the crimson flame orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and red petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Mulgedium roseum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
