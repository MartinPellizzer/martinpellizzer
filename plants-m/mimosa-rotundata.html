Mimosa rotundata

Mimosa rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It features small, fern-like leaves that are typically rounded or oval in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or forested areas.

Mimosa rotundata is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in partial shade.
