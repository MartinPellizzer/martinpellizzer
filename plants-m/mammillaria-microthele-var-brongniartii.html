Mammillaria microthele var. brongniartii

Mammillaria microthele var. brongniartii, commonly known as the Brongniart's pincushion cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem covered in small, white spines and dense, woolly areolas. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
