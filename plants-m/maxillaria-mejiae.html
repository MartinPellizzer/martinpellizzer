Maxillaria mejiae

Maxillaria mejiae, commonly known as the Mejia's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a compact rosette pattern.

Maxillaria mejiae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
