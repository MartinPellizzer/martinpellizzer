Mattia aucheri

Mattia aucheri, commonly known as the snowdrop, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. Mattia aucheri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its early blooming habit.
