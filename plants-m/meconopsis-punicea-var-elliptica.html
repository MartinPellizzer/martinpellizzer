Meconopsis punicea var. elliptica

Meconopsis punicea var. elliptica, commonly known as the purple meconopsis, is a herbaceous perennial native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens with specific care to mimic its natural habitat.
