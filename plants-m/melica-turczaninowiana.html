Melica turczaninowiana

Melica turczaninowiana, commonly known as Turczaninow's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and fine, linear leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Melica turczaninowiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
