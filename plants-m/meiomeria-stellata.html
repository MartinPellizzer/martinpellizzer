Meiomeria stellata

Meiomeria stellata, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy stems and leaves. Its botanical name reflects its distinctive floral structure, which resembles a star.

Meiomeria stellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
