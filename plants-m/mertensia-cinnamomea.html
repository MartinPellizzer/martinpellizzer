Mertensia cinnamomea

Mertensia cinnamomea, commonly known as cinnamon leaf bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
