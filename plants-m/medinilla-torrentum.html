Medinilla torrentum

Medinilla torrentum, commonly known as the torrent medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its clustered, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near streams or in dense forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Medinilla torrentum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
