Musa balbisiana var. dechangensis

Musa balbisiana var. dechangensis, commonly known as the Dechang banana, is a variant of the banana plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and produces clusters of fruit that are typically smaller than those of common bananas. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring warm temperatures and high humidity. It is often cultivated for its edible fruit, which has a distinct flavor and texture compared to other banana varieties.

The botanical features of this plant make it an important species in both agricultural and horticultural contexts.
