Machilus nanshoensis

Machilus nanshoensis, commonly known as the Nansho Chinese-laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the provinces of Sichuan and Yunnan, where it grows in subtropical forests. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, dark berries.

Machilus nanshoensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
