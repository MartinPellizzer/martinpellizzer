Mammillaria microcarpa f. gueldemanniana

Mammillaria microcarpa f. gueldemanniana, commonly known as the Gueldemann's miniature mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem with prominent, white radial spines and a dense covering of small, white tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
