Megalastrum indusiatum

Megalastrum indusiatum, commonly known as the Indian star anise, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, star-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in dense forests. Its fruits are used in traditional medicine and as a spice due to their strong flavor and essential oils.
