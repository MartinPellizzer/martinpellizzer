Mecranium birimosum

Mecranium birimosum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its native habitat. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Mecranium birimosum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
