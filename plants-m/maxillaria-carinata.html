Maxillaria carinata

Maxillaria carinata, commonly known as the ridged maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, ridged pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a contrasting lip. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows, contributing to its compact appearance.

Maxillaria carinata thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
