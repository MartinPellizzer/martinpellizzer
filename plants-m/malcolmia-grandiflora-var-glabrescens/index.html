Malcolmia Grandiflora Var. Glabrescens

Malcolmia grandiflora var. glabrescens is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant yellow blooms and delicate foliage. Native to the Mediterranean region, this variety thrives in warm, sunny climates with well-drained soil. Its compact growth habit makes it an excellent choice for rock gardens and container planting. The plant's unique appearance and easy care requirements have made it a popular addition to many gardens. With its cheerful flowers and resilient nature, it adds a touch of elegance to any landscape.
