Millettia dielsiana var. heterocarpa

Millettia dielsiana var. heterocarpa, commonly known as the giant sycamore or Chinese sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves. The tree produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
