Mammillaria inae

Mammillaria inae, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and dense covering of white to yellow spines. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in arid environments. It produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer.

Mammillaria inae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
