Milium compactum

Milium compactum, commonly known as the clustered millet, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, grass-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, white or pale green flowers in spikes, which appear in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Milium compactum is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
