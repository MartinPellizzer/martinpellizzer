Mikania scandens var. umbellifera

Mikania scandens var. umbellifera, commonly known as the giant fleabane, is a vigorous climbing vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that resemble dandelion blooms. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in open woodlands.

Due to its invasive nature, it can outcompete native vegetation in certain regions.
