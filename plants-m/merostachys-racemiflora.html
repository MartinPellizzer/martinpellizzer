Merostachys racemiflora

Merostachys racemiflora, commonly known as the raceme grass, is a member of the Poaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and ecological landscaping. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine, though its primary botanical interest lies in its structure and growth patterns.
