Masdevallia lotax

Masdevallia lotax, commonly known as the Snowy Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, snow-white flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in cool, high-altitude environments, often in shaded, humid forest understories. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve moisture in its native habitat.

Masdevallia lotax is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
