Mammillaria microhelia var. microheliopsis

Mammillaria microhelia var. microheliopsis, commonly known as the miniature sunflower cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, rounded stem covered in dense, white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
