Mammillaria floribunda

Mammillaria floribunda, commonly known as the clustered moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor succulent collections.
