Montbretia pottsii

Montbretia pottsii, commonly known as the Orange Sword, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features long, sword-like leaves that are dark green and often have a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is widely appreciated by gardeners and botanists alike.
