Mammillaria discolor var. prolifera

Mammillaria discolor var. prolifera, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem with white to pinkish tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces pale pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and container cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its slow growth rate and unique coloration make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
