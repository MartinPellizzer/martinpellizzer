Mikania manettiae

Mikania manettiae, commonly known as the "Mexican morning glory," is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

Mikania manettiae is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and forest edges, where it can thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
