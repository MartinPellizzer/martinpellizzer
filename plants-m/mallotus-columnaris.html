Mallotus columnaris

Mallotus columnaris, commonly known as the columnar hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming from summer to autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Mallotus columnaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
