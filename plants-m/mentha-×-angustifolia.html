Mentha × angustifolia

Mentha × angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf mint, is a hybrid species of the mint genus Mentha.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and strong, aromatic scent. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

Mentha × angustifolia is widely used in herbal medicine and as a flavoring agent in culinary applications.
