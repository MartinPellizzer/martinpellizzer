Maxillaria gentryi

Maxillaria gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and pseudobulbs that support its growth. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in hue, with a yellow lip that is highly ornamental.

Maxillaria gentryi is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
