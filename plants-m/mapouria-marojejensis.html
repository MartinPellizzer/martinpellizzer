Mapouria marojejensis

Mapouria marojejensis, commonly known as the Eastern African milk tree, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the tropical regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local communities.
