Macrotyloma densiflorum var. densiflorum

Macrotyloma densiflorum var. densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower cluster bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain seeds used for both culinary and ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm climate with adequate sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological and agricultural applications.
