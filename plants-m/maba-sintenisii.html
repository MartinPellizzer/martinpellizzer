Maba sintenisii

Maba sintenisii, commonly known as the Java maba, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
