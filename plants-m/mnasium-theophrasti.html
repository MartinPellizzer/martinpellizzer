Mnasium theophrasti

Mnasium theophrasti, commonly known as wall rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found growing in rocky or disturbed soils. It produces small, oval-shaped pods that contain several seeds.

Mnasium theophrasti is known for its peppery flavor and has been used in traditional culinary practices.
