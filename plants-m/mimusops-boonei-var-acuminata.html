Mimusops boonei var. acuminata

Mimusops boonei var. acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata varietal of the African plum tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, lanceolate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by fleshy, orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
