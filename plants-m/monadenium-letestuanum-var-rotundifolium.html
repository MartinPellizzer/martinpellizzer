Monadenium letestuanum var. rotundifolium

Monadenium letestuanum var. rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaf monadenium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing form and produces small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are rounded and fleshy, adapted to arid environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.

This variety is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
