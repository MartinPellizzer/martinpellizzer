Mimosa barbadetiman

Mimosa barbadetiman, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and the Caribbean.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are soft to the touch and turn a striking red or pink color when damaged. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is often considered a weed in certain areas.
