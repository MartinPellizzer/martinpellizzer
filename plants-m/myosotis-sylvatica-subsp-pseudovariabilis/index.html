Myosotis Sylvatica Subsp. Pseudovariabilis

Myosotis sylvatica subsp. pseudovariabilis is a lesser-known variant of the common forget-me-not plant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas across Europe. This subspecies exhibits subtle differences in flower color and leaf shape compared to its more common relative. Its delicate blue-violet blooms make it a sought-after addition to gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
