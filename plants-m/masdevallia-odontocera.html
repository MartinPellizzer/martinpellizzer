Masdevallia odontocera

Masdevallia odontocera, commonly known as the toothed lip masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive toothed lip that is often a deep purple or red. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
