Melochia Monoica

Melochia monoica is a unique flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It is known for its striking, palmately lobed leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along riverbanks. Its seeds are dispersed by animals, contributing to its spread across various habitats. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
