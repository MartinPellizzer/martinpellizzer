Mammillaria geminispina var. brevispina

Mammillaria geminispina var. brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem covered in short, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique spination and compact growth make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
