Malaxis cogniauxiana

Malaxis cogniauxiana, commonly known as the Madagascar ghost orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, translucent greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture for nourishment. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native region.
