Miconia glaberrima

Miconia glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is sometimes considered a weed in certain regions.
