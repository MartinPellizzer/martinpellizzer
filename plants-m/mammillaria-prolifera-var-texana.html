Mammillaria prolifera var. texana

Mammillaria prolifera var. texana, commonly known as the Texas mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to the arid regions of Texas.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color, covered with white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
