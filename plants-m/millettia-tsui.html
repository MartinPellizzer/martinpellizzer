Millettia tsui

Millettia tsui, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a climbing legume native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its long, flexible stems and clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with oval-shaped leaflets that provide a lush, green appearance.

Millettia tsui is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its medicinal properties.
