Melochia melissifolia var. melissifolia

Melochia melissifolia var. melissifolia, commonly known as the leafy melochia, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa and Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive greenish-white underside, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its foliage is also valued in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
