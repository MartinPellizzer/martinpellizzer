Mandevilla similaris

Mandevilla similaris, commonly known as the flame vine, is a vigorous evergreen climbing plant native to South America.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla similaris is often used in ornamental gardening for its ornate foliage and continuous flowering.
