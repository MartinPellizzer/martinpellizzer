Masdevallia bilabiata

Masdevallia bilabiata, commonly known as the Bilabiate Masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spurred petals and sepals that often display a combination of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant typically grows as a miniature to medium-sized orchid with a short, erect stem and few to several flowers. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Masdevallia bilabiata thrives in cool, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
