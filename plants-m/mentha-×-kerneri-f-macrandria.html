Mentha × kerneri f. macrandria

Mentha × kerneri f. macrandria, commonly known as the large-leaved mint, is a hybrid plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often grayish-green in color. The plant produces showy, purple flowers that are clustered in dense spikes. It is native to regions of East Asia, including China and Japan.

This variety is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
