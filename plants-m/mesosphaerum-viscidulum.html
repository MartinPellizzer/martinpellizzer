Mesosphaerum viscidulum

Mesosphaerum viscidulum, commonly known as the sticky mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
