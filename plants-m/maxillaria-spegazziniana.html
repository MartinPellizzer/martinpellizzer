Maxillaria spegazziniana

Maxillaria spegazziniana, commonly known as the Spegazzini's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid regions of South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a single, fleshy, dark green leaf. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers with yellowish-white petals and a distinctive violet or purple lip. Its flowers are typically 3-5 cm in diameter and bloom during the late winter to early spring.

Maxillaria spegazziniana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
