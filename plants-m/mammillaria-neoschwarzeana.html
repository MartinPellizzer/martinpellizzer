Mammillaria neoschwarzeana

Mammillaria neoschwarzeana, commonly known as the Schwarze's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and compact size make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
