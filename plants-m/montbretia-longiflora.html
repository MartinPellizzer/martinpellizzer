Montbretia longiflora

Montbretia longiflora, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright orange or yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered in a waxy coating. Its stems are upright and can grow up to 1 meter in height, supporting the showy inflorescences.

Montbretia longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
