Monardella linoides subsp. stricta

Monardella linoides subsp.

stricta, commonly known as narrowleaf monardella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
