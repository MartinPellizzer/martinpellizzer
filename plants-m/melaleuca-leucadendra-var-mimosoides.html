Melaleuca leucadendra var. mimosoides

Melaleuca leucadendra var. mimosoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is part of the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

The species is noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
