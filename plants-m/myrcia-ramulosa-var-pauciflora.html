Myrcia ramulosa var. pauciflora

Myrcia ramulosa var. pauciflora, commonly known as the scarlet myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical and subtropical gardens.
