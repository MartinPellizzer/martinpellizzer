Maytenus robustoides

Maytenus robustoides, commonly known as the robust tree or Maytenus tree, is a species native to South America, particularly found in Brazil, Paraguay, and Argentina.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a dense, rounded crown and produces small, fragrant flowers that are white or pale yellow. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed, making it an important food source for various wildlife species.
