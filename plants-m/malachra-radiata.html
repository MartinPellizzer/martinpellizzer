Malachra radiata

Malachra radiata, commonly known as the radiate malachra, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom.
