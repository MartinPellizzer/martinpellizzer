Melodinus aeneus

Melodinus aeneus, commonly known as the silver trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a climbing vine with tendrils that allow it to ascend trees and other structures. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Melodinus aeneus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
