Maxillaria candida

Maxillaria candida, commonly known as the white maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically has fleshy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria candida is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
