Mammillaria schiedeana subsp. giselae

Mammillaria schiedeana subsp.

giselae, commonly known as the Giselae Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, spiny cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular to cylindrical stem with prominent, white to pale yellow tubercles.

The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and arid environments due to its drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
