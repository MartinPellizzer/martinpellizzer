Matthiola lunata f. clausonis

Matthiola lunata f. clausonis, commonly known as the white-flowered wallflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its small, clustered flowers.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with four-petaled flowers. The plant typically features gray-green, hairy leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale yellow blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
