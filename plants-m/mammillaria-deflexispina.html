Mammillaria deflexispina

Mammillaria deflexispina, commonly known as the hooked spine cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and features a ribbed stem that becomes more pronounced with age. Its flowers are usually yellow or pale green, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
