Melicho

Melicho, commonly known as the "black-eyed Susan," is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the genus Rudbeckia and is characterized by its bright yellow petals and dark brown centers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and thrives in full sun. Melicho is often found in prairies, wetlands, and along stream banks.

Its showy flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
