Malapoenna cylicodaphne

Malapoenna cylicodaphne, commonly known as the cyclocarp, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a distinctive, spherical capsule that contains several seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
