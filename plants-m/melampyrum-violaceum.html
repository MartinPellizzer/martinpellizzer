Melampyrum violaceum, commonly known as purple touch-me-not, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Melampyrum violaceum is native to Europe and is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
