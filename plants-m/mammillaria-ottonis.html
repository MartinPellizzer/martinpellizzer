Mammillaria ottonis

Mammillaria ottonis, commonly known as the Otto's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
