Medicago orientalis var. austroeuropaea

Medicago orientalis var. austroeuropaea, commonly known as the European goat's rue, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive heart-shaped base and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces slender, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and wastelands.

Its medicinal properties have been historically utilized in traditional herbal remedies.
