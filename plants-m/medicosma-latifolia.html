Medicosma latifolia

Medicosma latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf medicinal plant, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features woody stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its botanical significance lies in its potential medicinal properties and ecological role in its native habitat.
