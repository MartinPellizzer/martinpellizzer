Malaxis Macrostachya

Malaxis macrostachya, also known as the large-stemmed orchid, is a rare and elegant plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This terrestrial orchid is characterized by its slender stems and delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and is often found in moist, forested areas. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
