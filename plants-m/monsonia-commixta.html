Monsonia commixta

Monsonia commixta, commonly known as the mixed monsonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features erect stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or purple in color.

Monsonia commixta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
