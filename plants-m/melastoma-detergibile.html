Melastoma detergibile

Melastoma detergibile, commonly known as the soapberry melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, often pink or red bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
