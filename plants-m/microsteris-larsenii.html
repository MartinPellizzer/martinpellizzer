Microsteris larsenii

Microsteris larsenii, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage. Microsteris larsenii is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is adapted to arid environments with minimal water availability.
