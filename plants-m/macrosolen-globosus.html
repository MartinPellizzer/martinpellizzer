Macrosolen globosus

Macrosolen globosus, commonly known as the globe senna, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the dry season. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
