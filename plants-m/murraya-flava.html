Murraya flava

Murraya flava, commonly known as the yellow murraya, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its fruits are berry-like, turning from green to red as they mature.

Murraya flava is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
