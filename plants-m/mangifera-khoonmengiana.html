Mangifera khoonmengiana

Mangifera khoonmengiana, commonly known as the Khoonmengian mango, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated fruits with a thick, fibrous pulp and a sweet, aromatic flavor. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a glossy green surface.

The flowers are small, fragrant, and clustered in panicles, contributing to its ornamental value as well as its fruit production.
