Myosotis alpestris var. corsicana

Myosotis alpestris var. corsicana, commonly known as the Corsican Forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Corsica.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
