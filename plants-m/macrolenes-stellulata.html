Macrolenes stellulata

Macrolenes stellulata, commonly known as the star-shaped leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are arranged in a star-like pattern, with each leaflet radiating outward from a central point. The plant typically features small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
