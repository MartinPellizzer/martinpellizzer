Myosurus minimus f. africanus

Myosurus minimus f. africanus, commonly known as the African mossy orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, moss-like appearance, with slender stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences.

This orchid is known for its unique morphology and is often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
