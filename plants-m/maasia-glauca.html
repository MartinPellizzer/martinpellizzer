Maasia glauca

Maasia glauca, commonly known as the blue-green moss, is a member of the family Pottiaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

Maasia glauca is well adapted to humid climates and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
