Mammillaria schaeferi var. longispina

Mammillaria schaeferi var. longispina, commonly known as the long-spined mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a compact, globular stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. Its spines are long and slender, often giving it a delicate appearance.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
