Mimophytum cardiophyllum

Mimophytum cardiophyllum, commonly known as the heartleaf burr, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant is typically found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a short lifespan, often completing its growth cycle within a single growing season.

The seeds are encased in a spiny capsule, which aids in dispersal by attaching to animal fur or clothing.
