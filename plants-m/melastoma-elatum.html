Melastoma elatum

Melastoma elatum, commonly known as the broadleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are often pink or red in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
