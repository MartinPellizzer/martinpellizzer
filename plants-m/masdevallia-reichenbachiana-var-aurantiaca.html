Masdevallia reichenbachiana var. aurantiaca

Masdevallia reichenbachiana var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange-tinged Masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, orange-red flowers with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, dark green leaves that form a rosette shape.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in specialized gardens.
