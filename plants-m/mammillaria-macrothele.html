Mammillaria macrothele

Mammillaria macrothele, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its thick, ribbed stems are covered in small, white spines.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
