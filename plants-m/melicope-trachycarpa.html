Melicope trachycarpa

Melicope trachycarpa, commonly known as the rough-leaved melicope, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

Melicope trachycarpa is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitats.
