Monadenium rhizophorum var. stoloniferum

Monadenium rhizophorum var. stoloniferum, commonly known as the stoloniferous root grass, is a perennial plant characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit.

It typically forms dense mats through the production of horizontal stolons, which root at the nodes and establish new plants. The plant has small, grass-like leaves that are usually narrow and tapering. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of ecological systems where it contributes to soil stabilization and habitat creation.
