Microstylis blumei

Microstylis blumei, commonly known as the slender-styled grass, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India and Southeast Asia. Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
