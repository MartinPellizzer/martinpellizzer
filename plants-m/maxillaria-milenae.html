Maxillaria milenae

Maxillaria milenae, commonly known as the Milena's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers, which are usually pink or purple, bloom in clusters and are known for their intricate, intricate structure.

Maxillaria milenae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
