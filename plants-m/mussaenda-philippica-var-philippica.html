Mussaenda philippica var. philippica

Mussaenda philippica var. philippica, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a glossy texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.
