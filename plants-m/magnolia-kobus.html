Magnolia kobus

Magnolia kobus, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall with a broad, spreading crown. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a pointed tip.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
