Mapouria depauperata

Mapouria depauperata, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, velvety flowers. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent central vein. It produces small, tubular flowers that are often purple or white, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Mapouria depauperata is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its preference for bright, indirect light and consistent moisture.
