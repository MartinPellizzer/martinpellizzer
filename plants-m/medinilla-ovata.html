Medinilla ovata

Medinilla ovata, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet tangerine shrub, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Medinilla ovata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and pleasant fragrance.
