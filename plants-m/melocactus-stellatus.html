Melocactus stellatus

Melocactus stellatus, commonly known as the star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and distinctive star-shaped spines. The plant produces white flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for desert climates.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
