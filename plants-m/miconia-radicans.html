Miconia radicans

Miconia radicans, commonly known as the creeping miconia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is native to Central and South America and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Miconia radicans is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
