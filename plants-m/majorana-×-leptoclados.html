Majorana × leptoclados

Majorana × leptoclados, commonly known as the hybrid marjoram, is a cross between Majorana hortensis and Majorana syriaca.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, oval leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically pale blue to purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in late summer.

It is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
