Maxillaria angustissima

Maxillaria angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect pseudobulbs and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria angustissima is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
