Mentha longifolia var. mollis

Mentha longifolia var. mollis, commonly known as woolly mint, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and aromatic, slightly minty scent. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly wrinkled texture.

Woolly mint is often used in culinary applications and traditional medicine for its refreshing and cooling properties.
