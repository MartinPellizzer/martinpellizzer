Maerua guerichii

Maerua guerichii, commonly known as the yellow thorn tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny branches and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Maerua guerichii plays a role in local ecosystems and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
