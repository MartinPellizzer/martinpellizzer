Muhlenbergia diffusa

Muhlenbergia diffusa, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery plumes that add ornamental value to gardens. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is drought-tolerant and often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.

Muhlenbergia diffusa is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
