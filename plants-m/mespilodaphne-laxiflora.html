Mespilodaphne laxiflora

Mespilodaphne laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered mespilodaphne, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and clustered in inflorescences, often with yellowish or greenish petals.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a reddish color, attracting various wildlife species.
