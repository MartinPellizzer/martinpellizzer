Mesembryanthemum turbinatum

Mesembryanthemum turbinatum, commonly known as the Turban Flower, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

Mesembryanthemum turbinatum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
