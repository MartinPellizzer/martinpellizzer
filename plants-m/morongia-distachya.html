Morongia distachya

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in dense inflorescences, often blooming during the rainy season.

Morongia distachya is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands, where it thrives in full sun.
