Melocactus melocactoides f. exsanguis

Melocactus melocactoides f. exsanguis, commonly known as the blood melocactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features a distinctive red or pinkish coloration, which is most prominent during the flowering season. Its flowers, which are typically yellow or orange, bloom in late spring and early summer.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in arid environments due to its low water requirements.
