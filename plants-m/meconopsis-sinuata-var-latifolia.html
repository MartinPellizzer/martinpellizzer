Meconopsis sinuata var. latifolia

Meconopsis sinuata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf meconopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens with specific care to mimic its natural growing conditions.
