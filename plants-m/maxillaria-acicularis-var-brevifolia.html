Maxillaria acicularis var. brevifolia

Maxillaria acicularis var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved needle orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a yellow or green lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or warm climates.
