Memecylon acuminatum var. flavescens

Memecylon acuminatum var. flavescens, commonly known as the golden memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves that are often reddish or yellowish beneath. Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture, though it is not widely cultivated outside its native range.
