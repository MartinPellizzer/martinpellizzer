Mendoncia caquetensis

Mendoncia caquetensis, commonly known as the Caquetá lily, is a flowering plant native to the Colombian region of Caquetá.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and showy inflorescences that bloom during the wet season.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
