Medicago × blancheana var. inermis

Medicago × blancheana var. inermis, commonly known as the white medic, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its pale yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are typical of the Fabaceae family. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It has been cultivated for its forage value and ornamental appeal.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
