Mentzelia holmgreniorum

Mentzelia holmgreniorum, commonly known as the Holmgren's blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tall, branching stems and showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Mentzelia holmgreniorum plays an important role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
