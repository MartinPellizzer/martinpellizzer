Myristica buchneriana

Myristica buchneriana, commonly known as the Borneo nutmeg, is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree, Myristica fragrans. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a red, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed, which is used for its aromatic properties.

The plant is valued for its unique fragrance and is often found in humid, shaded environments within its natural habitat.
