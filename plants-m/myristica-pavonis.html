Myristica pavonis

Myristica pavonis, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Banda Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the cinnamon and mace plants. The tree produces a fruit that contains the aromatic seeds used to make nutmeg and mace. Its bark and leaves also contain essential oils with medicinal properties.

The plant is valued for its economic and culinary significance in global trade.
