Marizia anthemoides

Marizia anthemoides, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the western United States.

The plant has succulent leaves that help it retain water in dry environments, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs.
