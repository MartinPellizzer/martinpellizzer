Matisia inaequalis

Matisia inaequalis, commonly known as the roughleaf matisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its rough, textured leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
