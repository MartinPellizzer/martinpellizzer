Mabea frutescens

Mabea frutescens, commonly known as the dwarf mabea or purple mabea, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of less than 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, showy purple or pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Mabea frutescens is often found in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in wetlands.
