Magnolia weerakitiana

Magnolia weerakitiana, commonly known as the Thai magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Thailand.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
