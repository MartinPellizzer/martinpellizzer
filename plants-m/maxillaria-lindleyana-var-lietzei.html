Maxillaria lindleyana var. lietzei

Maxillaria lindleyana var. lietzei, commonly known as the Lietze's Maxillaria, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of flowers that bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
