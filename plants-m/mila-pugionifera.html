Mila pugionifera

Mila pugionifera, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Mila pugionifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to add a lush, green texture to garden landscapes.
