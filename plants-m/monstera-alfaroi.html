Monstera alfaroi

Monstera alfaroi, commonly known as the Mexican giant shield fern, is a tropical plant native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed leaves that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.

Monstera alfaroi thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
