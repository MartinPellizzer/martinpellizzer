Miscanthus paniculatus

Miscanthus paniculatus, commonly known as the silvergrass or Chinese silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest throughout the growing season.

Miscanthus paniculatus is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for wildlife and its use in sustainable landscaping.
