Melilotoides tianschanica

Melilotoides tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan melilot, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant's flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees, playing a role in local ecosystems.
