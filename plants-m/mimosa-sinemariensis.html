Mimosa sinemariensis

Mimosa sinemariensis, commonly known as the velvetleaf mimosa, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences.

Mimosa sinemariensis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
