Maxillaria leptopus

Maxillaria leptopus, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features elongated, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its flowers are usually clustered in racemes and bloom during the spring and summer seasons.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
