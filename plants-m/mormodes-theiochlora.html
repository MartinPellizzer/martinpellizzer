Mormodes theiochlora

Mormodes theiochlora, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, strap-like leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warm seasons. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
