Melastoma denticulatum

Melastoma denticulatum, commonly known as the serrated melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in clusters. It produces small, fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
