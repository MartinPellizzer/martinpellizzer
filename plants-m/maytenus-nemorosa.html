Maytenus nemorosa

Maytenus nemorosa, commonly known as the silverleaf mayten, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Maytenus nemorosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
