Majana scutellarioides var. atropurpureus

Majana scutellarioides var. atropurpureus, commonly known as purple majana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to violet flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

The flowers bloom in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
