Melocactus rufispinus f. crassispinus

Melocactus rufispinus f. crassispinus, commonly known as the thick-spined melocactus, is a columnar cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem that is typically covered in dense, reddish-brown spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
