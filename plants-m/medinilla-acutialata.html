Medinilla acutialata

Medinilla acutialata, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This tropical plant is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and pleasant aroma.

Its leaves are glossy and dark green, adding to its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor settings.
