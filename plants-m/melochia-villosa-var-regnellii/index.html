Melochia Villosa Var. Regnellii

Melochia villosa var. regnellii is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with ample sunlight. This variant of Melochia is distinguished by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of bright yellow blooms. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions to flourish.
