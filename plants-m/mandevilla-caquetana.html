Mandevilla caquetana

Mandevilla caquetana, commonly known as the Colombian mandevilla, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla caquetana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
