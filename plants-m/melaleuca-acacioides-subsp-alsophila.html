Melaleuca acacioides subsp. alsophila

Melaleuca acacioides subsp.

alsophila, commonly known as the narrow-leaf paperbark, is a shrub or small tree native to northern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and fragrant, cream-colored flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small seeds, typical of the Myrtaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular plant for horticultural use.
