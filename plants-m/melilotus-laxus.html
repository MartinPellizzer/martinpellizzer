Melilotus laxus

Melilotus laxus, commonly known as slender melilot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Melilotus laxus is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
