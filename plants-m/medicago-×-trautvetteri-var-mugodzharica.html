Medicago × trautvetteri var. mugodzharica

Medicago × trautvetteri var. mugodzharica, commonly known as the Mugodzhar alfalfa, is a perennial legume native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to dry and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage crop. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
