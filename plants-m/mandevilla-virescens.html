Mandevilla virescens

Mandevilla virescens, commonly known as the yellow mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous evergreen vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens or as a houseplant.

Mandevilla virescens is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail, making it suitable for trellises, arbors, or hanging baskets.
