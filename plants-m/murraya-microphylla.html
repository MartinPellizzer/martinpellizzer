Murraya microphylla

Murraya microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved murraya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its fragrant yellow flowers are followed by orange-red berries that are edible but not particularly palatable.

Murraya microphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
