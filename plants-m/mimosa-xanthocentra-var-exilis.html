Mimosa xanthocentra var. exilis

Mimosa xanthocentra var. exilis, commonly known as the slender yellow mimosa, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, folding shut when disturbed. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its graceful foliage and subtle floral display.
