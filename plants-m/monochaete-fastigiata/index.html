Monochaete Fastigiata

Monochaete fastigiata is a striking plant known for its unique spiral arrangement of leaves. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to South America. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant green foliage. Its growth pattern resembles a helix, making it a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.
