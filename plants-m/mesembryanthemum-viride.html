Mesembryanthemum viride

Mesembryanthemum viride, commonly known as green ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
