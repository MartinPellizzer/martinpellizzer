Merremia digitata

Merremia digitata, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail along surfaces. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Merremia digitata is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability.
