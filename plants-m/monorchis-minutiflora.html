Monorchis minutiflora

Monorchis minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, tiny flowers that are typically less than 1 centimeter in diameter. The plant has a slender, erect stem and produces few leaves, which are usually narrow and strap-like. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish in color and are arranged in a loose inflorescence.

Monorchis minutiflora is primarily grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique and intricate floral structure.
