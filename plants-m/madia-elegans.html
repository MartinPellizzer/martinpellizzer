Madia elegans

Madia elegans, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in prairies and disturbed areas.

Madia elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
