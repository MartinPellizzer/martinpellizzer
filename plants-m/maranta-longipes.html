Maranta longipes

Maranta longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Maranta longipes is cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
