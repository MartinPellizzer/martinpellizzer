Mikania scandens

Mikania scandens, commonly known as mile-a-minute weed, is a fast-growing vine native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its aggressive growth habit, often spreading rapidly and covering large areas. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in moist, sunny environments.

Due to its invasive nature, it is considered a noxious weed in many regions.
