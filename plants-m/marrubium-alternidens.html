Marrubium alternidens

Marrubium alternidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved horehound, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with hairy, gray-green leaves that are alternate in arrangement. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Marrubium alternidens is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
