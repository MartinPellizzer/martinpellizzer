Mapania petiolata var. pumila

Mapania petiolata var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf mapania, is a compact, low-growing plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique subject for ecological and horticultural study.
