Melocactus mazelianus subsp. schatzlii

Melocactus mazelianus subsp.

schatzlii, commonly known as the Schatzlii melocactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico. It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive red or orange spines and a pale green body.

The plant features a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently in its arid environment. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and appear at the base of the plant.

This species is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water once established.
