Mammillaria rhodantha subsp. mollendorffiana

Mammillaria rhodantha subsp.

mollendorffiana, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in white to pale pink tubercles.

The plant produces small, pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
