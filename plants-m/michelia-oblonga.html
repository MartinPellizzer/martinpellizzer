Michelia oblonga

Michelia oblonga, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or fragrant Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in temperate climates.

Michelia oblonga is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
