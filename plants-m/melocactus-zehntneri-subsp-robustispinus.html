Melocactus zehntneri subsp. robustispinus

Melocactus zehntneri subsp.

robustispinus, commonly known as the robust spiny melocactus, is a columnar cactus native to the arid regions of northern South America. It features a tall, cylindrical stem that is typically ribbed and covered with dense, sharp spines.

The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the rainy season, which are followed by small, red fruits. It is well adapted to hot, dry environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and desert gardens.
