Miconia barkleyi

Miconia barkleyi, commonly known as the pink miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its bark is smooth and light-colored, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Miconia barkleyi is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
