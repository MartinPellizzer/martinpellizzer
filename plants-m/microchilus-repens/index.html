Microchilus Repens

Microchilus repens is a small, delicate plant known for its unique growth habits and ecological significance. This species is characterized by its creeping stems and tiny, clustered leaves that help it conserve moisture. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity.
