Myonima multiflora

Myonima multiflora, commonly known as the silverleaf myonima, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Myonima multiflora is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
