Marsdenia lanceolata

Marsdenia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the summer. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
