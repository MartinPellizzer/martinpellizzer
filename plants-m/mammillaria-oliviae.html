Mammillaria oliviae

Mammillaria oliviae, commonly known as Olivia's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5-7 centimeters and has a compact, rounded shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the axils of the ribs.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
