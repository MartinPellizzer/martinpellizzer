Maba olivacea

Maba olivacea, commonly known as the olive maba, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.

The plant also has traditional medicinal uses in various African cultures.
