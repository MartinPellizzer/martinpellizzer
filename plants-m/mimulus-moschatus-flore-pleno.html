Mimulus moschatus flore-pleno

Mimulus moschatus flore-pleno, commonly known as the full-flowered yellow monkeyflower, is a striking member of the Phrymaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow flowers, which are typically tubular with a distinctive spur at the back. Its flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies, due to their nectar-rich structure.

The plant is typically herbaceous, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal.
