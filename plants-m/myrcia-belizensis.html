Myrcia belizensis

Myrcia belizensis, commonly known as the Belize myrtle, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered together. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.

Myrcia belizensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
