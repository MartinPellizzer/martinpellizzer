Melanoseris hastata

Melanoseris hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved melampygus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Melampygaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
