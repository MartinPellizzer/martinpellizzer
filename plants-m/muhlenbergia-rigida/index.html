Muhlenbergia Rigida

Muhlenbergia rigida is a hardy grass species native to the central and eastern united states. it is known for its attractive silver-green foliage and delicate, feathery seed heads. this plant thrives in a variety of soil types and is drought tolerant once established. it is often used in native plant gardens and for erosion control. its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
