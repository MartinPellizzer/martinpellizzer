Myrciaria tolypantha var. angustifolia

Myrciaria tolypantha var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved myrtle, is a shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are red, fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local communities.
