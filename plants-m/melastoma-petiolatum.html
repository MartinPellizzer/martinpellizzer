Melastoma petiolatum

Melastoma petiolatum, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from white to pink or purple.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
