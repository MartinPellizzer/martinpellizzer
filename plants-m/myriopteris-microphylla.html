Myriopteris microphylla

Myriopteris microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaf moonwort, is a fern that belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Myriopteris microphylla is notable for its intricate leaf structure and its role in forest understory ecosystems.
