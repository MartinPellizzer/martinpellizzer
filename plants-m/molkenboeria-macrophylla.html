Molkenboeria macrophylla

Molkenboeria macrophylla, commonly known as the giant molkenboeria, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in warm climates.
