Melicope cornuta

Melicope cornuta, commonly known as the horned melicope, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive, branched, and often spiny stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

Melicope cornuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
