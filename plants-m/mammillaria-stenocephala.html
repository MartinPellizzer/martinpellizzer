Mammillaria stenocephala

Mammillaria stenocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and xeriscaping.
