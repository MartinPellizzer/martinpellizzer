Mesembryanthemum dolomiticum

Mesembryanthemum dolomiticum, commonly known as the Dolomitic Ice Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive drought conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species within the Aizoaceae family.
