Munychia cymbalarioides

Munychia cymbalarioides, commonly known as the rock fig, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces edible, fig-like fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
