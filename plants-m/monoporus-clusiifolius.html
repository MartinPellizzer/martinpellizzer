Monoporus clusiifolius

Monoporus clusiifolius, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are typically opposite and have a glossy, dark green appearance with a slightly waxy texture. It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
