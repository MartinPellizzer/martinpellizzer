Mesembryanthemum × octophyllum

Mesembryanthemum × octophyllum, commonly known as the hybrid ice plant, is a succulent plant belonging to the Aizoaceae family.

It is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and yellow. Its botanical features make it well-suited for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
