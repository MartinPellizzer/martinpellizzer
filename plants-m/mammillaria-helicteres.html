Mammillaria helicteres

Mammillaria helicteres, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white or pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
