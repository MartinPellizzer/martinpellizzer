Mirabilis longiflora var. wrightiana

Mirabilis longiflora var. wrightiana, commonly known as Wright's Marvelous Flower, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant belongs to the Nyctaginaceae family and is known for its unique ability to open its flowers in the evening and close them during the day. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
