Medicago Falcata Var. Suffruticosa

Medicago falcata var. suffruticosa is a hardy perennial plant known for its resilience in arid and semi-arid environments. It belongs to the legume family and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. This variety is characterized by its low-growing, shrub-like structure and small, trifoliate leaves. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects. Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for improving soil fertility.
