Mathewsia diffusa

Mathewsia diffusa, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mathewsia diffusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
