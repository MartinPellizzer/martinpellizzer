Melicope robusta

Melicope robusta, commonly known as the broadleaf melicope, is a tropical tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its rough, dark bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody fruits containing aromatic seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas.

Melicope robusta is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
