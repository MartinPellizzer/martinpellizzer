Macrosolen clavatus

Macrosolen clavatus, commonly known as the clustered spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Macrosolen clavatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
