Magnolia bawangensis

Magnolia bawangensis, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or *Magnolia bawangensis*, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The tree has smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its flowers emit a strong, sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
