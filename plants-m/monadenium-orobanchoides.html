Monadenium orobanchoides

Monadenium orobanchoides, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. The plant is characterized by its erect, spindly stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. It has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through its deep root system and water-storing tissues.
