Musa mannii var. mannii

Musa mannii var. mannii, commonly known as the dessert banana, is a tropical plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, and often curved leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of elongated, yellowish-green pseudostems that eventually develop into elongated, curved fruits. The fruit is typically sweet and starchy, with a creamy texture and a flavor reminiscent of a banana.

This variety is cultivated for its edible fruit and is widely grown in tropical regions for both local consumption and commercial purposes.
