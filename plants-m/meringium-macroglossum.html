Meringium macroglossum

Meringium macroglossum, commonly known as the giant tongue fern, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad fronds that can reach lengths of up to three meters. The plant features a robust, upright rhizome with a thick, fibrous structure that supports its massive foliage. Its leaves are typically divided into several segments, creating a feathery, lace-like appearance.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
