Molina sagittalis

Molina sagittalis, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or yellow flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Molina sagittalis is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
