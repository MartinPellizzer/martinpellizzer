Maxburretia rupicola

Maxburretia rupicola, commonly known as the rock fig, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Moraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits. It thrives in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
