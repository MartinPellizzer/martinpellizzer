Matthiola tristis f. lividiflora

Matthiola tristis f. lividiflora, commonly known as the dark-flowered stock, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, often purple or dark violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has linear, hairy leaves. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

The f. lividiflora form is distinguished by its darker flower color compared to the typical form of Matthiola tristis.
