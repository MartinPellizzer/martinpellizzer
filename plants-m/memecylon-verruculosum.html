Memecylon verruculosum

Memecylon verruculosum, commonly known as the rough memecylon, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, lowland forests. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in greenish or yellowish clusters.

The fruit is a globose, woody capsule that splits open to release the seeds.
