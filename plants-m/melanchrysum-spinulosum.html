Melanchrysum spinulosum

Melanchrysum spinulosum, commonly known as the spiny melanchrysum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, spiny leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and gardeners alike.
