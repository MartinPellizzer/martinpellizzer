Malcolmia patula var. gracilis

Malcolmia patula var. gracilis, commonly known as slender malcolmia, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs. The plant typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters, with a sprawling growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
