Masdevallia venus

Masdevallia venus, commonly known as the Venus's Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that resemble the mythical figure of Venus. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces intricate, long-lasting blooms.

The botanical features of Masdevallia venus make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
