Michelia hedyosperma

Michelia hedyosperma, commonly known as the Chinese Michelia, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Michelia hedyosperma is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in gardens for its pleasant aroma and seasonal blooms.
