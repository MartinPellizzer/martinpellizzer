Maxillaria cuencana

Maxillaria cuencana, commonly known as the Cuencan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria cuencana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
