Mammillaria brachytrichion

Mammillaria brachytrichion, commonly known as the short-hair mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a compact stem covered in white, woolly spines. The flowers are typically yellow or orange and bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This plant is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
