Mikania oreophila

Mikania oreophila, commonly known as the mountain mikania, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow and arranged in clusters, producing small seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Mikania oreophila thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
