Melandrium integripetalum

Melandrium integripetalum, commonly known as the narrowleaf catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, pink to reddish-purple flowers. The plant has simple, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically blooms from June to August and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
