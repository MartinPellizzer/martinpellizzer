Melastoma tiliifolium

Melastoma tiliifolium, commonly known as the white-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
