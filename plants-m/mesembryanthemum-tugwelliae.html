Mesembryanthemum tugwelliae

Mesembryanthemum tugwelliae, commonly known as the Tugwell's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
