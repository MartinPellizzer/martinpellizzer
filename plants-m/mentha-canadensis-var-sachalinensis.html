Mentha canadensis var. sachalinensis

Mentha canadensis var. sachalinensis, commonly known as Siberian mint, is a variant of the American mint species.

It is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia, including regions of Russia and China. The plant features square, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. Its aromatic leaves emit a strong menthol scent when crushed.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
