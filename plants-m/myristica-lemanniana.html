Myristica lemanniana

Myristica lemanniana, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy green leaves and fragrant, woody fruit. The tree produces a unique fruit that contains two seeds, one of which is the highly valued nutmeg, while the other is the mace. The plant is also noted for its aromatic oils and is often cultivated for its medicinal and culinary uses.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
