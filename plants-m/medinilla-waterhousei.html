Medinilla waterhousei

Medinilla waterhousei, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often blooming during the holiday season. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Medinilla waterhousei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
