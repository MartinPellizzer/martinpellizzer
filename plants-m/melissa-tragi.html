Melissa tragi

Melissa tragi, commonly known as goatweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in umbels. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to regions of Europe and Asia. It has feathery leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine.
