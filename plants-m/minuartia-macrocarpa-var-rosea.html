Minuartia macrocarpa var. rosea

Minuartia macrocarpa var. rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and alpine environments.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to rose, often with a white center. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging growing conditions.
