Mammillaria roemeri

Mammillaria roemeri, commonly known as the Roemeri Pincushion Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white to tan spines and small, globular tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
