Mammillaria exsudans

Mammillaria exsudans, commonly known as the weeping mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing species that prefers well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
