Microphlebodium muenchii

Microphlebodium muenchii, commonly known as the Muench's spider scale, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a terrestrial bromeliad characterized by its dense, overlapping leaf rosette that forms a water-holding cup. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. Its leaves are covered in fine, scale-like structures that give it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
