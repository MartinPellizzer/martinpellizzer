Maligia gracilis

Maligia gracilis, commonly known as the slender maligia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Maligiacaceae and is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or along riverbanks. Its roots are known for their medicinal properties, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Maligia gracilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its elegant appearance.
