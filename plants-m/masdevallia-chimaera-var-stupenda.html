Masdevallia chimaera var. stupenda

Masdevallia chimaera var. stupenda, commonly known as the Stupenda Masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, intricate flowers that display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure.

Its unique coloration and delicate structure make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
