Maranta zebrina

Maranta zebrina, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with alternating light and dark green stripes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Maranta zebrina is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental leaves and relatively easy care requirements.
