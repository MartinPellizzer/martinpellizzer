Magnolia sieboldii subsp. sinensis

Magnolia sieboldii subsp.

sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the Yunnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China. It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear.

The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly smaller flowers and more compact growth compared to the typical M. sieboldii.
