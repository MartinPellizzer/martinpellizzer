Myrobalanus Melanocarpa

Myrobalanus melanocarpa is a unique species of flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern India and Sri Lanka. It is known for its distinctive dark purple fruits and its ability to thrive in sandy, saline soils. This plant plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and preventing erosion. Its medicinal properties have also been studied for their potential health benefits. Despite its ecological and historical significance, it remains relatively understudied in modern botanical research.
