Mucuna lyonii

Mucuna lyonii, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety pubescence that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several elliptical seeds. Mucuna lyonii is known for its rapid growth and ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in agricultural systems.

It also has traditional medicinal uses in various cultures.
