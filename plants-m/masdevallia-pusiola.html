Masdevallia pusiola

Masdevallia pusiola, commonly known as the pink masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that typically feature a pink or red lip. The plant produces small, linear leaves and has a delicate, intricate inflorescence. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
