Melastoma vacillans var. pallens

Melastoma vacillans var. pallens, commonly known as the pale melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The flowers are typically pale pink to white, with five petals and prominent stamens. It produces small, fleshy berries that are edible and often consumed by local fauna.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
