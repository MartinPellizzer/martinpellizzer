Memecylon arnottianum var. grandifolium

Memecylon arnottianum var. grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf memecylon, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy berries.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as mountain forests and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
