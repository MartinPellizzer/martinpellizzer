Maurocenia phylliraea

Maurocenia phylliraea, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Maurocenia phylliraea is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
