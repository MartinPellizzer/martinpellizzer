Mussaenda rivularis var. rivularis

Mussaenda rivularis var. rivularis, commonly known as the river mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near streams or in forest undergrowth. The plant produces clusters of showy, yellowish-orange bract-like flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
