Melpomene pilosissima var. tsatchelae

Melpomene pilosissima var. tsatchelae, commonly known as the Tsatchelae mallow, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display a vibrant red or pink coloration. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
