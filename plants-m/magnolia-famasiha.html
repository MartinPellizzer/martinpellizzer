Magnolia famasiha

Magnolia famasiha, commonly known as the Madagascar magnolia, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy, and fragrant flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces pendulous, cup-shaped blooms that are typically white or pale pink, with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its ornamental flowers.

Magnolia famasiha thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
