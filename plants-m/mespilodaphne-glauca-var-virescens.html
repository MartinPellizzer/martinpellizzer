Mespilodaphne glauca var. virescens

Mespilodaphne glauca var. virescens, commonly known as the green-leaved mespilodaphne, is a variant of the mespilodaphne plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking visual contrast to its pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically consumed by local wildlife. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
