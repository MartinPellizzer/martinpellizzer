Mitrephora pictiflora

Mitrephora pictiflora, commonly known as the pictured-flowered mitrephora, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns, which give it its common name. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its ecological importance in its native habitat.
