Monachyron roseum

Monachyron roseum, commonly known as the pink-flowered orchid, is a rare and exquisite species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, velvety leaves and striking, bi-color blooms that range from soft pink to deep crimson. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its flowers typically bloom during the late spring to early summer months, adding a touch of elegance to its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity and beauty, Monachyron roseum is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
