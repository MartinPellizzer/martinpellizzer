Mammillaria ancistracantha

Mammillaria ancistracantha, commonly known as the spiny mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered with white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
