Mikania trimeria

Mikania trimeria, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a vigorous vine native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or spread along the ground. It has opposite leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Mikania trimeria is often found in moist, disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.
