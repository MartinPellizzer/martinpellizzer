Meliosma sinensis

Meliosma sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, ovate leaves with a distinctive white or silver underside. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Meliosma sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and floral display.
