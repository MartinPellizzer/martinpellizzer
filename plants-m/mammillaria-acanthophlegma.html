Mammillaria acanthophlegma

Mammillaria acanthophlegma, commonly known as the spiny hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
