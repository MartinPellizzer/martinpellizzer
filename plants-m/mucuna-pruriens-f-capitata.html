Mucuna pruriens f. capitata

Mucuna pruriens f. capitata, commonly known as the hairy nightshade or velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, trifoliate leaves, and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces large, pod-like fruits that contain numerous flat, kidney-shaped seeds. It is native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types.

The plant also has a hairy texture on its stems and leaves, which can cause an itchy sensation when touched.
