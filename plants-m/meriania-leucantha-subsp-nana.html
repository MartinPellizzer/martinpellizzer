Meriania leucantha subsp. nana

Meriania leucantha subsp.

nana, commonly known as the dwarf meriania, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers with a subtle pinkish tinge. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
