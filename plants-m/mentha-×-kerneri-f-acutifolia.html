Mentha × kerneri f. acutifolia

Mentha × kerneri f. acutifolia, commonly known as the acuteleaf hybrid mint, is a cultivated form of a hybrid between Mentha × piperita and Mentha × gracilis.

It is characterized by its narrow, acute leaves and a strong, aromatic scent similar to other mint species. This variety is often used in herbal teas and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, fragrant plant.
