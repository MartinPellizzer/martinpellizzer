Mammillaria knebeliana

Mammillaria knebeliana, commonly known as the Knebel's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a pale green to bluish-green body. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
