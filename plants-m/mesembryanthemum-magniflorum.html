Mesembryanthemum magniflorum

Mesembryanthemum magniflorum, commonly known as the showy ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and vibrant, daisy-like flowers.
