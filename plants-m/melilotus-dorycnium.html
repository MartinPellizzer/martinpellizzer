Melilotus dorycnium

Melilotus dorycnium, commonly known as yellow sweet clover, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been naturalized in various regions around the world. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed areas.

Melilotus dorycnium is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant for soil enrichment.
