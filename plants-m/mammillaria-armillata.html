Mammillaria armillata

Mammillaria armillata, commonly known as the ringed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive ringed appearance on its stem. The plant features white to pale blue spines and produces yellow flowers during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
