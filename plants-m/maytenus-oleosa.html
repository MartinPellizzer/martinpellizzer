Maytenus oleosa

Maytenus oleosa, commonly known as the oil tree or yellow mayten, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.
