Macrolobium pinnatum

Macrolobium pinnatum, commonly known as the featherleaf macrolobium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pinnately compound leaves that give it its common name. The leaves are typically bright green and have a feathery appearance, making it an ornamental plant. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its visual appeal.

The plant prefers warm, humid climates and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
