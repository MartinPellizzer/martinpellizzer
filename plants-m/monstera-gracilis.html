Monstera gracilis

Monstera gracilis, commonly known as the slender monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, fenestrated leaves that become more pronounced as the plant matures. The leaves are typically a deep green with a smooth texture and often have a waxy appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush foliage.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
