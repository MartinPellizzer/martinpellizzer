Mallotus leptophyllus

Mallotus leptophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. It typically grows in lowland rainforests and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
