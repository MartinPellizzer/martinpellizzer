Uragoga verticillata

Uragoga verticillata, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Uragoga verticillata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
