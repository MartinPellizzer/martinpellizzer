Uraria balansae

Uraria balansae, commonly known as the Balansae uraria, is a shrub native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a woody stem with thorny branches, which helps it survive in arid environments. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Uraria balansae is often used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization in degraded landscapes.
