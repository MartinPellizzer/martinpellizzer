Uragoga rolfseana

Uragoga rolfseana, commonly known as the Rolfsean orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces small, clustered blooms in shades of white and pale yellow.

Uragoga rolfseana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
