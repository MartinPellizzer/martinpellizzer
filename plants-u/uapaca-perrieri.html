Uapaca perrieri

Uapaca perrieri, commonly known as the African wolfberry, is a small shrub native to the tropical rainforests of Central and Eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are edible and have a tangy, slightly acidic flavor.
