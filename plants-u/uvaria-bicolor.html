Uvaria bicolor

Uvaria bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored uvaria, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive bi-colored flowers, which typically have white petals with a red or purple base. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor.
