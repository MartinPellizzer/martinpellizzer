It is characterized by its distinctive, horizontally branching fronds that form a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with small, lance-shaped pinnae that are arranged in a staggered manner.

Unona setigera thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.
