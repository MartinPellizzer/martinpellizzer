Urophyllum fuscum

Urophyllum fuscum, commonly known as the darkleaf euphorbia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant produces a sticky, milky sap that is typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in its native habitat.
