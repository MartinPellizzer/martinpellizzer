Urostachys patens

Urostachys patens, commonly known as the spreading urustachy, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in dense inflorescences. Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
