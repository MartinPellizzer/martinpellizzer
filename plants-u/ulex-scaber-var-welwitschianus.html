Ulex scaber var. welwitschianus

Ulex scaber var. welwitschianus, commonly known as the welwitsch spikeless burr.

It is a variant of the common burr plant, characterized by its reduced or absent spines compared to the typical Ulex scaber. This plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It produces yellow flowers in late spring and is often found in coastal and inland dunes.

The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in arid environments.
