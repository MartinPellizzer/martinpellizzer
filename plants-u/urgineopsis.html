Urgineopsis

Urgineopsis, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that open in the morning and close by midday. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
