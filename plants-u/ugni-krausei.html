Ugni krausei

Ugni krausei, commonly known as the Andean huckleberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant produces small, edible berries that are rich in antioxidants and are used in local cuisine. Its leaves are evergreen and have a leathery texture, while its flowers are typically white or pink.

Ugni krausei thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude forests.
