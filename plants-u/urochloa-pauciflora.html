Urochloa pauciflora

Urochloa pauciflora, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, disturbed soils, and along roadsides. The plant has slender, erect stems with small, clustered inflorescences that produce numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a hairy surface.

Urochloa pauciflora is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in dry conditions.
