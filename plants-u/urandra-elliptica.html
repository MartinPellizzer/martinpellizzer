Urandra elliptica

Urandra elliptica, commonly known as the broadleaf urandra, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elliptical, leathery leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in panicles, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

Urandra elliptica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
