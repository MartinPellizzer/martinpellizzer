Ulex nanus f. thorei

Ulex nanus f. thorei, commonly known as the Thorei's goat's rue, is a low-growing shrub native to the western Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, spiny stems and its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is relatively drought-tolerant.

This variety is a hardy, ornamental plant that is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance and ecological value.
