Uvaria Moluccana

Uvaria moluccana is a tropical evergreen shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. It is known for its small, dark purple berries that are both ornamental and edible. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for horticultural collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream botanical discussions.
