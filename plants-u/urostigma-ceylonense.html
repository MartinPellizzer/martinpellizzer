Urostigma ceylonense

Urostigma ceylonense, commonly known as the Ceylon star anise.

It is a shrub native to Sri Lanka, characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, star-shaped fruits. The plant belongs to the Rutaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.

The fruit, resembling a star anise, contains essential oils that contribute to its distinctive fragrance and therapeutic uses.
