Urostachys linifolius var. lancifolius

Urostachys linifolius var. lancifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star grass, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, clustered inflorescences with white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are linear and often have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
