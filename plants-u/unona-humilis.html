Unona humilis

Unona humilis, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Unona humilis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
