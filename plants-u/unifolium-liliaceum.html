Unifolium liliaceum

Unifolium liliaceum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lily, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is native to temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and woodland edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated for both aesthetic and medicinal purposes.
