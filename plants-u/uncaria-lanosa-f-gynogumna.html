Uncaria lanosa f. gynogumna

Uncaria lanosa f. gynogumna, commonly known as the hairy cat's claw, is a variant of the cat's claw plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and compound leaves with small, clustered leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, red, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its medicinal properties, with the roots and stems used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is well adapted to tropical rainforests.
