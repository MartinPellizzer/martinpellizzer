Uluxia

Uluxia, commonly known as the sacred lotus of the Andes, is a striking aquatic plant native to the high-altitude lakes of South America.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often blooming just above the water's surface. The plant has broad, waxy leaves that float on the water, aiding in photosynthesis while providing habitat for small aquatic life. Its roots anchor it in the muddy lakebed, and it plays a significant role in the local ecosystem.

Uluxia is also revered in indigenous cultures for its symbolic and spiritual importance.
