Ursinia heterodonta

Ursinia heterodonta, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-like inflorescence composed of small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Ursinia heterodonta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
