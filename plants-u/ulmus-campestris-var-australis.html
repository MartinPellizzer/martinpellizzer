Ulmus campestris var. australis

Ulmus campestris var. australis, commonly known as the Australian field elm, is a variant of the field elm native to Australia.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and smooth, gray bark. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by winged seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
