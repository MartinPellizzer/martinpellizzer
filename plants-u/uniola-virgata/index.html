Uniola Virgata

Uniola virgata is a hardy grass species native to the southeastern united states. it thrives in coastal and wetland environments, often found in sandy soils along the gulf coast. this plant is known for its ability to withstand salt spray and periodic flooding. its narrow leaves and compact growth make it a valuable plant for erosion control. despite its resilience, it remains a relatively uncommon species in the wild.
