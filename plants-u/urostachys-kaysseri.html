Urostachys kaysseri

Urostachys kaysseri, commonly known as the Kaysseri feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce fluffy seeds. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions. Its delicate appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.

Urostachys kaysseri plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and supports local pollinators.
