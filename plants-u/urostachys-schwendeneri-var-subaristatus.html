Urostachys schwendeneri var. subaristatus

Urostachys schwendeneri var. subaristatus, commonly known as the slender leafed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern China.

It features compact, low-growing rosettes with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
