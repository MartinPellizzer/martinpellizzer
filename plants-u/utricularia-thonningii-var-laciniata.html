Utricularia thonningii var. laciniata

Utricularia thonningii var. laciniata, commonly known as the laciniated bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and specialized bladders used to trap prey. The plant typically grows in water or moist soil, and its flowers are usually small and greenish-white. It has a unique ability to photosynthesize while also supplementing its nutrients through predation.

This variety is noted for its intricate leaf structures and is a subject of interest in botanical research.
