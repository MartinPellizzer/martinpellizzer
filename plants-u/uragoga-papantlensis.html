Uragoga papantlensis

Uragoga papantlensis, commonly known as the Papantla orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy blooms. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of forests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, adapted to capture limited sunlight.

Uragoga papantlensis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in local communities.
