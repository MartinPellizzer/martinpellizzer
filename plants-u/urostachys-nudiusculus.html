Urostachys nudiusculus

Urostachys nudiusculus, commonly known as the naked-stemmed star grass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a slender, unbranched stem that often lies prostrate on the ground. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Urostachys nudiusculus is adapted to harsh, alpine environments and is known for its resilience to cold and arid conditions.
