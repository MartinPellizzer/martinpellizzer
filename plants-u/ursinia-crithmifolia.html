Ursinia crithmifolia

Ursinia crithmifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ursinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Ursinia crithmifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
