Uragoga vieillardii

Uragoga vieillardii, commonly known as the Cape Verde orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces strikingly colorful, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, showcasing intricate floral structures with specialized pollination mechanisms. Its botanical features include strap-like leaves and a short, erect stem.
