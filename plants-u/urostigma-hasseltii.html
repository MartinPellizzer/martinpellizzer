Urostigma hasseltii

Urostigma hasseltii, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by distinctive, kidney-shaped seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Urostigma hasseltii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
