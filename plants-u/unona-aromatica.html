Unona aromatica

Unona aromatica, commonly known as the aromatic unona, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its essential oils, extracted from the leaves and flowers, are valued for their aromatic properties and potential therapeutic uses.

Unona aromatica thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.
