Urophyllum xanthorrhoeum

Urophyllum xanthorrhoeum, commonly known as the common grass tree, is a tall, perennial herb native to Australia.

It features a fibrous root system and a distinctive, tall, cylindrical stem that can reach up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces a dense panicle of small, yellowish flowers, which are borne on long, branched stalks. Its leaves are long, narrow, and grass-like, arranged in a rosette at the base.

The species is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and heathlands.
