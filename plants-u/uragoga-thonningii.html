Uragoga thonningii

Uragoga thonningii, commonly known as the African orchid or thonning's orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverine areas. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often with a waxy texture.

Uragoga thonningii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
