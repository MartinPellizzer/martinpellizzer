Ulex × dalilae

Ulex × dalilae, commonly known as the hybrid goat's beard, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Ulex europaeus and Ulex parviflorus.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant is native to the British Isles and thrives in coastal and sandy soils. Its foliage consists of leathery, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green in color.

Ulex × dalilae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
