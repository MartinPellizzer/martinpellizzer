Urera altissima

Urera altissima, commonly known as the giant urera, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.
