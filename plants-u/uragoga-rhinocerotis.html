Uragoga rhinocerotis

Uragoga rhinocerotis, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, horn-like inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are broad and glossy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Uragoga rhinocerotis is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a rare horticultural specimen.
