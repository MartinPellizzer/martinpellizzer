Uncinia strictissima

Uncinia strictissima, commonly known as the rigid hookweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and slender, hairy stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, often covered in fine hairs.

Uncinia strictissima is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
