Uragoga tabernifolia

Uragoga tabernifolia, commonly known as the tropical evergreen shrub, is a plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrant blooms.
