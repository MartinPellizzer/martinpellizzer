Urospatha langsdorffiana

Urospatha langsdorffiana, commonly known as the Brazilian water lily, is a tropical aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots are submerged, anchoring it in shallow waters and absorbing nutrients from the sediment.

Urospatha langsdorffiana plays an important role in maintaining water quality and supporting biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
