Ulex africanus var. delestrei

Ulex africanus var. delestrei, commonly known as the African Scotch broom, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and spiny, trifoliate leaves. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is sometimes used in ecological restoration due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
