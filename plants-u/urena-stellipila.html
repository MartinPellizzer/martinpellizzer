Urena stellipila

Urena stellipila, commonly known as star urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows up to 2 meters in height, characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

Urena stellipila is often found in disturbed areas and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
