Urostachys orionis

Urostachys orionis, commonly known as the starry stiltgrass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Urostachys orionis is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
