Ultragossypium × armadense

Ultragossypium × armadense, commonly known as the armored cotton.

It is a hybrid plant species resulting from the crossbreeding of Gossypium barbadense and Gossypium hirsutum. This plant is notable for its thick, waxy coating on its leaves and stems, which provides protection against herbivores and environmental stress. The botanical features include large, lobed leaves and fluffy, white cotton fibers that are used in textile production.

It is primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions where it thrives in warm, humid climates.
