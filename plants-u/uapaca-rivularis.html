Uapaca rivularis

Uapaca rivularis, commonly known as the river uapaca, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as riverine forests and swampy areas. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often greenish or yellowish in color, and it produces edible berries that are an important food source for various animals.

The tree is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
