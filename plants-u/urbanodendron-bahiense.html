Urbanodendron bahiense

Urbanodendron bahiense, commonly known as the Bahamian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface, while its stems are woody and often branched.

Urbanodendron bahiense thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and well-drained soils.
