Urostigma gardeniifolium

Urostigma gardeniifolium, commonly known as the gardenia-like leaf urostigma, is a perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are broad and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
