Ursinia laciniata

Ursinia laciniata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ursinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Ursinia laciniata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
