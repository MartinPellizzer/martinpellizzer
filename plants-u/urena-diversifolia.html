Urena diversifolia

Urena diversifolia, commonly known as velvetleaf or Indian mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces capsules containing numerous small, dry seeds that are dispersed by wind. Urena diversifolia is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in some areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
