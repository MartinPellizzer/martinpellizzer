Uapaca densifolia var. perrieri

Uapaca densifolia var. perrieri, commonly known as the Kipango tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces edible berries that are an important food source for both wildlife and humans. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and local biodiversity.
