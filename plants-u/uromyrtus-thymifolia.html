Uromyrtus thymifolia

Uromyrtus thymifolia, commonly known as the honey bush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

The plant's botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
