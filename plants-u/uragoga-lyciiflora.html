Uragoga lyciiflora

Uragoga lyciiflora, commonly known as the red-flowered nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive red, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to orange as it matures.

Uragoga lyciiflora is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
