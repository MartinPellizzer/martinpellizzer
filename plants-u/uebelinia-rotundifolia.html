Uebelinia rotundifolia

Uebelinia rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaved euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are circular in shape, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Uebelinia rotundifolia is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
