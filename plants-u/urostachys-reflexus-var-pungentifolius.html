Urostachys reflexus var. pungentifolius

Urostachys reflexus var. pungentifolius, commonly known as the spiny leaf sedum, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, spiny leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular ornamental plant in xeriscaping.
