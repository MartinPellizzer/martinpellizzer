Uragoga succulenta

Uragoga succulenta, commonly known as the desert rose, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often blooming during the cooler months. Its root system is shallow and adapted to absorb moisture from infrequent rains.

Uragoga succulenta is popular as an ornamental plant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
