Urbanodendron

Urbanodendron, commonly known as the city tree, is a unique plant adapted to thrive in urban environments.

It possesses thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in polluted air. The plant's deep root system allows it to anchor itself in compacted soil found in city streets. Urbanodendron is also known for its ability to absorb heavy metals, making it useful in phytoremediation efforts.

Its resilience and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for urban landscaping.
