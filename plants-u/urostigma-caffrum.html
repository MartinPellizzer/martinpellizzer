Urostigma caffrum

Urostigma caffrum, commonly known as the Cape umbrella tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, rounded foliage that gives it a distinctive umbrella-like appearance. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are thick and leathery, adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Urostigma caffrum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
