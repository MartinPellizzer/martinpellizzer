Urceolina × clibranii

Urceolina × clibranii, commonly known as the hybrid urn lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Urceolina bulbifera and Urceolina clibranii, characterized by its unique urn-shaped flowers. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
