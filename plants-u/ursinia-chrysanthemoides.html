Ursinia chrysanthemoides

Ursinia chrysanthemoides, commonly known as the Cape chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered blooms throughout the summer months.

Ursinia chrysanthemoides is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant color and hardy nature.
