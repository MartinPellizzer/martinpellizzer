Ungnadia heterophylla

Ungnadia heterophylla, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, with simple, alternate leaves that vary in shape depending on the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

The plant is notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its use in traditional medicine.
