Ursinia tenuifolia

Ursinia tenuifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf ursinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a small shrub with narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ursinia tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
