Urceola densiflora

Urceola densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered urn plant, is a species native to the humid forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, urn-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a dense, compact cluster. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique floral structure and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
