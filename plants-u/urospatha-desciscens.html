Urospatha desciscens

Urospatha desciscens, commonly known as the hanging fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous fronds that drape from its stems, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant's leaves are typically bi-lobed and have a soft, feathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Urospatha desciscens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
