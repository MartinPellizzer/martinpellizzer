Urostachys christii var. orgaosensis

Urostachys christii var. orgaosensis, commonly known as the Orgaosensis stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
