Urostachys attenuatus

Urostachys attenuatus, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and the Himalayas.

Its small yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.
