Urochloa yuanmouensis

Urochloa yuanmouensis, commonly known as the Yuanmou grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. It has small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Urochloa yuanmouensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in restoration projects.
