Uraria aequilobata

Uraria aequilobata, commonly known as the Indian senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the Indian subcontinent and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in racemes. It produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Uraria aequilobata is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
