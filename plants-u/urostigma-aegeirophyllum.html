Urostigma aegeirophyllum

Urostigma aegeirophyllum, commonly known as the Aegean starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Urostigma aegeirophyllum is often found in rocky or dry habitats, making it a characteristic species of Mediterranean scrublands.
