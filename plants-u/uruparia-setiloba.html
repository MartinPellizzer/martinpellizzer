Uruparia setiloba

Uruparia setiloba, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Uruparia setiloba is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.
