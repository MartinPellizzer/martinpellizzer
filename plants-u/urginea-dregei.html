Urginea dregei

Urginea dregei, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem and produces a single, large bloom that can reach up to 30 cm in height. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soil.

Urginea dregei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
