Uvifera billbergii

Uvifera billbergii, commonly known as the Cape ivy, is a climbing shrub native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, fragrant white flowers clustered in umbels. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and dense foliage.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
