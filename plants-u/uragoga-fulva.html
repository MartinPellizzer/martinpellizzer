Uragoga fulva

Uragoga fulva, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant has a slender, erect stem with a smooth, waxy surface. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Uragoga fulva prefers warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
