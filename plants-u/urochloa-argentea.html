Urochloa argentea

Urochloa argentea, commonly known as silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant typically grows in open areas, including lawns, pastures, and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Urochloa argentea is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
