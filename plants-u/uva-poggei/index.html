Uva Poggei

Uva poggei is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Italy. Known for its striking violet flowers and delicate foliage, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species is often found in rocky soils and shaded forest floors, making it a challenging plant to cultivate. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Uva poggei is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and restore its natural habitat.
