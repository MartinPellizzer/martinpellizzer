Urena lobata var. umbonata

Urena lobata var. umbonata, commonly known as the hairy urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
