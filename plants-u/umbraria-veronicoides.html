Umbraria veronicoides

Umbraria veronicoides, commonly known as the slender veronicah, is a herbaceous plant native to the wetlands and marshes of eastern North America.

It typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters, featuring slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple, blooming from June to August. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight reddish hue on the underside.

Umbraria veronicoides thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in association with other wetland flora.
