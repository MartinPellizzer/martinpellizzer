Urena microcarpa

Urena microcarpa, commonly known as small-fruited urena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant features hairy stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its small, pale yellow flowers are clustered in inflorescences, and it produces small, round, indehiscent fruits.

Urena microcarpa is often found in disturbed habitats and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
