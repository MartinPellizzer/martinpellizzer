Ursinia merxmuelleri

Ursinia merxmuelleri, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in umbel-like clusters, which give it its common name. It has narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Ursinia merxmuelleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
