Uragoga ixoroides

Uragoga ixoroides, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a flowering shrub that produces distinctive, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red and orange. The plant typically grows in humid, shady environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a smooth texture and a deep green color.

Uragoga ixoroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
