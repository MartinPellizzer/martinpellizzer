Urostachys linifolius var. flexibilis

Urostachys linifolius var. flexibilis, commonly known as the flexible leaf grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically flexible and can be found in dense clusters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
