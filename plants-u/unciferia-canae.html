Unciferia canae

Unciferia canae, commonly known as the Cape York thornveld grass, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, blade-like leaves with a rough texture and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland ecosystems.

Unciferia canae is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it resilient to drought.
