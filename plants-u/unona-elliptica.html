Unona elliptica

Unona elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed unona, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features elongated, elliptic leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often blooming during the wet season. Its root system is fibrous and can be used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Unona elliptica thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.
