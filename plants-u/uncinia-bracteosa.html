Uncinia bracteosa

Uncinia bracteosa, commonly known as the hooked bract grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. Uncinia bracteosa is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and rocky areas.
