Urena lobata var. sinuata

Urena lobata var. sinuata, commonly known as silky urena, is a tropical annual herb native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in a fibrous, three-lobed capsule that splits open when mature.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
