Urostachys intermedius

Urostachys intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate urostachys, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
