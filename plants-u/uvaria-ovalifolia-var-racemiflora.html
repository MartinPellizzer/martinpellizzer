Uvaria ovalifolia var. racemiflora

Uvaria ovalifolia var. racemiflora, commonly known as the racemiflorous uvaria, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences, which bloom during the rainy season. Its fruits are fleshy berries that mature to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.

This species is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
