Urostachys kuesteri

Urostachys kuesteri, commonly known as the Kuester's feather grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration that helps it blend into its natural environment.

Urostachys kuesteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
