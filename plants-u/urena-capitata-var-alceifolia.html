Urena capitata var. alceifolia

Urena capitata var. alceifolia, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, globular inflorescence composed of numerous yellow to orange flowers. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
