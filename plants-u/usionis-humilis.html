Usionis humilis

Usionis humilis, commonly known as the lowly usionis, is a small herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a distinct, grass-like aroma. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Usionis humilis is known for its resilience to humid conditions and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
