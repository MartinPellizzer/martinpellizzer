Urostachys bonae-voluntatis var. minor

Urostachys bonae-voluntatis var. minor, commonly known as the Happy Bells, is a low-growing, mat-forming succulent native to southern China.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and vibrant floral display.
