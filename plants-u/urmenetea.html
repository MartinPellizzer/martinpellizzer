Urmenetea

Urmenetea, commonly known as the common urmenetea, is a species native to certain regions of Europe.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and tall, slender flowering stems. The flowers are typically small and clustered in umbels, often displaying shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

The plant plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations due to its nectar-rich blooms.
