Urtica lucida

Urtica lucida, commonly known as the shining nettle, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a dense covering of fine, stinging hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Urtica lucida is characterized by its pale green to yellowish flowers that appear in clusters.
