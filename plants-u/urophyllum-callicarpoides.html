Urophyllum callicarpoides

Urophyllum callicarpoides, commonly known as the red-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, red or orange flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically orange or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Urophyllum callicarpoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
