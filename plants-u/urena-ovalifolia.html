Urena ovalifolia

Urena ovalifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf or smallleaf urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds. Urena ovalifolia is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and agricultural areas.

It is sometimes considered a weed due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
