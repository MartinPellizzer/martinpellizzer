Urostachys aloifolius

Urostachys aloifolius, commonly known as the silver leaf saxifrage, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
