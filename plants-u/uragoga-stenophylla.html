Uragoga stenophylla

Uragoga stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf uragoga, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. It is primarily found in specific regions of Malaysia and Indonesia, where its habitat is increasingly threatened by deforestation.
