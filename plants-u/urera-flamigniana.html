Urera flamigniana

Urera flamigniana, commonly known as the flame-leaf urera, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, broad, palmate leaves that are often a vibrant green with red or yellow veins. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and a subject of interest for plant enthusiasts.
