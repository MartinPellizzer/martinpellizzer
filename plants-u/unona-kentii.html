Unona kentii

Unona kentii, commonly known as the Kentii spider plant, is a unique member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features long, arching leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Unona kentii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in various light conditions.
