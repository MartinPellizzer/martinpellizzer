Urtica chamaedryoides var. runyonii

Urtica chamaedryoides var. runyonii, commonly known as the Runyon's nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of stinging nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and fine, stinging hairs on both sides. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
