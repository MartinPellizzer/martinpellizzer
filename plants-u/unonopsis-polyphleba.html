Unonopsis polyphleba

Unonopsis polyphleba, commonly known as the broad-leaved unon, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and glossy leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
