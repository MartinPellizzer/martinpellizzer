Uapaca bojeri

Uapaca bojeri, commonly known as the African cherry or red mombasa, is a deciduous tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, reddish bark and broad, lobed leaves. Uapaca bojeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
