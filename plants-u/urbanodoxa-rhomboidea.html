Urbanodoxa rhomboidea

Urbanodoxa rhomboidea, commonly known as the rhomboid bellflower, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive rhombus-shaped leaves and showy, bell-like flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens and small spaces.
