Ulmaria palmata

Ulmaria palmata, commonly known as white hellebore, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant features a short, thick rhizome and produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a waxy coating.

Ulmaria palmata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
