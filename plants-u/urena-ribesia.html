Urena ribesia

Urena ribesia, commonly known as wild cotton or velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruits are capsules that split open to release numerous small, fluffy seeds.

Urena ribesia is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
