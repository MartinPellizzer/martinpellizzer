Urachne panicoides var. vulgaris

Urachne panicoides var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common urachne, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, or grass family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
