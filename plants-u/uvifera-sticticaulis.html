Uvifera sticticaulis

Uvifera sticticaulis, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a globose berry that ripens to a dark purple or black color.

Uvifera sticticaulis is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
