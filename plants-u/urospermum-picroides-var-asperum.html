Urospermum picroides var. asperum

Urospermum picroides var. asperum, commonly known as roughleaf hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features upright stems with serrated, rough-textured leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in flat-topped clusters, blooming from late spring to early summer.

It is characterized by its robust growth and adaptability to arid conditions.
