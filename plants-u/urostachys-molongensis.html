Urostachys molongensis

Urostachys molongensis, commonly known as the Molong grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, white to pale green flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue and a rough texture.

Urostachys molongensis is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils in arid and semi-arid regions.
