Utricularia racemosa var. filicaulis

Utricularia racemosa var. filicaulis, commonly known as the slender-whorled bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and specialized bladder-like structures used to trap prey. The plant typically grows in water or moist soil, often in aquatic environments. Its flowers are small and clustered in a raceme, giving it a delicate appearance.

This variety is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor conditions.
