Urostachys mollicomus var. major

Urostachys mollicomus var. major, commonly known as the soft-leaved star grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are soft to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a favored choice for gardeners interested in native flora.
