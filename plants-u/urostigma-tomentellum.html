Urostigma tomentellum

Urostigma tomentellum, commonly known as the woolly bladder nut, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces distinctive, bladder-like fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Urostigma tomentellum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
