Uncasia Torreyana

Uncasia torreyana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western United States. It thrives in rocky soils and dry climates, often found in coastal regions. Known for its small, clustered white flowers, it adds a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered species from habitat loss.
