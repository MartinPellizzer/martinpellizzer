Urophyllum camposii

Urophyllum camposii, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a woody stem with a smooth, reddish-brown bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy green surface.

Urophyllum camposii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates.
