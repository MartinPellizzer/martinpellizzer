Uncinia purpurata

Uncinia purpurata, commonly known as the purple hook fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, creeping rhizomes and small, clustered fronds. The plant reproduces through spores rather than seeds, and its structure is adapted for efficient water retention in its natural habitat.
