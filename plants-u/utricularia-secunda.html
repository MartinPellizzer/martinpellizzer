Utricularia secunda

Utricularia secunda, commonly known as the lesser bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, bladder-like traps used for capturing microscopic organisms, which provide it with essential nutrients. Its rapid growth and unique trapping mechanism make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
