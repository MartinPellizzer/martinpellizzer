Ulmaria × purpurea var. elegans

Ulmaria × purpurea var. elegans, commonly known as the purple tansy, is a hybrid species belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and yellowish-white flowers that bloom in umbels. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 cm.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in native gardens.
