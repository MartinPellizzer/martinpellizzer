Ungernia oligostroma

Ungernia oligostroma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisies, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and silvery-gray, which helps reduce water loss in hot, dry climates.

Ungernia oligostroma is known for its resilience and is often found in regions of southern Africa.
