Uromyrtus allisoniana

Uromyrtus allisoniana, commonly known as the Allison's ironwood, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are small, leathery, and often covered with fine hairs.

Uromyrtus allisoniana is adapted to arid environments and is known for its hardy, drought-resistant nature.
