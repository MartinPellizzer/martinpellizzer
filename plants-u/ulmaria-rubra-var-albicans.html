Ulmaria rubra var. albicans

Ulmaria rubra var. albicans, commonly known as white hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a long, stout rhizome that stores nutrients and aids in its survival through harsh conditions.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
