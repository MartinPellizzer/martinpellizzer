Ursinia nudicaulis

Ursinia nudicaulis, commonly known as the narrowleaf ursinia, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Ursinia nudicaulis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
