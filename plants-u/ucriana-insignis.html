Ucriana insignis

Ucriana insignis, commonly known as the Brazilian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and twining stems that climb along supports. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other morning glory species.

Ucriana insignis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
