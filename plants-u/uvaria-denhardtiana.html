Uvaria denhardtiana

Uvaria denhardtiana, commonly known as the Denhardt's starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that are edible but not widely cultivated for commercial purposes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary rainforests.
