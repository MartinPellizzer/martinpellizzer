Utricularia linearis

Utricularia linearis, commonly known as the slender bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like stems and tiny, bladder-like structures called utricles, which are used for capturing prey. Utricularia linearis is a carnivorous plant that supplements its nutrient intake by trapping and digesting small aquatic organisms.

Its rapid growth and unique trapping mechanism make it an interesting subject for both ecological and botanical study.
