Uncinia capillaris

Uncinia capillaris, commonly known as the slender hookweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Uncinia capillaris is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through its hooked seeds, which cling to animals and clothing.
