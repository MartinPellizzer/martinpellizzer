Uragoga micromyrtus

Uragoga micromyrtus, commonly known as the small myrtle.

It is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. The plant features small, aromatic leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is typically found in sandy or rocky soils along the coastline.

Uragoga micromyrtus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in coastal ecosystems.
