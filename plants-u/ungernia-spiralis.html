Ungernia spiralis

Ungernia spiralis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
