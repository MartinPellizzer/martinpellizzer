Uragoga multicolor

Uragoga multicolor, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a strikingly colorful plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, often multicolored petals. The plant typically features a compact growth habit with long, slender stems and showy flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its colorful blooms.

Uragoga multicolor is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained, moist soil.
