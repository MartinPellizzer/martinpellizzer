Uragoga suaveolens

Uragoga suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented uragoga, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy surface.

Uragoga suaveolens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
