Urospatha sagittifolia var. spruceana

Urospatha sagittifolia var. spruceana, commonly known as the arrowleaf urospatha, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features lanceolate to arrow-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive basal rosette structure. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. Its botanical characteristics include fibrous roots and a preference for moist, well-drained soils.

It is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories.
