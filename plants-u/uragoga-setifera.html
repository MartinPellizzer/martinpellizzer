Uragoga setifera

Uragoga setifera, commonly known as the slender hairgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. Native to certain regions of South America, it thrives in moist, shady environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically wind-pollinated.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
