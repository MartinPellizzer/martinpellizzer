Urostachys catachiensis

Urostachys catachiensis, commonly known as the Sierra Madre feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
