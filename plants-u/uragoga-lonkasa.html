Uragoga lonkasa

Uragoga lonkasa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its showy, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of purple, blue, and white. The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, often with a waxy coating. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Uragoga lonkasa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
