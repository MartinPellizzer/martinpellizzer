Urostachys callitrichifolius

Urostachys callitrichifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grassvetch, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
