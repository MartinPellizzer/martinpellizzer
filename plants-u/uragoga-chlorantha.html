Uragoga chlorantha

Uragoga chlorantha, commonly known as the green-winged orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pale green or white, with prominent yellow markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Uragoga chlorantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
