Unona congensis

Unona congensis, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. It is often found in humid, forested environments at higher elevations.

Unona congensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
