Urena texana

Urena texana, commonly known as Texas urena, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, disturbed areas such as roadsides and prairies. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Urena texana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
