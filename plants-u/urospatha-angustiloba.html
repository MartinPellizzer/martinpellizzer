Urospatha angustiloba

Urospatha angustiloba, commonly known as the narrow-lobed waterleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its flowering stems bear small, clustered inflorescences with greenish-white flowers.

Urospatha angustiloba is noted for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional remedies.
