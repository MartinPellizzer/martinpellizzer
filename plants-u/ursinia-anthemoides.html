Ursinia anthemoides

Ursinia anthemoides, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Ursinia anthemoides is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
