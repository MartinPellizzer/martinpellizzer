Urostachys brasilianus var. schwackeanus

Urostachys brasilianus var. schwackeanus, commonly known as the Brazilian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are borne on short, erect inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
