Ulex

Ulex, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of North Africa, and it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Ulex is often used in traditional medicine and has been historically employed for its purported healing properties.
