Uleiorchis prataensis

Uleiorchis prataensis, commonly known as the meadow orchid, is a species of orchid native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a herbaceous plant with slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its flowering period usually occurs in late spring to early summer.

Uleiorchis prataensis is characterized by its simple, linear leaves and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
