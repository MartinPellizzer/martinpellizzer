Urostachys reflexus var. densifolius

Urostachys reflexus var. densifolius, commonly known as the dense-leaved stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial succulent native to rocky habitats in China.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are densely packed, giving it a lush, mat-like appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white, typically in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its adaptability to harsh environments and ornamental value make it a popular choice among horticulturists.
