Uragoga brachythere

Uragoga brachythere, commonly known as the short-stemmed fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its compact, upright growth habit.

It features a rhizomatous stem that produces numerous fronds, which are typically lanceolate in shape. The plant's leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Uragoga brachythere is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and ecological significance.
