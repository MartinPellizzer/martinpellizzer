Ursinia abrotanifolia

Ursinia abrotanifolia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Ursinia abrotanifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
