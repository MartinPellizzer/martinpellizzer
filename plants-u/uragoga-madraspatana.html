Uragoga madraspatana

Uragoga madraspatana, commonly known as the Madras nightshade, is a climbing shrub native to southern India.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its fruits are berries that change from green to red as they mature.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a distinctive odor.
