Urtica buchtienii

Urtica buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtienii nettle, is a species of stinging nettle native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy surface, and its stems are covered in fine, stinging hairs. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its root system is fibrous and rhizomatous.

This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
