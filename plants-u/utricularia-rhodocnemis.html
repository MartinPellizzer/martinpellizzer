Utricularia rhodocnemis

Utricularia rhodocnemis, commonly known as the red-stem bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and specialized bladder-like structures used for capturing prey.
