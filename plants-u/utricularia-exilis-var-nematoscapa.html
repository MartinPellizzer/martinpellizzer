Utricularia exilis var. nematoscapa

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and specialized bladder-like structures used for trapping prey. This variant is native to wetland and aquatic environments in tropical and subtropical regions. Its roots are often rhizoidal, anchoring it to substrates in water or moist soil.
