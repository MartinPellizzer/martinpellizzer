Uapaca heudelotii

Uapaca heudelotii, commonly known as the African date palm, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are often consumed fresh or processed into products like matoke.
