Unona lawii

Unona lawii, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to deep red, depending on the species. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. Unona lawii thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to the humid, warm climate of Hawaii.

It plays an important ecological role in providing nectar for native pollinators.
