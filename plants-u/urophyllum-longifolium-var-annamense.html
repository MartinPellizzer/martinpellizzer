Urophyllum longifolium var. annamense

Urophyllum longifolium var. annamense, commonly known as the Annamense Urophyllum, is a variant of the long-leaved urophyllum species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage.
