Urocarpidium

Urocarpidium, commonly known as the bladder wort, is a genus of aquatic or semi-aquatic plants belonging to the family Urochordaceae.

These plants are characterized by their small, bladder-like structures called pneumatocysts, which aid in buoyancy and gas exchange. The genus is notable for its simple morphology and adaptation to aquatic life.
