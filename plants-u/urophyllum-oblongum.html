Urophyllum oblongum

Urophyllum oblongum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Urophyllum oblongum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
