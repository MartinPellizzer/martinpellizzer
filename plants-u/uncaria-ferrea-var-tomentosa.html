Uncaria ferrea var. tomentosa

Uncaria ferrea var. tomentosa, commonly known as the hairy cat's claw, is a climbing vine native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
