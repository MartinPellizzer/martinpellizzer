Urostachys luederwaldtii

Urostachys luederwaldtii, commonly known as the Luederwaldt's feather grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, wiry stems and delicate, thread-like leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, pale yellow flowers. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid environments.

Urostachys luederwaldtii is well adapted to drought conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
