Uragoga persimilis

Uragoga persimilis, commonly known as the delicate orchid, is a rare and elegant flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
