Unamia

Unamia, commonly known as the "single leaf plant," is a unique species within the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its singular, elongated leaf that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Unamia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
