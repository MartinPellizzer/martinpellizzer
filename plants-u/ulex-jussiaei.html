Ulex jussiaei

Ulex jussiaei, commonly known as the common sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the shoreline. The plant produces yellow flowers with bracts that resemble small spines, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it is adapted to withstand salty sea air and strong winds.

Ulex jussiaei plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for various small organisms.
