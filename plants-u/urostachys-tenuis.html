Urostachys tenuis

Urostachys tenuis, commonly known as the slender leaved saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fleshy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
