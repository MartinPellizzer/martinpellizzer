Urostachys setacea var. brasiliensis

Urostachys setacea var. brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, hair-like inflorescences that resemble feathers, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a soft, silky texture.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
