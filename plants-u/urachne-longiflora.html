Urachne longiflora

Urachne longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered urachne, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight hairy texture.

Urachne longiflora is typically found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
