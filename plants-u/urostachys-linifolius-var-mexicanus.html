Urostachys linifolius var. mexicanus

Urostachys linifolius var. mexicanus, commonly known as Mexican silverleaf, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
