Utricularia polygaloides

Utricularia polygaloides, commonly known as the bladderworts, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that contain specialized bladder-like structures used to trap and digest small aquatic organisms. Utricularia polygaloides plays an important ecological role in its habitat by controlling populations of microorganisms and contributing to nutrient cycling.
