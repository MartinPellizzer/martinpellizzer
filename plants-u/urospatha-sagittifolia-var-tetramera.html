Urospatha sagittifolia var. tetramera

Urospatha sagittifolia var. tetramera, commonly known as the arrowleaf urospatha, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a robust, branching stem.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
