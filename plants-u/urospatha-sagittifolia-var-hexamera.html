Urospatha sagittifolia var. hexamera

Urospatha sagittifolia var. hexamera, commonly known as the arrowleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, arrow-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are typically followed by cylindrical, fleshy fruits. Its rhizomes are thick and starchy, making it a valuable crop for food and traditional medicine.

The species is well adapted to moist, shaded environments in the understory of tropical forests.
