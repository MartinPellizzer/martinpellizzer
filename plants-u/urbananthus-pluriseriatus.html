Urbananthus pluriseriatus

Urbananthus pluriseriatus, commonly known as the cityscape rose, is a flowering plant native to urban environments.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, multi-colored petals that reflect the diversity of city life. The plant thrives in polluted air and compacted soils, making it a resilient species for urban landscaping. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, adapting to limited sunlight and space.

Urbananthus pluriseriatus plays a role in improving air quality and providing greenery in densely populated areas.
