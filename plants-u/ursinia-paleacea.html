Ursinia paleacea

Ursinia paleacea, commonly known as the paleaceas or the desert star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ursinia paleacea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its resilience and beauty.
