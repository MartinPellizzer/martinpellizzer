Utricularia × ochroleuca

Utricularia × ochroleuca, commonly known as the hybrid water whorl.

This plant is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Utricularia foliosa and Utricularia aurea, combining traits from both parent species. It is a carnivorous plant that thrives in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, often found in slow-moving freshwater habitats. Its botanical features include small, delicate leaves with specialized bladders for trapping prey.

The plant exhibits a unique morphology with whorled arrangements of leaves and a slender, branching stem structure.
