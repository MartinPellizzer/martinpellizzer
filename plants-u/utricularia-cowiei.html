Utricularia cowiei

Utricularia cowiei, commonly known as the African bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, floating bladders that trap and digest small aquatic organisms. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats. Its leaves are thin and ribbon-like, with the bladders attached to them.
