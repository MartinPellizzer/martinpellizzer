Uragoga Australis

Uragoga australis is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the southern regions of the planet. It thrives in harsh, arid environments where few other species can survive. Its unique adaptations allow it to extract moisture from the air and store it in specialized structures. The plant is known for its striking, bioluminescent flowers that glow faintly at night. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, Uragoga australis is considered a critically endangered species.
