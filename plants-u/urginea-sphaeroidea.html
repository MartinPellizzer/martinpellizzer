Urginea sphaeroidea

Urginea sphaeroidea, commonly known as the globe-shaped African tulip, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a distinctive, rounded bulb that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy coating.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
