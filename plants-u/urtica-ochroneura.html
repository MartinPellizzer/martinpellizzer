Urtica ochroneura

Urtica ochroneura, commonly known as the orange nettle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various stinging nettles. The plant is characterized by its hairy, serrated leaves and small greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.
