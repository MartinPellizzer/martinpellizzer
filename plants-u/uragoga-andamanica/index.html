Uragoga Andamanica

The uragoga andamanica is a rare flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands. It is known for its striking violet petals and unique leaf patterns. This species thrives in humid, tropical climates with rich soil. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this botanical treasure.
