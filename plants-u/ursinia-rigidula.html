Ursinia rigidula

Ursinia rigidula, commonly known as the rigid burweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. Its rigid, spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers make it easily identifiable.

Ursinia rigidula is also noted for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in local remedies.
