Umbilicus vulgaris subsp. patens

Umbilicus vulgaris subsp.

patens, commonly known as the common umbilicaria, is a species of lichen that belongs to the family Umbilicariaceae. It is characterized by its distinctive, flattened, disc-like thallus with a central umbilical depression.

The plant typically grows on rocks, soil, or decaying wood in temperate and boreal regions. It is a foliose lichen, meaning it has leaf-like structures, and it often forms dense mats.

The thallus is usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it contains various secondary metabolites that contribute to its ecological role.
