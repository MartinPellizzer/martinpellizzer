Urostigma xanthophyllum var. ovatocordatum

Urostigma xanthophyllum var. ovatocordatum, commonly known as the broad-heart bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette, and its stems are erect with a tendency to branch towards the top. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The variety ovatocordatum is distinguished by its broader, heart-shaped leaves compared to the typical form.
