Urginea wightiana

Urginea wightiana, commonly known as the giant tiger lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features large, showy flowers with bright orange petals and a distinctive red throat, resembling a tiger's eye. The plant produces a tall, erect stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, making it a dramatic addition to gardens. Its bulbs are fleshy and store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
