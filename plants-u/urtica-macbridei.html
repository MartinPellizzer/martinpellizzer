Urtica macbridei

Urtica macbridei, commonly known as the MacBride stinging nettle, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its stems are covered in fine hairs that can cause an irritating rash upon contact.

Urtica macbridei is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and stream banks.
