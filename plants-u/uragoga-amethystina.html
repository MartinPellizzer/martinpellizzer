Uragoga amethystina

Uragoga amethystina, commonly known as the amethystina orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and violet flowers, which bloom in clusters along its elongated stems. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. Uragoga amethystina thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique coloration and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
