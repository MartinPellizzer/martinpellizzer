Uragoga coriacea

Uragoga coriacea, commonly known as the rough bark tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, rugged bark that provides protection against environmental stressors. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Uragoga coriacea is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
