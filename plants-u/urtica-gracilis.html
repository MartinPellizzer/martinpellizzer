Urtica gracilis

Urtica gracilis, commonly known as slender nettle, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that are covered in fine, stinging hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have serrated margins. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, which are wind-pollinated.
