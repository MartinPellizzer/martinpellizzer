Urophyllum mayumbense

Urophyllum mayumbense, commonly known as the Mayumbense orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinct lip that is often darker. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is adapted to the humid conditions of its native habitat.
