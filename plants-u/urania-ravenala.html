Urania ravenala

Urania ravenala, commonly known as the traveller's tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of long, pinnate leaves that resemble a fan. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the stem. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

The leaves are also used in traditional practices and for their fibrous material.
