Uvifera padiformis

Uvifera padiformis, commonly known as the pad-shaped liverwort, is a non-vascular plant belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its flat, pad-like thallus that forms dense mats on moist, shaded surfaces. Its simple structure and ability to thrive in low-light conditions make it an important indicator species for assessing environmental health.
