Uragoga britoguianensis

Uragoga britoguianensis, commonly known as the Britoguan mistflower, is a rare flowering plant native to the cloud forests of northern South America.

The plant has a unique ability to thrive in high humidity and partial shade, making it well adapted to its misty, mountainous habitat. Its flowers emit a faint fragrance, which attracts specific pollinators such as moths and early-flying bees.
