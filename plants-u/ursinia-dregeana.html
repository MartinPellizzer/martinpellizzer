Ursinia dregeana

Ursinia dregeana, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Ursinia dregeana is also valued for its drought tolerance and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
