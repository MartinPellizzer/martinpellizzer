Urophyllum moluccanum var. angustatum

Urophyllum moluccanum var. angustatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed urophyllum, is a flowering plant native to the Moluccas in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often found in shaded undergrowth in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
