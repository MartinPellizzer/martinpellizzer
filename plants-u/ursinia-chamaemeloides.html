Ursinia chamaemeloides

Ursinia chamaemeloides, commonly known as the Cape daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, dark brown seeds in its composite flower heads.

Ursinia chamaemeloides is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
