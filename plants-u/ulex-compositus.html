Ulex compositus

Ulex compositus, commonly known as the Scotch heath, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles and parts of western Europe.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red, spiny fruits. Its leaves are small, leathery, and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

Ulex compositus is often found in heathland and coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.
