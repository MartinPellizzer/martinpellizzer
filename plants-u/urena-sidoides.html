Urena sidoides

Urena sidoides, commonly known as the silky urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a soft, hairy texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The plant is known for its fibrous stems, which have been used in traditional crafts and textiles.
