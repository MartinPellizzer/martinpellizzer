Urostigma pyrifolium

Urostigma pyrifolium, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its bark is smooth and light gray, often exuding a milky sap when cut.

Urostigma pyrifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
