Ursopuntia textoris var. setiger

Ursopuntia textoris var. setiger, commonly known as the hairy leaf cactus, is a unique species native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and modified leaves that have a woolly texture. The plant features clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its hairy foliage helps to reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

This variety is notable for its distinctive appearance and adaptability to extreme conditions.
