Urostigma platypodum f. ellipticum

Urostigma platypodum f. ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic flat-leaf sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically flat and arranged in a dense, grass-like manner. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the sedge family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.

This variety is noted for its distinct leaf shape and adaptability to wetland environments.
