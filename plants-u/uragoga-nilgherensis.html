Uragoga nilgherensis

Uragoga nilgherensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri uragoga, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are elliptical and have a slightly hairy texture.

Uragoga nilgherensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
