Uranodactylus silaifolius

Uranodactylus silaifolius, commonly known as the silaifolius fern, is a unique plant species characterized by its intricate frond structure.

It features a tall, slender stem with compound leaves that are divided into numerous narrow, linear segments. The plant exhibits a delicate, ethereal appearance, often growing in shaded, moist environments. Its reproductive structures are concealed within specialized leaf clusters, typical of many ferns.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
