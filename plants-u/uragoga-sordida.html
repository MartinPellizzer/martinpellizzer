Uragoga sordida

Uragoga sordida, commonly known as the gloomy uragoga, is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to the misty highlands of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on the forest floor or along the bases of ancient trees.
