Ursinia macropoda

Ursinia macropoda, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ursinia macropoda is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
