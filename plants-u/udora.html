Udora

Udora, commonly known as the bluebell vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It features striking blue and purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Udora thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is a climbing plant that can be used in gardens to cover trellises or walls.
