Urophyllum talbotii

Urophyllum talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's uruphyllum, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. Urophyllum talbotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
