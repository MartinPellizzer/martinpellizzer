Urospermum picroides

Urospermum picroides, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and its yellow, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves with a rough, textured surface. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Urospermum picroides is often found in disturbed areas and can spread through its creeping root system.
