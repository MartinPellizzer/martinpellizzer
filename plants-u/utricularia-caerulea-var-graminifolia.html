Utricularia caerulea var. graminifolia

Utricularia caerulea var. graminifolia, commonly known as the blue humped bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and specialized bladder-like structures used to trap aquatic prey. The plant is adapted to nutrient-poor environments, relying on captured prey for essential nutrients.
