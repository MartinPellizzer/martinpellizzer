Uragoga foliosa

Uragoga foliosa, commonly known as the leafy fern, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to two meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity to flourish. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Uragoga foliosa plays an important ecological role by providing habitat for various small organisms.
