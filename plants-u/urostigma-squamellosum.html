Urostigma squamellosum

Urostigma squamellosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it its common name. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-yellow and appear in clusters.

Urostigma squamellosum is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
