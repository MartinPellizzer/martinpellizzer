Uragoga hirta

Uragoga hirta, commonly known as the hairy uragoga, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverbanks. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in greenish-white or yellowish hues.

Uragoga hirta is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
