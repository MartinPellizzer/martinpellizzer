Urachne lanata

Urachne lanata, commonly known as the woolly urachne, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Urachne lanata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
