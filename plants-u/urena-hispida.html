Urena hispida

Urena hispida, commonly known as hairy urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is an annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually ovate or lanceolate in shape. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in spikes and bloom during the warm seasons.

Urena hispida is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
