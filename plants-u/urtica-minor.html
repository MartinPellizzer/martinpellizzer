Urtica minor

Urtica minor, commonly known as lesser nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with fine hairs that can cause skin irritation upon contact. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in spikes and are wind-pollinated.

Urtica minor is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
