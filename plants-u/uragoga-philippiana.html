Uragoga philippiana

Uragoga philippiana, commonly known as the Philippine water lily, is a semi-aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and marshes. The plant has broad, floating leaves that provide shade and support aquatic life. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

Uragoga philippiana plays an important role in maintaining water quality and habitat diversity in its native ecosystems.
