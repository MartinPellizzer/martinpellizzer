Uncarina decaryi

Uncarina decaryi, commonly known as the Decary's burr.

It is a climbing shrub native to Madagascar, characterized by its woody stems and distinctive, spiny fruits. The plant produces large, orbicular berries that contain several seeds enclosed in a fibrous, persistent pericarp. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

Uncarina decaryi is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments and is often found in forested areas.
