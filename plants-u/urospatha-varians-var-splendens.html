Urospatha varians var. splendens

Urospatha varians var. splendens, commonly known as the splendent arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 2 meters in height, featuring broad, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its rhizomatous root system is notable for its starchy, edible roots, which are used in traditional cuisines.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
