Urginea chlorantha

Urginea chlorantha, commonly known as the blue star lily, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring, often with a star-like shape. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem and broad, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
