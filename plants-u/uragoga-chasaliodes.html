Uragoga chasaliodes

Uragoga chasaliodes, commonly known as the Chasaliode orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with a distinctive inflorescence that bears showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. The orchid has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil of its habitat.
