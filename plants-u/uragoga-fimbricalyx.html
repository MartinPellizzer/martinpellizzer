Uragoga fimbricalyx

Uragoga fimbricalyx, commonly known as the featherleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that have a feathery appearance due to their fine, hair-like margins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Uragoga fimbricalyx is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
