Urtica celebica

Urtica celebica, commonly known as the Celebes nettle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes many stinging nettle species. The plant is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, round fruits.

Urtica celebica is often found in moist, shaded areas and can be used in traditional medicine.
