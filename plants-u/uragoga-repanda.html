Uragoga repanda

Uragoga repanda, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant red flowers with a distinctive spider-like appearance, which bloom in late summer. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often a deep green color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies.

Uragoga repanda is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in some Asian traditions.
