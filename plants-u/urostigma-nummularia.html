Urostigma nummularia

Urostigma nummularia, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of silver-gray, spoon-shaped leaves that reflect sunlight. The plant produces yellow flowers with prominent central disks, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Urostigma nummularia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
