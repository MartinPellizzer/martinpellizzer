Unigenes humifusa

Unigenes humifusa, commonly known as the groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers that attract pollinators. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, achene-like seeds.

Unigenes humifusa is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
