Uromorus philippinensis

Uromorus philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine flatback turtle, is a species of sea turtle found in the coastal waters of the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dark, uniform coloration with no distinct markings. The species has a flattened, streamlined shell that aids in its aquatic lifestyle. It is primarily a herbivorous species, feeding on seagrass and algae.

Due to habitat loss and poaching, it is listed as endangered by the IUCN.
