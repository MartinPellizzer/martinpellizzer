Urostigma eugeniifolium

Urostigma eugeniifolium, commonly known as the golden star grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of South America.

It features slender, erect stems that often grow in dense clusters, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in loose panicles, which bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Urostigma eugeniifolium is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
