Umtiza listeriana

Umtiza listeriana, commonly known as the Lister's umtiza, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.
