Ulex genistoides f. willkommii

Ulex genistoides f. willkommii, commonly known as the Willkomm's buckthorn, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in early spring. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically valuable species in its native range.
