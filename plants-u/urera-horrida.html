Urera horrida

Urera horrida, commonly known as the rough urera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, lowland forests. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are woody capsules that release seeds when mature.

Urera horrida is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
