Utricularia reticulata var. stricticaulis

Utricularia reticulata var. stricticaulis, commonly known as the net leaf bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its intricate, branched structure and small, translucent bladders used to trap prey. The plant typically grows in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, such as marshes and ponds. Its leaves are often arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, delicate flowers.
