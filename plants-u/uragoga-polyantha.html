Uragoga polyantha

Uragoga polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a woody stem, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

Uragoga polyantha is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
