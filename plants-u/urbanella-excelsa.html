Urbanella excelsa

Urbanella excelsa, commonly known as the noble lily, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Urbanella excelsa is also valued for its fragrance and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
