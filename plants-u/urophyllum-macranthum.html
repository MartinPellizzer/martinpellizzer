Urophyllum macranthum

Urophyllum macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered urophyllum, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, large, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Urophyllum macranthum is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.
