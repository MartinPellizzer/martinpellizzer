Ulex ianthocladus var. calycotomoides

Ulex ianthocladus var. calycotomoides, commonly known as the yellow thornbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth and yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
