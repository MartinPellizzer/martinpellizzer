Uvaria lobbiana

Uvaria lobbiana, commonly known as the white uvaria, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, simple, and often elliptical leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
