Uragoga guerzeensis f. puberula

This particular form is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage, which distinguishes it from other forms of the species. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a slightly waxy texture that helps reduce water loss in its arid habitat. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, and it thrives in well-drained soils with minimal water.
