Uvaria reticulata

Uvaria reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined uvaria, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits that are edible.

Uvaria reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
