Urostigma platypodum f. minor

Urostigma platypodum f. minor, commonly known as the lesser flatpod daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence with yellow disc flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has small, narrow leaves and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in cultivation.
