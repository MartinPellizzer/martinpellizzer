Urachne obtusa

It is native to southern Africa, where it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a spherical, fleshy berry that ripens to a bright orange color.

Urachne obtusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
