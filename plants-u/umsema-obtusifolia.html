Umsema obtusifolia

Umsema obtusifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are often velvety to the touch. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight, making it a popular houseplant.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and relatively easy to care for in indoor environments.
