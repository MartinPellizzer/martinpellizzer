Urena pedersenii

Urena pedersenii, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Urena pedersenii is known for its milky sap, which can be toxic if ingested.
