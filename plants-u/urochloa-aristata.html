Urochloa aristata

Urochloa aristata, commonly known as silverleaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and its tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Urochloa aristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
