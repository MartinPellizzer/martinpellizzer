Urostigma saussureanum

Urostigma saussureanum, commonly known as the Himalayan false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb with a basal rosette of leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has a fibrous root system and is adapted to cold, mountainous environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at elevations ranging from 2,500 to 5,000 meters.
