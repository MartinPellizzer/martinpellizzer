Unona greveana

Unona greveana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating.

Unona greveana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
