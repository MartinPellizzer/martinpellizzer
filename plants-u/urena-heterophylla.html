Urena heterophylla

Urena heterophylla, commonly known as the variable urena, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, with leaves that can be either simple or compound depending on the environment. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Urena heterophylla is known for its adaptability and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
