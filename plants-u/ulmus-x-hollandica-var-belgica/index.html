Ulmus × Hollandica Var. Belgica

The ulmus × hollandica var. belgica is a unique hybrid elm known for its hardiness and ornamental appeal. Native to northern europe, this variety is a cross between the wych elm and the hollandica elm. it features a distinctive weeping form that adds visual interest to gardens and urban landscapes. its ability to thrive in harsh conditions makes it a popular choice for street trees and parks. despite its beauty, it faces challenges from diseases that have impacted many elm species.
