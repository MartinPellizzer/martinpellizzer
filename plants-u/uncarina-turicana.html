Uncarina turicana

Uncarina turicana, commonly known as the Turcian burr.

It is a climbing shrub belonging to the family Apocynaceae, characterized by its woody stems and thorny tendrils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by distinctive, spiny fruit capsules. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a rough, hairy texture.

Uncarina turicana is native to tropical regions of South America and is often found in humid, forested environments.
