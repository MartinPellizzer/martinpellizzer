Urospatha meyeri

Urospatha meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer's sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, evergreen fronds that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

Urospatha meyeri plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for small organisms in its native ecosystems.
