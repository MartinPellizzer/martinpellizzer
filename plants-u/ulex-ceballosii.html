Ulex ceballosii

Ulex ceballosii, commonly known as the Ceballos's buckthorn, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, often at higher elevations. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Ulex ceballosii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant seasonal display.
