Unxia anemonifolia

Unxia anemonifolia, commonly known as the Chinese anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that resemble those of the anemone, hence its common name. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, woolly texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Unxia anemonifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.
