Utricularia stellaris var. inflexa

Utricularia stellaris var. inflexa, commonly known as the star bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and specialized bladder-like structures used to trap prey. The plant typically grows in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, such as marshes and shallow water bodies. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale in color, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.

The varietal form *inflexa* is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and growth habits compared to other varieties of *Utricularia stellaris*.
