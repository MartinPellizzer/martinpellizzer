Uragoga domingensis var. fuertesii

Uragoga domingensis var. fuertesii, commonly known as the Fuertesii Uragoga, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
