Urospatha maculata

Urospatha maculata, commonly known as the spotted fern, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant features a creeping rhizome with sporangia that are clustered in sori on the underside of the leaves. Its foliage is often marked with small, dark spots, giving it its common name.

Urospatha maculata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
