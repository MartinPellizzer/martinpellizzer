Uterveria breynia

Uterveria breynia, commonly known as the "bark tree," is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has a woody stem with a distinctive, bark-like appearance. It thrives in arid and coastal environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.

Uterveria breynia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
