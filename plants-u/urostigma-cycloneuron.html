Urostigma cycloneuron

Urostigma cycloneuron, commonly known as the cycloneuron grass, is a perennial herb native to certain regions of Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical structure makes it suitable for use in erosion control and as a forage plant for livestock.
