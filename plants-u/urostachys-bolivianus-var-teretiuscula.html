Urostachys bolivianus var. teretiuscula

Urostachys bolivianus var. teretiuscula, commonly known as the slender leaved stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to South America.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
