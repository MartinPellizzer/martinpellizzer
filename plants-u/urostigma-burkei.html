Urostigma burkei

Urostigma burkei, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, red berries that contain seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils.

Urostigma burkei is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
