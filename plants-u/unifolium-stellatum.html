Unifolium stellatum

Unifolium stellatum, commonly known as starflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in a compact, low-growing habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Botanically, it belongs to the genus *Packera* in the family Asteraceae, and its flowers are typically arranged in a single, showy head.
