Urtica sikokiana

Urtica sikokiana, commonly known as the African nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of stinging nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and fine, stinging hairs on both sides. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.

Urtica sikokiana is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional African medicine for various ailments.
