Urginea gracilis

Urginea gracilis, commonly known as the slender African giant lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a large, elongated bulb that stores nutrients, supporting its growth in arid environments. The plant produces tall, narrow leaves that can reach several feet in height, often with a bluish-gray hue. Its most notable feature is its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Urginea gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun.
