Ursinia apiculata

Ursinia apiculata, commonly known as the Cape ironwood, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and often covered in fine hairs.

Ursinia apiculata is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in harsh, coastal conditions.
