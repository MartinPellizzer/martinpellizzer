Utricularia stellaris var. filiformis

Utricularia stellaris var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender star bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to aquatic and semi-aquatic environments in Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, thread-like leaves that are often submerged, with small, bladder-like structures used to trap prey. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater, requiring high humidity and good water quality.

Its unique morphology and carnivorous habits make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
