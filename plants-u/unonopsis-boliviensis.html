Unonopsis boliviensis

Unonopsis boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean region of Bolivia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that typically display vibrant yellow or orange hues. The plant grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, high-altitude environments. Its leaves are elongated and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Unonopsis boliviensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
