Urostigma dekdekena

Urostigma dekdekena, commonly known as the Kudu tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The tree produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry woodlands and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

The plant also plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
