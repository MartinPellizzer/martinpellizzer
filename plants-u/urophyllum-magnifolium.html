Urophyllum magnifolium

Urophyllum magnifolium, commonly known as the grandleaf beauty, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of showy, white and pink flowers with a subtle fragrance, blooming during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
