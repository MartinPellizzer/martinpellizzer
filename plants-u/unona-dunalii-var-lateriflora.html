Unona dunalii var. lateriflora

Unona dunalii var. lateriflora, commonly known as the coastal dune morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dune ecosystems. It has a trailing growth habit and produces capsule fruits containing numerous small seeds.

This species is adapted to salt spray and wind exposure, making it an important pioneer species in dune stabilization.
