Urostigma eugenioides

Urostigma eugenioides, commonly known as the broadleaf tree, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils.
