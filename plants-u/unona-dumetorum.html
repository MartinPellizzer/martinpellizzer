Unona dumetorum

Unona dumetorum, commonly known as devil's lettuce, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the dandelion family (Compositae) and is characterized by its rosette of dark green, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its psychoactive properties, it is considered a hallucinogenic plant and is classified as a controlled substance in many regions.
