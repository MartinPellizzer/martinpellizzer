Unedo edulis

Unedo edulis, commonly known as the edible starfruit, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Caricaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which turns yellow when ripe. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly pubescent underside.

The fruit is not only ornamental but also valued for its sweet, tangy flavor and nutritional content.
