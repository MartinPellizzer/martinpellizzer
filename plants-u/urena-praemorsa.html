Urena praemorsa

Urena praemorsa, commonly known as the black urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, with five petals. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Urena praemorsa is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas.
