Uragoga puberulina

Uragoga puberulina, commonly known as the hairy uragoga, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, pubescent leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Uragoga puberulina is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in traditional medicine.
