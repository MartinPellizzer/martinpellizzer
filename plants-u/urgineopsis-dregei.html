Urgineopsis dregei

Urgineopsis dregei, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of pink and white.
