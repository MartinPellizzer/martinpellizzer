Urostachys limidus

Urostachys limidus, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Urostachys limidus is well-suited to sandy soils and can thrive in environments with minimal rainfall.
