Utricularia dregei

Utricularia dregei, commonly known as the Cape bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, aquatic plant that typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats. The plant features tiny bladders that trap small aquatic organisms, which it uses as a food source. Its leaves are thin and ribbon-like, adapted for underwater life.

Utricularia dregei is known for its rapid growth and unique trapping mechanism, making it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
