Unona monilifera

Unona monilifera, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like inflorescences, which consist of clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Unona monilifera is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species in regions with limited water availability.
