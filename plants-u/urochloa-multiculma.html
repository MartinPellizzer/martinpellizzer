Urochloa multiculma

Urochloa multiculma, commonly known as silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and silver-gray foliage due to the presence of fine pubescence. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in erosion control and forage production.

Urochloa multiculma is also valued for its ornamental appeal in landscaping.
