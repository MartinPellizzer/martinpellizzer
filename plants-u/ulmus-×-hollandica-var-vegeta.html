Ulmus × hollandica var. vegeta

Ulmus × hollandica var. vegeta, commonly known as the Clonal Elm, is a hybrid species of elm native to Europe.

It is a small, compact tree with a dense, rounded crown and smooth, gray bark. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a slightly serrated margin and a dark green upper surface. It is highly resistant to Dutch elm disease, making it a valuable species for urban landscaping.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
