Uvularia sinensis

Uvularia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.
