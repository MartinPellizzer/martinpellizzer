Uvifera brasiliensis

Uvifera brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a leathery texture, while its bark is smooth and grayish in appearance.

Uvifera brasiliensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
