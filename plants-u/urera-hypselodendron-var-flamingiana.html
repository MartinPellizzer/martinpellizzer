Urera hypselodendron var. flamingiana

Urera hypselodendron var. flamingiana, commonly known as the Flame Urera, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange foliage, which gives it its dramatic appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in panicles.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates for its visual appeal.
