Ulina germanica

Ulina germanica, commonly known as the German violet, is a flowering plant native to Central and Southern Europe.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Ulina germanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
