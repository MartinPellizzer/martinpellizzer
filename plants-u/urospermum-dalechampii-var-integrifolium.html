Urospermum dalechampii var. integrifolium

Urospermum dalechampii var. integrifolium, commonly known as the smoothleaf star-thistle, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have smooth margins, which distinguish it from other varieties. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This plant is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads.
