Urena capitata var. rotundifolia

Urena capitata var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often rounded at the base. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, rounded clusters at the ends of branches. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
