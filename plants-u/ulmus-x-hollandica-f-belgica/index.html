Ulmus × Hollandica F. Belgica

The ulmus × hollandica f. belgica is a unique hybrid elm known for its ornamental value and hardiness. It is a cross between the wych elm and the hollandica elm, resulting in a tree with distinctive features. This variety is prized for its compact size and ability to thrive in urban environments. Its heart-shaped leaves and smooth bark make it a popular choice for landscaping. Despite its beauty, it faces challenges from diseases that affect elm species.
