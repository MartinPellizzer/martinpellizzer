Urophyllum oresitrophum

Urophyllum oresitrophum, commonly known as the sticky bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, glandular leaves that secrete a sticky substance, which can trap insects. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. Its growth habit is typically upright, with multiple stems arising from the base.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
