Ulearum sagittatum var. sagittatum

Ulearum sagittatum var. sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Its rapid growth makes it a popular choice for aquatic gardens and water feature landscapes.
