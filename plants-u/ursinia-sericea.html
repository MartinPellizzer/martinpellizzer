Ursinia sericea

Ursinia sericea, commonly known as the silver leaf ursinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ursinia sericea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
