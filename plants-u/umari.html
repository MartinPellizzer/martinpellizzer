Umari

Umari, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners. Umari is known for its ability to bloom year-round under optimal conditions.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a versatile choice for both decorative and therapeutic purposes.
