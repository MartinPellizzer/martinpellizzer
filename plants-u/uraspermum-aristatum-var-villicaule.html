Uraspermum aristatum var. villicaule

Uraspermum aristatum var. villicaule, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are surrounded by green bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is native to certain regions of North America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
