Urochloa ciliatissima

Urochloa ciliatissima, commonly known as slender horsegram, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves with finely serrated margins. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers in spike-like clusters, which are followed by small, triangular seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used for forage and soil stabilization.

Urochloa ciliatissima is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
