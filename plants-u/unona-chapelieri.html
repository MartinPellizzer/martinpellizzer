Unona chapelieri

Unona chapelieri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Unona chapelieri reproduces through both seeds and vegetative propagation, making it adaptable for cultivation in various settings.
