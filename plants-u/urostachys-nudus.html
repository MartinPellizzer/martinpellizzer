Urostachys nudus

Urostachys nudus, commonly known as the naked-stemmed grass.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often leafless or nearly so, giving it its common name. Its small, spike-like inflorescence consists of numerous tiny, spikelet-like flowers.

Urostachys nudus is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments.
