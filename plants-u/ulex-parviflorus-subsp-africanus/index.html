Ulex Parviflorus Subsp. Africanus

Ulex parviflorus subsp. africanus is a unique plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its distinctive yellow flowers and spiny stems. This subspecies thrives in sandy and well-drained soils. It plays an important role in local ecosystems and traditional medicine. Its hardy nature makes it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
