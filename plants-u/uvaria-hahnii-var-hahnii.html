Uvaria hahnii var. hahnii

Uvaria hahnii var. hahnii, commonly known as the small-flowered uvaria, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that turns red when mature.

Uvaria hahnii var. hahnii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid climates.
