Urophyllum dewevrei

Urophyllum dewevrei, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct, aromatic scent. Its flowers emit a strong fragrance that attracts nocturnal pollinators such as moths.

Urophyllum dewevrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique blooming habits.
