Unona odorata

Unona odorata, commonly known as the fragrant wood or sweetwood tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant leaves and bark, which emit a distinctive scent. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree with a dense canopy and smooth, grayish bark. Its leaves are usually simple, alternate, and have a waxy texture.

Unona odorata is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
