Uragoga hirsuticalyx

Uragoga hirsuticalyx, commonly known as the hairy-leaf uragoga, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are covered in fine, hairy textures, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its botanical structure includes a woody stem with thick, corky bark.

Uragoga hirsuticalyx is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
