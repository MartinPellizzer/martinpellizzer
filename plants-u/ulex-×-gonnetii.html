Ulex × gonnetii

Ulex × gonnetii, commonly known as the Scotch broom, is a hybrid shrub native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and woodland areas.

Ulex × gonnetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in hedgerows.
