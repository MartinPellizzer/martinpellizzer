Unona chloroxantha

Unona chloroxantha, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in clumps, often in well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
