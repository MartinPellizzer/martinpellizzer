Umbilicus winkleri

Umbilicus winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler's umbilicaria, is a species of lichen belonging to the family Umbilicariaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, disk-like thallus with a central umbilical depression, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows on moist, shaded rocks or soil in temperate regions. Its vegetative body is composed of a symbiotic association between a fungal partner and a photosynthetic alga.

Umbilicus winkleri is often found in forested areas and is an indicator species for certain environmental conditions.
