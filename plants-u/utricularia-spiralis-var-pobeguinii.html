Utricularia spiralis var. pobeguinii

It is characterized by its small, spirally arranged leaves that form a rosette structure. The plant produces tiny, bladder-like traps that capture and digest small aquatic organisms. It thrives in nutrient-poor, freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams.
