Urostigma liebmannianum

Urostigma liebmannianum, commonly known as the golden candelabra, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, branched inflorescence that resembles a candelabra, with clusters of golden-yellow flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near rivers. Its foliage consists of large, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy.

Urostigma liebmannianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
