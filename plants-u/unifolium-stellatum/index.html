Unifolium Stellatum

Unifolium stellatum is a rare and striking plant known for its unique star-shaped foliage. Found primarily in arid and semi-arid regions, it thrives in harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are arranged in a spiral pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and often overlooked by botanists. This plant holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitat.
