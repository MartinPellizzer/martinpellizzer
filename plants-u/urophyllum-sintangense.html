Urophyllum sintangense

Urophyllum sintangense, commonly known as the Sintang flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has elliptical or ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm, humid climates.

Urophyllum sintangense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
