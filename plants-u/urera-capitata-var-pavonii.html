Urera capitata var. pavonii

Urera capitata var. pavonii, commonly known as the pink urera, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural gardens.
