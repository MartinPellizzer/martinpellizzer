Umbilicus linearifolius

Umbilicus linearifolius, commonly known as the slender-leafed umbilicus, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
