Urostigma ovatum

Urostigma ovatum, commonly known as the white sandflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Urostigma ovatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
