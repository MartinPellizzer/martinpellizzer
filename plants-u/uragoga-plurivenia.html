Uragoga plurivenia

Uragoga plurivenia, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover.
