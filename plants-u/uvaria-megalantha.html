Uvaria megalantha

Uvaria megalantha, commonly known as the large-fruited uvaria, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes cashew and mango. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature.

Uvaria megalantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
