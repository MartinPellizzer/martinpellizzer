Urochloa venosa

Urochloa venosa, commonly known as slender grass or slender blady grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, blade-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and is often used for forage in livestock grazing.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
