Ussuria

Ussuria, commonly known as the Siberian pine, is a coniferous tree native to the Russian Far East and parts of northeastern China.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The tree produces cones that contain winged seeds, which are dispersed by the wind. Ussuria thrives in cold climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.

Its wood is valued for its durability and resistance to decay.
