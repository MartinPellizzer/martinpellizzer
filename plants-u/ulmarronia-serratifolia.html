Ulmarronia serratifolia

Ulmarronia serratifolia, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers. It is often found in dry woodland and savanna habitats.

Ulmarronia serratifolia is valued for its durability and is used in construction and traditional medicine.
