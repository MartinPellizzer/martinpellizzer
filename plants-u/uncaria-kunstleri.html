Uncaria kunstleri

Uncaria kunstleri, commonly known as the African arrowroot or wild coffee.

It is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa, characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries containing elliptical seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like coffee and gardenia.
