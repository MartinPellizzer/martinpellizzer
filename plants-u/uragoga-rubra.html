Uragoga rubra

Uragoga rubra, commonly known as the red-stemmed beauty, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a glossy green surface. This plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
