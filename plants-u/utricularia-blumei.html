Utricularia blumei

Utricularia blumei, commonly known as the Blume's bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and specialized bladder-like structures used to trap prey. The plant typically grows in water or very moist soil, often in shaded environments. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually green or yellowish in color.
