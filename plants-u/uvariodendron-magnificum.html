Uvariodendron magnificum

Uvariodendron magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that are arranged in a feathery pattern. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Uvariodendron magnificum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
