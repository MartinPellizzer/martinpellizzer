Urostachys linifolius var. gracilis

Urostachys linifolius var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaf stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
