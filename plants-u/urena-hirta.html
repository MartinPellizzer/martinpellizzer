Urena hirta

Urena hirta, commonly known as Indian velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into pods containing several seeds. Urena hirta is known for its rapid growth and is often found in disturbed soils, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
