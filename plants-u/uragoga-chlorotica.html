Uragoga chlorotica

Uragoga chlorotica, commonly known as the green-winged orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking greenish-blue petals and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated primarily by bees and other insects.

Uragoga chlorotica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
