Urostigma natalense

Urostigma natalense, commonly known as Natal umbrella tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-like shape formed by its spreading branches and dense foliage. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It produces clusters of white flowers followed by small, round fruits.

Urostigma natalense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
