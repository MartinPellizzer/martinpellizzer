Ursinia anthemoides subsp. anthemoides

Ursinia anthemoides subsp.

anthemoides, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its most striking characteristic is its clusters of bright yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
