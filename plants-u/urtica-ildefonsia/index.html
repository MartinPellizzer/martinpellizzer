Urtica Ildefonsia

Urtica ildefonsia is a fascinating plant known for its unique and often dangerous characteristics. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid and shaded environments. Its leaves are covered in stinging hairs that can cause severe irritation upon contact. Despite its harmful reputation, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. This plant has also been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
