Uvariopsis korupensis

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by berry-like fruits. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies on tropical plant diversity.
