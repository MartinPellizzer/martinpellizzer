Urena cylindrica

Urena cylindrica, commonly known as slender urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 1-2 meters tall, with slender, cylindrical stems that are often hairy. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Urena cylindrica is known for its adaptability to various soil types and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and waste lands.
