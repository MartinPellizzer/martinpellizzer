Urtica chichicaztli

Urtica chichicaztli, commonly known as the Mexican stinging nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with fine, irritating hairs that cause a skin reaction upon contact. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Urtica chichicaztli is used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
