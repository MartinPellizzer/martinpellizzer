Ulmus Campestris Subsp. Montana

Ulmus campestris subsp. montana is a rare and hardy variant of the field elm known for its adaptability to harsh environments. This subspecies thrives in rocky and arid regions where other plants struggle to survive. Its unique growth patterns and resilience make it a valuable subject for ecological studies. Despite its durability, it faces threats from disease and habitat loss. Understanding this plant offers insights into plant survival strategies in extreme conditions.
