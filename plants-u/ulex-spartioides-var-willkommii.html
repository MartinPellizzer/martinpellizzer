Ulex spartioides var. willkommii

Ulex spartioides var. willkommii, commonly known as the Spanish heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or arid environments. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are needle-like and evergreen, providing year-round structure.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
