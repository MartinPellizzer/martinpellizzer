Urochloa piligera

Urochloa piligera, commonly known as slender grass or slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and panicle-like inflorescence, which gives it a delicate, weeping appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands. It is drought-tolerant and can grow in a variety of light conditions, making it adaptable to different environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping and erosion control.
