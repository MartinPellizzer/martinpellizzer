Unonopsis megalophylla

Unonopsis megalophylla, commonly known as the giant fern, is a strikingly large fern species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its expansive, feather-like fronds that can reach impressive lengths, often exceeding two meters in height. The plant's leaves are composed of numerous pinnae, which are arranged in a highly ornate and intricate pattern. Unonopsis megalophylla thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
