Urena cana

Urena cana, commonly known as white urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 1-2 meters tall, with opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas and along roadsides.

Urena cana is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a useful plant in soil enrichment.
