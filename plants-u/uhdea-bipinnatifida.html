Uhdea bipinnatifida

Uhdea bipinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its bipinnatifid leaves, which are deeply divided into fine, feather-like segments. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands.

Uhdea bipinnatifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
