Urceolina microcrater

Urceolina microcrater, commonly known as the small crater urn.

This plant is characterized by its unique urn-shaped flowers, which are typically small and clustered in dense inflorescences. The leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions with high humidity.

The plant's botanical structure supports its role in ecological systems, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
