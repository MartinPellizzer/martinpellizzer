Ulmus campestris minor

Ulmus campestris minor, commonly known as the field elm.

It is a small to medium-sized tree native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually smooth and dark green on top. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, appearing in clusters, and it produces small, winged samaras for seed dispersal.

The tree is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its historical use in hedgerows and windbreaks.
