Urena armitiana var. spenceri

Urena armitiana var. spenceri, commonly known as Spencer's urena, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are often borne on long peduncles. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
