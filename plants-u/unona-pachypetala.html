Unona pachypetala

Unona pachypetala, commonly known as the thick-petaled orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant mix of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant has thick, fleshy petals that contribute to its distinctive appearance and attract various pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Unona pachypetala is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
