Uva cylindrica

Uva cylindrica, commonly known as the cylinder grape or cylindrical grape, is a flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to the eastern United States and is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical fruit that resembles a small grape. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.
