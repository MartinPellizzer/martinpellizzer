Urelytrum isalense

Urelytrum isalense, commonly known as the Isla Grande water lily, is a aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. Urelytrum isalense thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
