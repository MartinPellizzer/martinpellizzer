Ulmus × Hollandica F. Major

Ulmus × hollandica f. major is a unique cultivar of the field maple known for its striking, large leaves and ornamental value. This hybrid tree combines the characteristics of several elm species, resulting in a hardy and adaptable plant. It is often favored in gardens for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The foliage turns a vibrant yellow in autumn, adding seasonal interest to the landscape. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for smaller spaces and urban environments.
