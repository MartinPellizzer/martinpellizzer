Ursinia brevicaulis

Ursinia brevicaulis, commonly known as the dwarf urson, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Ursinia brevicaulis is often used in rock gardens and xeriscapes due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
