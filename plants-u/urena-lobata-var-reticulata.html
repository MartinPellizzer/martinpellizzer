Urena lobata var. reticulata

Urena lobata var. reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
