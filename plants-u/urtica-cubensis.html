Urtica cubensis

Urtica cubensis, commonly known as Cuban nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a fuzzy texture and fine stinging hairs that can cause irritation upon contact. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

Urtica cubensis is often found in moist, shady areas such as forests and along stream banks.
