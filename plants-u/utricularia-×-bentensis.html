Utricularia × bentensis

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Utricularia gibba and Utricularia aurea, combining traits from both parent species. These bladders are typically less than 1 mm in size and are equipped with a sensitive trigger to capture tiny aquatic organisms.

Utricularia × bentensis is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats, where it thrives in nutrient-poor environments.
