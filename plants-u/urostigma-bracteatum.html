Urostigma bracteatum

Urostigma bracteatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas.

Urostigma bracteatum is known for its rapid growth and ability to become a weed in cultivated fields.
