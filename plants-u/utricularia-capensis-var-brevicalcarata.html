Utricularia capensis var. brevicalcarata

Utricularia capensis var. brevicalcarata, commonly known as the Cape bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, aquatic or semi-aquatic species that thrives in shallow, nutrient-poor water environments. The plant features delicate, floating leaves with tiny bladders that trap small aquatic organisms. Its flowers are usually white or pink and are borne on slender stems.

Botanically, it is characterized by its specialized structures for nutrient acquisition and its ability to adapt to various water conditions.
