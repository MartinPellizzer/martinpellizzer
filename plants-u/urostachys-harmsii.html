Urostachys harmsii

Urostachys harmsii, commonly known as the Harms' mountain grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Urostachys harmsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
