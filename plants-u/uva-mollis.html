Uva mollis

Uva mollis, commonly known as the soft grape or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are soft and velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Uva mollis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

It is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
