Unona erecta

Unona erecta, commonly known as the upright fern, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and delicate, feather-like leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Unona erecta thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
