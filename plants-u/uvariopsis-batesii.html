Uvariopsis batesii

Uvariopsis batesii, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.

Uvariopsis batesii is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional African medicine.
