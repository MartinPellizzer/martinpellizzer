Ursinia saxatilis

Ursinia saxatilis, commonly known as the rock ursinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Ursinia saxatilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
