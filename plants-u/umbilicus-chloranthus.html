Umbilicus chloranthus

Umbilicus chloranthus, commonly known as the green umbilical plant, is a rare and unique species belonging to the Chloranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbilical-like structure at the base of its leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically broad and waxy, with a deep green coloration.

Umbilicus chloranthus is often noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
