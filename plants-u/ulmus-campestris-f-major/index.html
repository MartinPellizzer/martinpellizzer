Ulmus Campestris F. Major

Ulmus campestris f. major is a unique variant of the field elm known for its distinctive growth pattern and historical significance. This form exhibits a more open and sprawling structure compared to the typical field elm, making it a striking addition to arboreal landscapes. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in urban and rural environments alike. The plant has been cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience to environmental stressors. Its study offers insights into genetic variation and adaptation in elm species.
