Unisema rotundifolia

Unisema rotundifolia, commonly known as the roundleaf unisema, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features rounded, broad leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. The plant is native to specific regions with humid climates, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
