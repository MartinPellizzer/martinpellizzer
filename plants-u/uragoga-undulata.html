Uragoga undulata

Uragoga undulata, commonly known as the wavy leaf orchid, is a rare and exotic flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, undulating leaf patterns and delicate, fragrant blossoms that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.

Uragoga undulata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
