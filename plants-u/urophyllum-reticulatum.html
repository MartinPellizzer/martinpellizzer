Urophyllum reticulatum

Urophyllum reticulatum, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, net-like venation pattern on its leaves, which gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors including white, yellow, and green.

Urophyllum reticulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
