Urophyllum macrophyllum

Urophyllum macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf urophyllum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Urophyllum macrophyllum is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
