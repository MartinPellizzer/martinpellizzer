Uraria leptostachya

Uraria leptostachya, commonly known as small-stemmed uraria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Asia, including India, and thrives in dry, arid environments. The plant features small, trifoliate leaves and produces clusters of yellow or greenish-yellow flowers. Its seed pods are slender and often contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Uraria leptostachya is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
