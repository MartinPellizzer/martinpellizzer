Urceolina × lowii

Urceolina × lowii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant with unique botanical characteristics.

It is a cross between two species of orchids, typically Urceolina macrophylla and Urceolina lowii, resulting in a hybrid with a blend of traits from both parents. The plant is known for its striking, showy flowers that often feature vibrant colors and intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
