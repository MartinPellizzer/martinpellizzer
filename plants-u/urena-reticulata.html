Urena reticulata

Urena reticulata, commonly known as the clusterleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features palmate leaves with 5 to 7 lobes, and its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in clusters. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds.

Urena reticulata is known for its adaptability to various soil types and is often found in disturbed areas.
