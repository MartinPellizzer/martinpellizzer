Uragoga eriantha

Uragoga eriantha, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
