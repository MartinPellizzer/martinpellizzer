Urostachys nubigenus

Urostachys nubigenus, commonly known as the cloud-like sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that grow in dense clusters. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a short, stout rhizome.

Urostachys nubigenus is valued for its resilience to harsh climatic conditions and is of interest in ecological studies of mountain flora.
