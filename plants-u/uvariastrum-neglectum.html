Uvariastrum neglectum

Uvariastrum neglectum, commonly known as the slender uvaria, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental horticulture.
