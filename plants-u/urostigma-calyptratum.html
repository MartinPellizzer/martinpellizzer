Urostigma calyptratum

Urostigma calyptratum, commonly known as the yellow daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a central disk of smaller florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Urostigma calyptratum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
