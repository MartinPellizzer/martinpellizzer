Urtica glaberrima

Urtica glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of stinging nettles. The plant is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Urtica glaberrima produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in umbels.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
