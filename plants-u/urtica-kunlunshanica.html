Urtica kunlunshanica

Urtica kunlunshanica, commonly known as the Kunlun mountain nettle, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of stinging nettles. The plant is characterized by its serrated leaves and fine hairs that can cause skin irritation upon contact. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Urtica kunlunshanica is valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
