Urostachys linifolius var. tenuifolius

Urostachys linifolius var. tenuifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features compact, rosette-forming stems with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to harsh environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
