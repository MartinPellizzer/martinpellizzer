Uraria sinensis

Uraria sinensis, commonly known as Chinese uraria, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a woody stem and compound leaves that have three leaflets. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Uraria sinensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
