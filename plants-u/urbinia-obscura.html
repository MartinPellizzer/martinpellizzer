Urbinia obscura

Urbinia obscura, commonly known as the dark urbinia, is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pollinated by nocturnal insects. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its native arid environment.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Urbinia obscura is considered a conservation concern.
