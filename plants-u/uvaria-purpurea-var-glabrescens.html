Uvaria purpurea var. glabrescens

Uvaria purpurea var. glabrescens, commonly known as the purple uvaria, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle purple hue. The plant has smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
