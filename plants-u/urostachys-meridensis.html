Urostachys meridensis

Urostachys meridensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean stachys, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of small, violet or purple flowers. It is often found in open woodlands and along rocky slopes.

Urostachys meridensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
