Uraria linearis

Uraria linearis, commonly known as the slender uraria, is a leguminous plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is native to regions of Asia, including India, China, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds.

Uraria linearis is often found in open areas, disturbed soils, and along roadsides, where it thrives in full sun.
