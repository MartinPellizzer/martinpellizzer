Unona luensis

Unona luensis, commonly known as the Luensis fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to the soil while absorbing nutrients.

Unona luensis plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat for various small organisms.
