Ursinia filiformis

Ursinia filiformis, commonly known as the slender ursinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, thread-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ursinia filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
