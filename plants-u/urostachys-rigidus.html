Urostachys rigidus

Urostachys rigidus, commonly known as the rigid stachys, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern China.

It features upright, slender stems covered in dense, stiff hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, textured surface.

Urostachys rigidus is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
