Uncinia breviculmis

Uncinia breviculmis, commonly known as the short-stalked hookweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in clusters at the base of the stems.
