Urostigma clusiifolium var. acutiusculum

Urostigma clusiifolium var. acutiusculum, commonly known as the sharp-leafed clusiifolium, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features simple, opposite, and often acuminate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
