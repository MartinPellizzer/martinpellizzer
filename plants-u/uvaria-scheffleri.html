Uvaria scheffleri

Uvaria scheffleri, commonly known as the African star fruit or African uvaria, is a tropical plant belonging to the Anacardiaceae family.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy fruits that resemble star shapes. It is native to regions of Africa and thrives in humid, tropical climates.

The fruit is edible and often used in local cuisines, while the plant also has traditional medicinal uses.
