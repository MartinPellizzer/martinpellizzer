Uragoga petiolaris

Uragoga petiolaris, commonly known as the leafy vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green coloration. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its stems are flexible and covered in fine hairs, aiding in its climbing habit.

Uragoga petiolaris is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage.
