Uragoga oleoides

Uragoga oleoides, commonly known as the oil plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant produces a valuable essential oil, which is extracted from its leaves and used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy. Uragoga oleoides thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its medicinal properties.
