Utricularia nervosa var. minor

Utricularia nervosa var. minor, commonly known as the lesser whorled bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and specialized bladders used to trap prey. The plant typically grows in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, such as ponds and marshes. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-white, with a unique whorled arrangement of petals.

This variety is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor waters.
