Unona fulgens

Unona fulgens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a striking addition to tropical landscapes.
