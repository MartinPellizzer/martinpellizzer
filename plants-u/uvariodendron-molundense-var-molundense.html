Uvariodendron molundense var. molundense

Uvariodendron molundense var. molundense, commonly known as the Molundense Oleander, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree characterized by its dense, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. Its flowers are typically pink or white, and they attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
