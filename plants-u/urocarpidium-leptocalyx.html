Urocarpidium leptocalyx

Urocarpidium leptocalyx, commonly known as the slender-flowered urocarp, is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers.

It belongs to the family Urocarpaceae, which is primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as under forest canopies or in rocky crevices. The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
