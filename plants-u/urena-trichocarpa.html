Urena trichocarpa

Urena trichocarpa, commonly known as velvetleaf, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Urena trichocarpa is also known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it a common weed in many regions.
