Unona jucunda

Unona jucunda, commonly known as the happy tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its fast growth rate and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets, and it produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Unona jucunda is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for reforestation and ornamental landscaping.
