Uragoga carronis

Uragoga carronis, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a unique plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

Uragoga carronis is a perennial herb that prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
