Urachne trichotoma

Urachne trichotoma, commonly known as the hairy urachne, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Urachne trichotoma produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family.
