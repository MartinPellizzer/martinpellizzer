Urachne virescens

Urachne virescens, commonly known as the green urachne, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and has a distinctive, slender stem with small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, making it visually appealing.

Urachne virescens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
