Ulex australis subsp. australis

Ulex australis subsp.

australis, commonly known as the southern hedgehogberry, is a shrub native to southern Europe and northern Africa. It is characterized by its spiny stems and bright orange-red flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
