Uvaria sessiliflora

Uvaria sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flowered uvaria, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Uvaria sessiliflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
