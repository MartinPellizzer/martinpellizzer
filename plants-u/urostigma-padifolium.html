Urostigma padifolium

Urostigma padifolium, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

However, it is considered invasive in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive nature.
