Utricularia papilliscapa

Utricularia papilliscapa, commonly known as the hairy bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, bladder-like traps used to capture small aquatic organisms. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.
