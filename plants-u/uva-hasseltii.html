Uva hasseltii

Uva hasseltii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant blooms throughout the growing season.

Uva hasseltii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
