Urena lobata var. multifida

Urena lobata var. multifida, commonly known as the multilobe urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial shrub that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant features palmately lobed leaves with multiple divisions, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom throughout the growing season.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
