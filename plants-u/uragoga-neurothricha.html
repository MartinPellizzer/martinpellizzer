Uragoga neurothricha

Uragoga neurothricha, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive hairy leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a subtle fragrance that attracts specific pollinators.

Uragoga neurothricha is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
