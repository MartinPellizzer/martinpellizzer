Urceolaria exotica

Urceolaria exotica, commonly known as the exotic urn flower, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique urn-shaped blooms, which are typically a vibrant shade of purple or pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery coating, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care, it has become a popular choice for botanical gardens and exotic plant collections.
