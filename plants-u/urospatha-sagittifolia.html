Urospatha sagittifolia

Urospatha sagittifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped or arrowhead-shaped leaves, which are typically large and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.

Urospatha sagittifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
