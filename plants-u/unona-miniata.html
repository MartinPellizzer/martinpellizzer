Unona miniata

Unona miniata, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. Its roots are fibrous and require well-draining soil to prevent rot.

Unona miniata is prized for its ornamental value and is often grown as a houseplant.
