Uvaria ovalifolia var. acrantha

Uvaria ovalifolia var. acrantha, commonly known as the smooth uvaria, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptic leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, blue-black berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
