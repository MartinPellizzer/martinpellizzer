Uragoga umbrosa

Uragoga umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its dark green, velvety fronds.

It thrives in low-light conditions, making it ideal for shaded garden areas or indoor spaces. The plant's leaves are broad and leathery, often with a slightly curled edge that adds to its ornamental appeal. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spore-bearing capsules.

Uragoga umbrosa is valued for its ability to purify the air and create a lush, verdant atmosphere in its surroundings.
