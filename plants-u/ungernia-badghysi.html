Ungernia badghysi

Ungernia badghysi, commonly known as the Kashmir saffron.

It is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region, particularly found in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers cool, moist environments and is typically found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
