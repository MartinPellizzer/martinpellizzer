Urena scabriuscula

Urena scabriuscula, commonly known as rough urena, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by triangular, dry fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Urena scabriuscula is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
