Urena lobata var. nummularia

Urena lobata var. nummularia, commonly known as silverleaf urena, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that are densely covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
