Uragoga fockeana

Uragoga fockeana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Uragoga fockeana is also valued for its ability to thrive indoors with proper light and watering conditions.
