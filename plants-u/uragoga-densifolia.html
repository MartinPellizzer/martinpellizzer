Uragoga densifolia

Uragoga densifolia, commonly known as the denseleaf uragoga, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and compact growth habit. The leaves are typically broad and leathery, with a glossy surface that helps in water retention. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
