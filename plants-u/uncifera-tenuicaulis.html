Uncifera tenuicaulis

Uncifera tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed lily, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base, contributing to its elegant appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous habitats with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
