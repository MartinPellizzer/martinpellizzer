Uvaria polyantha

Uvaria polyantha, commonly known as the fragrant evergreen or sweet-scented uvaria, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Uvaria polyantha is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
