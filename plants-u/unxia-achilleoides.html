Unxia achilleoides

Unxia achilleoides, commonly known as the Chinese yarrow, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces numerous small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Unxia achilleoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
