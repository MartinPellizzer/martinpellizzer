Urophyllum ellipticum

Urophyllum ellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf euphorbia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features simple, elliptic leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are surrounded by colorful bracts, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

The plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
