Urophyllum porphyraceum

Urophyllum porphyraceum, commonly known as the crimson flame fern, is a striking plant characterized by its vivid red and orange foliage.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features delicate, feathery fronds that are often adorned with a waxy, porphyrin-rich coating, giving it its distinctive coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
