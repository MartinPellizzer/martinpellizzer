Ugona

Ugona, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety petals that come in a variety of colors. The plant has rosette-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. Ugona thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular indoor plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
