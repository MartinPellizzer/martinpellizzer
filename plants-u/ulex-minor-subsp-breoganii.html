Ulex minor subsp. breoganii

Ulex minor subsp.

breoganii, commonly known as the Scottish heath or lesser Scotch broom, is a flowering plant native to western Europe. It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its yellow flowers and needle-like leaves.

The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas. Ulex minor subsp.

breoganii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
