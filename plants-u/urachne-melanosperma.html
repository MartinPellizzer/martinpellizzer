Urachne melanosperma

Urachne melanosperma, commonly known as black-seeded urachne, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, greenish flowers. The plant produces distinctive seeds that are dark in color, giving it its common name. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
