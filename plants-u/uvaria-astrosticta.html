Uvaria astrosticta

Uvaria astrosticta, commonly known as the star-streaked uvaria, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and produces small, white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are fleshy and contain multiple seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Uvaria astrosticta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some pharmacological significance in local herbal practices.
