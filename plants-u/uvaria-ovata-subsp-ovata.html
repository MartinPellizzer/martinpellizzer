Uvaria ovata subsp. ovata

Uvaria ovata subsp.

ovata, commonly known as the smooth uvaria, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature. The fruits are edible and have a sweet, aromatic flavor, making it a valuable species in local cuisines.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along riverbanks.
