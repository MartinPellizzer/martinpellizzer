Uniyala malabarica

Uniyala malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar starfruit, is a tropical shrub native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits, which are typically yellow or orange when ripe. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is edible, with a sweet and tart flavor, and is often used in local cuisines.
