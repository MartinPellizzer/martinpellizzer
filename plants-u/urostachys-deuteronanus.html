Urostachys deuteronanus

Urostachys deuteronanus, commonly known as the narrowleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery bloom. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
