Ursinia crithmoides

Ursinia crithmoides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. It has linear, hairy leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Ursinia crithmoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
