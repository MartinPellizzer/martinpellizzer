Urginea ecklonii

Urginea ecklonii, commonly known as the African giant lily, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces large, showy flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy during the winter months. Its bulbs can reach significant sizes, making them a popular subject for horticultural study.

The species is also noted for its ability to withstand drought conditions once established.
