Uragoga cyanosperma

Uragoga cyanosperma, commonly known as the blue-spore fern, is a unique vascular plant characterized by its striking blue pigmentation in its spores.

Its spores are released in a distinctive blue hue, which is due to the presence of a specific pigment called cyanospermine.

Uragoga cyanosperma thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
