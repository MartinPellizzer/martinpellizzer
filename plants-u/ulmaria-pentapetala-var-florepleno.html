Ulmaria pentapetala var. florepleno

Ulmaria pentapetala var. florepleno, commonly known as the full-flowered greater pogonia, is a rare and delicate orchid native to specific regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and marshes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat requirements, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
