Urbananthus

Urbananthus, commonly known as the city flower, is a hybrid plant developed to thrive in urban environments.

It is characterized by its hardy nature and ability to withstand pollution and compacted soil. The plant typically features vibrant, showy blooms that come in a variety of colors. It is often used in landscaping to beautify city spaces and improve air quality.

Due to its adaptability, Urbananthus has become a symbol of resilience in urban ecosystems.
