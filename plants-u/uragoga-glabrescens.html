Uragoga glabrescens

Uragoga glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth uragoga, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along riverbanks. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Uragoga glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
