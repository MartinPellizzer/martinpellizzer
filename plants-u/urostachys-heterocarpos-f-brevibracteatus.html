Urostachys heterocarpos f. brevibracteatus

Urostachys heterocarpos f. brevibracteatus, commonly known as the short-bracted sandverbena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The inflorescence consists of dense clusters of small, pink to reddish flowers. This variant is distinguished by its reduced bracts, which are shorter and more subtle compared to the typical form.

It is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
