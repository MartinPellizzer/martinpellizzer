Urostigma abutilifolium

Urostigma abutilifolium, commonly known as the silky leaf tree, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its soft, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
