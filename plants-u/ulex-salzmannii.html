Ulex salzmannii

Ulex salzmannii, commonly known as the Salzmann's burr, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces distinctive spiny, cone-like fruits known as burrs. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its leaves are evergreen, narrow, and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Ulex salzmannii is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
