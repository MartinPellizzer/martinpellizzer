Ulex europaeus subsp. gallii

Ulex europaeus subsp.

gallii, commonly known as the common goat's-rush, is a flowering plant in the family Fabaceae. It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny, upright stems and small, pea-like flowers.

The plant produces distinctive seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in coastal areas and sandy habitats.
