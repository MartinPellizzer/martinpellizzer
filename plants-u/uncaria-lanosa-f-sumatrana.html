Uncaria lanosa f. sumatrana

Uncaria lanosa f. sumatrana, commonly known as the hairy cat's claw.

This plant is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant's hairy texture is due to its dense pubescence, which gives it the common name.
