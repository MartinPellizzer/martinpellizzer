Uterveria

Uterveria, commonly known as the sacred tree of the Yoruba people, is a tropical evergreen tree native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and broad, glossy leaves that emit a strong, aromatic scent. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, with its leaves and bark used in traditional healing practices. Uterveria thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its cultural significance extends beyond its botanical features, playing a vital role in spiritual and healing rituals.
