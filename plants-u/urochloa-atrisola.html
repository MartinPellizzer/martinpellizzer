Urochloa atrisola

Urochloa atrisola, commonly known as blackleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Urochloa atrisola is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in erosion control in suitable climates.
