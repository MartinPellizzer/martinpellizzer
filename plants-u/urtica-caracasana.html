Urtica caracasana

Urtica caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas nettle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of stinging nettles. The plant is characterized by its opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, irritating hairs. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters.

Urtica caracasana is often found in moist, shaded areas and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
