Urena lobata var. yunnanensis

Urena lobata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan urena, is a variant of the Urena genus native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves with serrated edges. The flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
