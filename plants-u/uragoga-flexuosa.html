Uragoga flexuosa

Uragoga flexuosa, commonly known as the wandering fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, flexible fronds that can trail along the ground or climb supports. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically dark green with a glossy texture and can grow up to several meters in length.

Uragoga flexuosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
