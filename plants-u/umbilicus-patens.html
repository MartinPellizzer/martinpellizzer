Umbilicus patens

Umbilicus patens, commonly known as the star umbilicus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbellate inflorescence, which consists of small white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a star-like pattern. Umbilicus patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
