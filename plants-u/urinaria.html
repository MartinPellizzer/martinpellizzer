Urinaria

Urinaria, commonly known as bladder campion, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

These plants are characterized by their small, often pink or white flowers and hairy stems. They are typically found in dry, rocky habitats and are native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. Urinaria species are herbaceous plants that can be annual or perennial, depending on the species.
