Urostigma natalense var. minor

Urostigma natalense var. minor, commonly known as Natal dwarf milkweed, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
