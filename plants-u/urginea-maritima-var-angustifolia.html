Urginea maritima var. angustifolia

Urginea maritima var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved African giant lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy white to pale purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are often narrower than the typical variety, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal environments.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
