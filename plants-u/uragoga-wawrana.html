Uragoga wawrana

Uragoga wawrana, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Uragoga wawrana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper humidity and light conditions.
