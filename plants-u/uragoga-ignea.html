Uragoga ignea

Uragoga ignea, commonly known as the flame leaf plant, is a striking species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red foliage, which becomes more intense under direct sunlight. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the warm seasons. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing a unique texture to its appearance.

Uragoga ignea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
