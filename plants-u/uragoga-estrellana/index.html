Uragoga Estrellana

The Uragoga estrellana is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes. Known for its striking bioluminescent properties, it emits a soft glow under moonlight. This unique trait has made it a subject of fascination for botanists and folklore enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, the plant remains elusive, with very few documented sightings in the wild. Its mysterious nature continues to inspire both scientific research and local legends.
