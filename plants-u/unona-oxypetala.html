Unona oxypetala

Unona oxypetala, commonly known as the sharp-lobed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks.

Unona oxypetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
