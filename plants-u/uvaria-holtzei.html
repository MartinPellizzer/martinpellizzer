Uvaria holtzei

Uvaria holtzei, commonly known as the African starfruit or African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by round, yellow fruits resembling starfruit.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes in its native habitat.
