Uvifera cubensis

Uvifera cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban grapefruit tree, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant produces citrus-like fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. Its botanical features include compound leaves and a branched root system adapted to coastal environments.

Uvifera cubensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
