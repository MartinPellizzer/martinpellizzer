Urginea natalensis

Urginea natalensis, commonly known as Natal arrowhead, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy bulbs that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. It flowers with pale green or white blooms that appear in the early summer.

Due to its medicinal properties and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
