Unona discolor

Unona discolor, commonly known as the white sand tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale gray bark and oval-shaped leaves that are usually a glossy green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range from white to pale yellow, followed by ornamental, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

Due to its attractive appearance and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental plant.
