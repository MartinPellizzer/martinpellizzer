Uvaria lurida var. macrophylla

Uvaria lurida var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed uvaria, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 15 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
