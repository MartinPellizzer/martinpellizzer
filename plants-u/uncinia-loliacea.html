Uncinia loliacea

Uncinia loliacea, commonly known as the grass of Parnassus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia, this plant thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. It is often found growing in open, rocky habitats at high elevations.

The plant is notable for its delicate appearance and its role in supporting local pollinators.
