Ulmus Scabra Var. Major

Ulmus scabra var. major is a rare and hardy species of elm known for its distinctive bark and resilience. Native to specific regions in Asia, this variant thrives in challenging environmental conditions. Its unique physical characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike. Despite its adaptability, it remains underappreciated and under-studied in modern plant science. This article explores its biology, habitat, and potential ecological significance.
