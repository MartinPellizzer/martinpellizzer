Urospatha varians var. spectabilis

Urospatha varians var. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy sword fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features long, arching fronds that can grow up to two meters in length, with a distinctive, sword-like shape. The leaves are typically bright green with a smooth, waxy texture and can develop a reddish tinge during the winter months. This fern thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive cycle in the wild.
