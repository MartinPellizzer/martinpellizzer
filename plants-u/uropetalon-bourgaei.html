Uropetalon bourgaei

Uropetalon bourgaei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Uropetalon bourgaei is also notable for its rapid growth and ability to produce fruit within a few years of planting.
