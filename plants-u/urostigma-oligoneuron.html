Urostigma oligoneuron

Urostigma oligoneuron, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a silvery or grayish underside, giving the plant its common name. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, clustered in panicles, and it produces small, round fruits that split open to release seeds.
