Uraria barbata

Uraria barbata, commonly known as the barbed uraria, is a herbaceous plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces cylindrical, woody pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and medicinal studies.
