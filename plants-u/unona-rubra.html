Unona rubra

Unona rubra, commonly known as the red-stemmed fern, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green fronds that contrast with its reddish-brown stems. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its leaves are typically pinnately compound, with a feathery appearance that adds a decorative touch to gardens.

Unona rubra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to purify the air.
