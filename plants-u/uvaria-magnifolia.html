Uvaria magnifolia

Uvaria magnifolia, commonly known as the magnificent uvaria, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers.
