Urostigma rhododendrifolium

Urostigma rhododendrifolium, commonly known as the red-flowered rock heath, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils, often in forested or open habitats. Its leaves are leathery and elliptical, with a distinctive reddish or purple hue on the underside.

Urostigma rhododendrifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
