Ursinia punctata

Ursinia punctata, commonly known as the spotted ursinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ursinia punctata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
