Unona elegans

Unona elegans, commonly known as the elegant unona, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palm-like leaves that can grow up to two meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and pink. Unona elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
