Uncaria domatiifera subsp. domatiifera

Uncaria domatiifera subsp.

domatiifera, commonly known as the creeping cat's claw, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its woody stems and small, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface.

The plant produces tiny, white or pinkish flowers that give way to red, fleshy berries. It is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
