Ursinia natalensis

Ursinia natalensis, commonly known as Natal daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in grasslands and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Ursinia natalensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
