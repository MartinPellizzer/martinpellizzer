Uraria cochinchinensis

Uraria cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Chinese uraria, is a leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
