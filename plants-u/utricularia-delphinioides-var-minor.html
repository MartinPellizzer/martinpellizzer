Utricularia delphinioides var. minor

Utricularia delphinioides var. minor, commonly known as the lesser water whorl, is a carnivorous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, aquatic or semi-aquatic species that thrives in shallow, still water environments. The plant features whorled leaves and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It uses specialized bladder-like structures to trap and digest small aquatic organisms.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
