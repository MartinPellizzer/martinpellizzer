Urostigma magnificum

Urostigma magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent starflower, is a striking perennial herb native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring, displaying shades of purple and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are upright, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Urostigma magnificum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value have made it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
