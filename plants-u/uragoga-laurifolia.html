Uragoga laurifolia

Uragoga laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy laurel.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically oval in shape. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries.

Uragoga laurifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
