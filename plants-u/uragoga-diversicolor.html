Uragoga diversicolor

Uragoga diversicolor, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multi-colored petals that range from pink to purple and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. Its unique floral structure includes a prominent lip petal that aids in pollination by attracting specific insect species.

Uragoga diversicolor is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
