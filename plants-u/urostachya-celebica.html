Urostachya celebica

Urostachya celebica, commonly known as the Celebes panicgrass, is a species of flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems.
