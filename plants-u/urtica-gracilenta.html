Urtica gracilenta

Urtica gracilenta, commonly known as slender nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and fine, stinging hairs on both sides. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Urtica gracilenta is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
