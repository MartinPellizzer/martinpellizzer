Unona ambongoensis

Unona ambongoensis, commonly known as the Namibian starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It is characterized by its striking star-shaped leaves that resemble the arms of a starfish, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are fleshy and store water, enabling it to survive in drought conditions.

Unona ambongoensis is a rare and intriguing species that showcases the adaptability of plants in extreme environments.
