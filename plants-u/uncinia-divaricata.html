Uncinia divaricata

Uncinia divaricata, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses and sedges. The plant is characterized by its distinctive zigzag arrangement of its stems, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Uncinia divaricata typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
