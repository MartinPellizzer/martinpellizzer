Uncaria rostrata

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
