Urostachys tafaensis

Urostachys tafaensis, commonly known as the Tafa's feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, arid environments. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Urostachys tafaensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
