Uvaria marenteria f. obtusiuscula

Uvaria marenteria f. obtusiuscula, commonly known as the blunt-leaved uvaria, is a variant of the species Uvaria marenteria, which belongs to the Anacardiaceae family.

It is characterized by its elliptical, thick, and bluntly rounded leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy.
