Uleiorchis liesneri

Uleiorchis liesneri, commonly known as the liesner's orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its arid or semi-arid habitat. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted to reduce water loss.

Uleiorchis liesneri is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and limited distribution.
