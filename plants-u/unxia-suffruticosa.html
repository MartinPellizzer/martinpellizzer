Unxia suffruticosa

Unxia suffruticosa, commonly known as the Chinese mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with spiny margins and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been introduced to other parts of the world.

Unxia suffruticosa is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
