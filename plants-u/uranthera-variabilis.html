Uranthera variabilis

Uranthera variabilis, commonly known as the variable uranthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
