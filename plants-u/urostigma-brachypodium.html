Urostigma brachypodium

Urostigma brachypodium, commonly known as the shortgrass bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the central and eastern United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue to purple flower spikes in the summer. It is characterized by its short stature and is often found in grassland ecosystems.
