Ursinia subflosculosa

Ursinia subflosculosa, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, gray-green leaves.

Ursinia subflosculosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
