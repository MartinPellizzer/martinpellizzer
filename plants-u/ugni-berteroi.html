Ugni berteroi

Ugni berteroi, commonly known as the Andean hawthorn, is a shrub native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its fruits are edible and have a tart flavor, often used in local cuisines.

Ugni berteroi plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food for various wildlife species.
