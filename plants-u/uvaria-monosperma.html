Uvaria monosperma

Uvaria monosperma, commonly known as the African star fruit or black star fruit, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 6 meters tall, characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round, yellow or orange berry that turns black when mature.

The fruit is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor, often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
