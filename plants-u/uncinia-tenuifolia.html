Uncinia tenuifolia

Uncinia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender hookweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. Uncinia tenuifolia is primarily valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
