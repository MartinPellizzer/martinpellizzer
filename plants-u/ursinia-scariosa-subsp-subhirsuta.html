Ursinia scariosa subsp. subhirsuta

Ursinia scariosa subsp.

subhirsuta, commonly known as the hairy ursinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in arid and semi-arid environments.
