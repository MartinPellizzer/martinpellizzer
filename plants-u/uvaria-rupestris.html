Uvaria rupestris

Uvaria rupestris, commonly known as the rock uvaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows on rocky slopes and in dry habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

Uvaria rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
