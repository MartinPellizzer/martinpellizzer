Uvifera retusa

Uvifera retusa, commonly known as the smooth grapefruit tree, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its botanical features include smooth, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit.

Uvifera retusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.
