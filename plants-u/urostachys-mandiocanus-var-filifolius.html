Urostachys mandiocanus var. filifolius

Urostachys mandiocanus var. filifolius, commonly known as the silky leaf spurge, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
