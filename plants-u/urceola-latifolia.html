Urceola latifolia

Urceola latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf urn plant, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb with distinctive, urn-shaped flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Urceola latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
