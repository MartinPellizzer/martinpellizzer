Urginea scilla var. pancration

Urginea scilla var. pancration, commonly known as the Pancratic Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces tall, erect stems with showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often spotted or streaked with contrasting colors. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early spring blooming habit, adding a touch of color to gardens in late winter or early spring.
