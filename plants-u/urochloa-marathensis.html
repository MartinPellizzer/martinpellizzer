Urochloa marathensis

Urochloa marathensis, commonly known as marathen grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The leaves are typically narrow, with a bluish-green color and a rough texture. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

This plant is often used in forage and erosion control due to its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
