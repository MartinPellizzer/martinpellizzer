Urceola imberbis

Urceola imberbis, commonly known as the smooth urn-plant, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its smooth, urn-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous fruits. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
