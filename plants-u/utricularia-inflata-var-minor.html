Utricularia inflata var. minor

Utricularia inflata var. minor, commonly known as the inflated bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its inflated, bladder-like structures that trap small aquatic organisms for nutrient absorption. Its unique morphology and carnivorous adaptation make it an interesting subject in botanical studies.
