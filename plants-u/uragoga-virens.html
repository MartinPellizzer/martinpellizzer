Uragoga virens

Uragoga virens, commonly known as the emerald green fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lush, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color. Uragoga virens thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to add a touch of lush greenery to gardens.
