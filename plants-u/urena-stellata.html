Urena stellata

Urena stellata, commonly known as star urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas, roadsides, and dry forests.

Urena stellata is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
