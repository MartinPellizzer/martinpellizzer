Ursinia discolor

Ursinia discolor, commonly known as the yellow ursinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It has hairy, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Ursinia discolor is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
