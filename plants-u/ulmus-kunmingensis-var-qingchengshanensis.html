Ulmus kunmingensis var. qingchengshanensis

Ulmus kunmingensis var. qingchengshanensis, commonly known as the Qingchengshan elm, is a variant of the Kunming elm native to China.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and smooth, grey bark. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions. It has been cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.

Its unique genetic traits make it a subject of interest in botanical research.
