Uragoga calliantha

Uragoga calliantha, commonly known as the silverleaf mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on various host trees in arid and semi-arid regions.

Uragoga calliantha plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food for certain wildlife species despite its toxic properties.
