Urginea indica

Urginea indica, commonly known as the African giant hyacinth, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem and produces clusters of tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often persisting through the winter.

Urginea indica is adapted to arid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
