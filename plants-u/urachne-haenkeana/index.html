Urachne Haenkeana

Cape honeysuckle, scientifically known as Urachne haenkeana, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red tubular flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its unique appearance and hardy nature have made it a favored subject for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its ornamental appeal, it is important to note that it can be invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
