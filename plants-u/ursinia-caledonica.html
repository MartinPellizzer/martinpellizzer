Ursinia caledonica

Ursinia caledonica, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-like inflorescence composed of small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance.

Ursinia caledonica is also valued for its resilience to coastal winds and drought.
