Urginea tenella

Urginea tenella, commonly known as the red tulip, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a distinctive dark red or purple base. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem and broad, strap-like leaves that are usually dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
