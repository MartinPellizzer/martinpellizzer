Urochloa bolbodes

Urochloa bolbodes, commonly known as the globular grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and dense foliage, which makes it suitable for ornamental and erosion control purposes. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green coloration.

Urochloa bolbodes is often used in landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
