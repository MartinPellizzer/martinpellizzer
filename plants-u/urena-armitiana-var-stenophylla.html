Urena armitiana var. stenophylla

Urena armitiana var. stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf velvetleaf, is a variant of the Urena genus native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in many parts of the world.
