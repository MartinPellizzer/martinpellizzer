Uvaria cordifolia

Uvaria cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf uvaria, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Uvaria cordifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
