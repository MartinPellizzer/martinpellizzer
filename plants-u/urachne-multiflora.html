Urachne multiflora

Urachne multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered urachne, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters along the stems. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss.

Urachne multiflora is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
