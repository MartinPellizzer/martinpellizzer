Ursinia argentea

Ursinia argentea, commonly known as silver ursinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Ursinia argentea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
