Urostigma erythrostictum

Urostigma erythrostictum, commonly known as the red-streaked sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It typically features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor itself in loose, sandy soils.

Urostigma erythrostictum is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to thrive in harsh, nutrient-poor conditions.
