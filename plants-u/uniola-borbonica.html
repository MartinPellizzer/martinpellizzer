Uniola borbonica

Uniola borbonica, commonly known as the dwarf grass or dwarf pampas grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening. It is characterized by its low-growing habit and ability to withstand coastal conditions.

Uniola borbonica is also valued for its use in erosion control and as a decorative plant in coastal landscapes.
