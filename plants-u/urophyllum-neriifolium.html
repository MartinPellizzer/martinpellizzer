Urophyllum neriifolium

Urophyllum neriifolium, commonly known as the black-leafed evergreen, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its bark is thick and rugged, providing a protective layer against environmental stress.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
