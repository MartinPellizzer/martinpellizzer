Urena schultzii

Urena schultzii, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its milky sap, which is toxic, is a notable feature and can cause skin irritation.

Urena schultzii is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
