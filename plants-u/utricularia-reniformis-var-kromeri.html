Utricularia reniformis var. kromeri

Utricularia reniformis var. kromeri, commonly known as the kidney-shaped bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, bladder-like traps used to capture prey. The plant typically grows in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, such as marshes and ponds. Its leaves are usually oval or kidney-shaped, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This variety is known for its rapid growth and unique trapping mechanism.
