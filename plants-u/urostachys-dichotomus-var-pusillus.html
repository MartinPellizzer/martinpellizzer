Urostachys dichotomus var. pusillus

Urostachys dichotomus var. pusillus, commonly known as the dwarf bamboo or small-branch bamboo, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems that often branch dichotomously, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are small, linear, and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its overall delicate look. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
