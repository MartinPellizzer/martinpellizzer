Urbanisol aristatus

Urbanisol aristatus, commonly known as the cityscape spire, is a unique plant adapted to thrive in urban environments.

It possesses a tall, slender stem with a series of elongated, needle-like leaves that help reduce water loss in dry conditions. The plant's root system is highly efficient at absorbing nutrients from compacted soils found in city settings. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are pollinated by wind rather than insects.

Due to its resilience and low maintenance requirements, it is often used in urban landscaping to enhance green spaces.
