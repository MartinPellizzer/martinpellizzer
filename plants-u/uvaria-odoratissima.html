Uvaria odoratissima

Uvaria odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant uvaria, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are fragrant and attract various pollinators. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red when mature.

Uvaria odoratissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
