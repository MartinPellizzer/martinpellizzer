Uragoga nervosa

Uragoga nervosa, commonly known as the slender nerve-leaf plant, is a herbaceous species native to tropical regions of South America.

It features delicate, linear leaves with prominent venation patterns, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming during the wet season. Its fibrous roots and rhizomatous growth habit allow it to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Uragoga nervosa is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
