Urtica takedana

Urtica takedana, commonly known as Japanese nettle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Urtica takedana is characterized by its stinging hairs that release an irritant when touched.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
