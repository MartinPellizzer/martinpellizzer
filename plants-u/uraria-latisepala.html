Uraria latisepala

Uraria latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepal uraria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its pods are hairy and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Uraria latisepala is often found in open areas, wastelands, and along roadsides, thriving in dry to moderately wet conditions.
