Ursinia Foeniculacea

Ursinia foeniculacea is a unique flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. This plant is known for its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Ursinia foeniculacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
