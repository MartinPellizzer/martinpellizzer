Uragoga phyllocalymmoides

Uragoga phyllocalymmoides, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
