Urtica triangularis

Urtica triangularis, commonly known as the triangular nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. The plant is characterized by its triangular stems and opposite, serrated leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small green flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

Urtica triangularis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
