Uncaria lanosa var. glabrata

Uncaria lanosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth cat's claw, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, glossy green foliage. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries. Its roots and stems contain alkaloids, which have been used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
