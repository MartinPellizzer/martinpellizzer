Ursinia longiscapa

Ursinia longiscapa, commonly known as the long-stemmed ursinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Ursinia longiscapa is often grown as an ornamental plant for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
