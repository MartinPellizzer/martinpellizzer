Urginea anthericoides var. secundiflora

Urginea anthericoides var. secundiflora, commonly known as the purple star lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive star-like shape. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, emerging from a large, fleshy bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
