Uraria prunellifolia

Uraria prunellifolia, commonly known as the Indian senna or small senna, is a deciduous shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are trifoliate and have a distinctive shape. The plant produces small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bean-like pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Uraria prunellifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.
