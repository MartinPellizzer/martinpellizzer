Uncasia cuneifolia

Uncasia cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and belongs to the Asteraceae family. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, blooming in the spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Uncasia cuneifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
