Urostigma clusiifolium

Urostigma clusiifolium, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that resemble flames.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant has compound leaves with multiple small, lance-shaped leaflets. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

Urostigma clusiifolium thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
