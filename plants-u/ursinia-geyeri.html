Ursinia geyeri

Ursinia geyeri, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in spring and summer. Ursinia geyeri thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
