Urostigma subtriplinervium f. major

Urostigma subtriplinervium f. major, commonly known as the large-veined urostigma, is a variant of the Urostigma subtriplinervium species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive, trifoliate leaves with prominent veins. The leaves are typically arranged alternately along the stem, and the plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
