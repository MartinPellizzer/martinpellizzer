Umbilicus simplex

Umbilicus simplex, commonly known as the common umbilicus, is a small, annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters and features simple, alternate leaves that are lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

The plant is characterized by its unique, umbilical-like appearance, which gives it its common name.
