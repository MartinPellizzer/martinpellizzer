Utricularia vulcanica

Utricularia vulcanica, commonly known as the volcanic bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to volcanic regions.

It possesses specialized bladder-like structures that trap small aquatic organisms. The plant thrives in nutrient-poor, acidic water environments, often found near geothermal springs. Its rapid growth and unique trapping mechanism make it an intriguing subject for botanical study.

Utricularia vulcanica exhibits adaptations to extreme conditions, including high temperatures and mineral-rich soils.
