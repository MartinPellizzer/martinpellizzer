Ursinia affinis

Ursinia affinis, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are typically arranged in a rounded, umbrella-like shape, giving it its common name. Ursinia affinis is an evergreen shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
