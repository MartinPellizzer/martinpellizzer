Urochilus crebriflorus

Urochilus crebriflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered urochilus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in coastal and inland regions.
