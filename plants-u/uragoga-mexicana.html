Uragoga mexicana

Uragoga mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican uragoga, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect growth habit and branching stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a rough texture and a distinct pubescent surface.
