Urtica globosa

Urtica globosa, commonly known as the globe nettle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, woolly inflorescence that resembles a small globe, giving it its common name. The plant produces small green flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine stinging hairs, typical of the Urtica genus.

Urtica globosa is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.
