Urostachys dichotomus var. spegazzinii

Urostachys dichotomus var. spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini's dichotomous sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to low moisture conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or reddish in color. It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants.
