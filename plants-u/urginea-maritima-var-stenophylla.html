Urginea maritima var. stenophylla

Urginea maritima var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved African narcissus, is a variant of the African daffodil.

It is a bulbous perennial plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
