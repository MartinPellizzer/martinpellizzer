Urostigma lentiginosum

Urostigma lentiginosum, commonly known as the spotted bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibacillaceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, bladder-like structures that trap small aquatic organisms, aiding in nutrient acquisition. Urostigma lentiginosum is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-rich waters.
