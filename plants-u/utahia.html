Utahia

Utahia, commonly known as the Utahia plant, is a unique species native to the arid regions of Utah.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access water from deep underground.

Utahia plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
