Uebelinia nigerica

Uebelinia nigerica, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shrubland and woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Uebelinia nigerica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
