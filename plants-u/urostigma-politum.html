Urostigma politum

Urostigma politum, commonly known as the smooth bladdernut, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland conservation efforts.
