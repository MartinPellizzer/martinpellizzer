Uncinia × rubrovaginata

Uncinia × rubrovaginata, commonly known as the red-vaginal uncinia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive red-tinged bracts that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its flowers are small and clustered in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
