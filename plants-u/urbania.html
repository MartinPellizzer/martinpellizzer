Urbania

Urbania, commonly known as the city flower, is a hardy perennial plant native to urban environments.

It thrives in compacted soil and limited sunlight, making it ideal for city gardens and green spaces. The plant features small, delicate flowers in shades of blue and purple, which bloom in late spring. Its ability to tolerate pollution and poor soil conditions makes it a symbol of resilience in urban landscapes.

Urbania is often used in landscaping to enhance the aesthetic appeal of concrete areas.
