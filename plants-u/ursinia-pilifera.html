Ursinia pilifera

Ursinia pilifera, commonly known as the African feather flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Ursinia pilifera is also valued for its historical use in traditional medicine.
