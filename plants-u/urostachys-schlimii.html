Urostachys schlimii

Urostachys schlimii, commonly known as the Schlim's stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water and withstand arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Urostachys schlimii is known for its drought tolerance and ability to survive in harsh, rocky environments.
