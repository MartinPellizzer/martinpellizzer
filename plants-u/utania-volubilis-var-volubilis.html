Utania volubilis var. volubilis

Utania volubilis var. volubilis, commonly known as the climbing nightshade, is a vigorous vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth habit and ability to climb using its tendrils. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Botanically, it belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers.
