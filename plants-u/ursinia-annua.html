Ursinia annua

Ursinia annua, commonly known as the annual ursinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and produces small, yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and it is often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

Ursinia annua is cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
