Uvularia nitida

Uvularia nitida, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in woodland habitats, preferring partial shade and moist, well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Uvularia nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
