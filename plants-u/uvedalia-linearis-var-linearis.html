Uvedalia linearis var. linearis

Uvedalia linearis var. linearis, commonly known as the slender uvedalia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are typically covered in fine, hairy hairs. The leaves are linear and elongated, often with a bluish-gray hue due to dense pubescence. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
