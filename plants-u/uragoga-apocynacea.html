Uragoga apocynacea

Uragoga apocynacea, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while the flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually pink or purple in color.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its beauty.
