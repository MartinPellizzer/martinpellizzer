Urostigma canaliculatum

Urostigma canaliculatum, commonly known as the narrow-veined grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with prominent veins. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it identifiable by its distinctive leaf venation and growth form.
