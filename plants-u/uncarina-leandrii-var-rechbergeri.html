Uncarina leandrii var. rechbergeri

Uncarina leandrii var. rechbergeri, commonly known as the Rechberger's unicorn plant, is a unique succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that can grow up to several meters in length, often resembling a horn or a twisted vine. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration.

This variety is prized for its unusual appearance and ability to thrive in arid conditions with minimal care.
