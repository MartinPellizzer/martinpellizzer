Urophyllum chinense subsp. chinense

Urophyllum chinense subsp.

chinense, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia. It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer.

The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

The subspecies is closely related to other members of the Campanulaceae family, sharing similar structural and ecological characteristics.
