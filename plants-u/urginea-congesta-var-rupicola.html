Urginea congesta var. rupicola

Urginea congesta var. rupicola, commonly known as the rock rush, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous plant that typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually white or pale purple, appearing in clusters at the top of the stem.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a succulent.
