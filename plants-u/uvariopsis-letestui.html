Uvariopsis letestui

Uvariopsis letestui, commonly known as the Letestu's uvariopsis, is a tropical plant belonging to the Annonaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of western Africa, particularly in countries like Cameroon and Gabon. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in axillary or terminal inflorescences.

The fruit is a smooth, fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow or orange when mature.
