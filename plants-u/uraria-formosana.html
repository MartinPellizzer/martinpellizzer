Uraria formosana

Uraria formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan uraria, is a leguminous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
