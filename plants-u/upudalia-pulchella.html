Upudalia pulchella

Upudalia pulchella, commonly known as the elegant bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and marshy areas. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Upudalia pulchella is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in humid climates.
