Urginea flexuosa

Urginea flexuosa, commonly known as the wild African lily, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. Its bulbs are underground and serve as storage organs, allowing the plant to survive dry periods.

Urginea flexuosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
