Uncaria africana var. domatiifera

Uncaria africana var. domatiifera, commonly known as the African climbing tea or African cat's claw, is a woody vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its twining stems and pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with parts used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
