Udora verticillata var. minor

Udora verticillata var. minor, commonly known as the lesser water plantain, is a aquatic herbaceous plant native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It features whorled leaves that are typically narrow and strap-like, with a bluish-green coloration. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving water and is often found in temperate regions.

Its presence is an indicator of healthy wetland ecosystems.
