Uvifera klotzschiana

Uvifera klotzschiana, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, ovate leaves that are covered in a fine layer of silvery-white pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a small, round berry that changes from green to red as it matures.

Uvifera klotzschiana is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
