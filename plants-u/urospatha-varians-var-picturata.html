Urospatha varians var. picturata

Urospatha varians var. picturata, commonly known as the picturata variant of the variable sword fern, is a striking fern species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped fronds that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The fronds are covered in intricate patterns of silvery-white and green, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
