Urbanella cuspidata

Urbanella cuspidata, commonly known as the spiny urbanella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and spiny, woody stems. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats. Its leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing year-round interest.

Urbanella cuspidata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to urban environments.
