Urena muricata

Urena muricata, commonly known as the rough urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that typically grows up to 2 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins and produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters. Its fruits are cylindrical, three-angled capsules that release seeds when mature.

Urena muricata is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
