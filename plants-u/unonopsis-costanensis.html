Unonopsis costanensis

Unonopsis costanensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the humid forests of Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
