Ulex airensis

Ulex airensis, commonly known as the Spanish heath, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, spiny stems and small, evergreen leaves. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and heathlands.

Ulex airensis is adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is relatively drought-tolerant.
