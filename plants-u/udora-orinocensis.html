Udora orinocensis

Udora orinocensis, commonly known as the Orinoco udora, is a flowering plant native to the Orinoco River basin in South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found near waterways. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Udora orinocensis is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to humid climates.
