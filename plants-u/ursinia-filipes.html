Ursinia filipes

Ursinia filipes, commonly known as the silver leaf ursinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, yellow flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Ursinia filipes is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
