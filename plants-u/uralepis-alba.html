Uralepis alba

Uralepis alba, commonly known as the white uralepis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Uralepis alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
