Urbanella procera

Urbanella procera, commonly known as the tall urbanella, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Urbanella procera is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in urban environments.
