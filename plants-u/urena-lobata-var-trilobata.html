Urena lobata var. trilobata

Urena lobata var. trilobata, commonly known as the three-lobed urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, trifoliate leaves with three distinct lobes, and small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release tiny seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
