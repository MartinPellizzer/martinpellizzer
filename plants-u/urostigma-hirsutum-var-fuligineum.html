Urostigma hirsutum var. fuligineum

Urostigma hirsutum var. fuligineum, commonly known as the sooty false nettle, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, dark green leaves that often have a sooty appearance due to the dense pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
