Urophyllum assahanicum

Urophyllum assahanicum, commonly known as the Assahan sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and savannas. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Urophyllum assahanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
