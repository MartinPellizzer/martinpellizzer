Urtica darwinii

Urtica darwinii, commonly known as the Darwin's nettle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and stinging hairs, which can cause irritation upon contact. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Urtica darwinii is a perennial herb that can grow up to 1 meter in height.
