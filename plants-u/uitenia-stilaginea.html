Uitenia stilaginea

Uitenia stilaginea, commonly known as the sticky vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems that produce a sticky, resinous substance. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by fleshy, red berries.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential healing properties.
