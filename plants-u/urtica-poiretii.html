Urtica poiretii

Urtica poiretii, commonly known as the giant nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial herb that can grow up to 2 meters in height, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and stinging hairs. The plant belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. Its flowers are small and green, arranged in dense clusters, and it produces a capsule fruit containing numerous seeds.

Urtica poiretii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas.
