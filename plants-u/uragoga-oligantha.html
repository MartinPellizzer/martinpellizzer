Uragoga oligantha

Uragoga oligantha, commonly known as the slender urara, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Uragoga oligantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and resilience in dry conditions.
