Uragoga pacimonica

Uragoga pacimonica, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique leaf structure helps it conserve moisture, making it well-adapted to its native habitat.

Due to habitat loss, Uragoga pacimonica is considered endangered and is protected in many regions.
