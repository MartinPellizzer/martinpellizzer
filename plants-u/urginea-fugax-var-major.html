Urginea fugax var. major

Urginea fugax var. major, commonly known as the giant tiger flower, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy, white or pale yellow flowers with prominent, tiger-like markings. The plant produces a single, erect stem that can grow up to 2 meters tall, supporting its dramatic blooms. Its bulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water and nutrients in arid environments.

This species is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and dramatic flowering display.
