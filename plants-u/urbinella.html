Urbinella

Urbinella, commonly known as the golden bell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
