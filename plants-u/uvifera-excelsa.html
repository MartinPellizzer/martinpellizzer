Uvifera excelsa

Uvifera excelsa, commonly known as the noble date palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its long, pinnate leaves and produces large, clustered inflorescences that eventually bear edible dates. The plant thrives in warm, arid climates and requires well-drained soil. Its trunk is smooth and often develops a fluted appearance with age.

Uvifera excelsa is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in traditional agriculture.
