Urostigma rubrum

Urostigma rubrum, commonly known as the red bladdergrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods that give it its common name. Urostigma rubrum is typically found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
