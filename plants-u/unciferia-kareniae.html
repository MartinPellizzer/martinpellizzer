Unciferia kareniae

Unciferia kareniae, commonly known as the Karenian mistplant, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, pale green leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its unique botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires protected environments to thrive.
