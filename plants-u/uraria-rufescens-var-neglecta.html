Uraria rufescens var. neglecta

Uraria rufescens var. neglecta, commonly known as the slender uraria, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and savannas. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in ecological restoration projects.
