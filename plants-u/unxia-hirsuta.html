Unxia hirsuta

Unxia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy unxia, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family, which includes roses and other shrubs. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in the spring.

Unxia hirsuta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
