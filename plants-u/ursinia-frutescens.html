Ursinia frutescens

Ursinia frutescens, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Ursinia frutescens is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
