Urtica millettii

Urtica millettii, commonly known as Chinese nettle, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with fine, stinging hairs that can cause irritation upon contact. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Urtica millettii produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often used in traditional medicine.
