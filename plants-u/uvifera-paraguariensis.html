Uvifera paraguariensis

Uvifera paraguariensis, commonly known as the passion fruit vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It produces vibrant orange or yellow fruits that are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces striking flowers with a complex structure. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit is widely cultivated for its edible seeds and juice.
