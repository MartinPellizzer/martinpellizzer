Urostachys rubiginosus

Urostachys rubiginosus, commonly known as the red-stemmed grass, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that often display a reddish hue, especially when mature. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Urostachys rubiginosus is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
