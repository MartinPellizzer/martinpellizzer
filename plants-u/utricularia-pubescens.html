Utricularia pubescens

Utricularia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is a small, aquatic or semi-aquatic plant that thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Utricularia pubescens typically grows in dense mats and is often found in ponds, ditches, and marshes.

Its hairy appearance, due to fine pubescence on its stems and leaves, is a distinguishing feature.
