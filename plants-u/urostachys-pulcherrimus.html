Urostachys pulcherrimus

Urostachys pulcherrimus, commonly known as the beautiful stonecrop, is a member of the Crassulaceae family.

It is a low-growing, succulent perennial that features fleshy, thick leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. Native to China, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
