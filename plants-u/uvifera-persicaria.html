Uvifera persicaria

Uvifera persicaria, commonly known as broadleaf plantain, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is often found in moist, disturbed soils and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It has a robust root system and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Uvifera persicaria is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
