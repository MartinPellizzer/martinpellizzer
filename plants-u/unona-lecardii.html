Unona lecardii

It is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology, featuring elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Unona lecardii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
