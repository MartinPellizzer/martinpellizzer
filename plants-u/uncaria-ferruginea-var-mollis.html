Uncaria ferruginea var. mollis

Uncaria ferruginea var. mollis, commonly known as the velvet cat's claw, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its reddish-brown stems and small, oval-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small yellow flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.

Its growth habit and unique appearance make it a distinctive addition to tropical gardens.
