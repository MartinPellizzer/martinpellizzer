Urtica nicaraguensis

Urtica nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan nettle, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of stinging nettles. The plant is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its small greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels, and it produces small, smooth fruits.

Urtica nicaraguensis is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and stream banks.
