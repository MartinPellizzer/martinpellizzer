Unona eminii

Unona eminii, commonly known as the eminii tree, is a rare and endemic species native to a specific region in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible fruits with a unique, slightly tart flavor.
