Urostigma leucotrichum

Urostigma leucotrichum, commonly known as the white-haired grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered with fine, white hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy surface.

Urostigma leucotrichum is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
