Utricularia beaugleholei

Utricularia beaugleholei, commonly known as the Cape York bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to Australia.

It is a small, aquatic plant that typically grows in shallow, still waters. The plant features tiny bladders that trap and digest small aquatic organisms. Its leaves are simple and often submerged, while its stems are thin and branching.
