Uragoga myrsinifolia

Uragoga myrsinifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for climbing structures or as a ground cover in shaded areas.
