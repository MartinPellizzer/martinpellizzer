Uvifera nodosa

Uvifera nodosa, commonly known as the nodding uvifera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its unique nodding inflorescence and adaptability to wet conditions make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
