Urginea undulata var. caeculi

Urginea undulata var. caeculi, commonly known as the purple star lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy flowers with violet petals and a distinctive, star-like shape, blooming in late summer. The plant produces a single, elongated flower stem that rises above the foliage, making it a standout in gardens. Its bulbs are adapted to survive in arid conditions, storing water and nutrients for extended periods.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates.
