Urophyllum moluccanum

Urophyllum moluccanum, commonly known as the Moluccan starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Moluccas in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Urophyllum moluccanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
