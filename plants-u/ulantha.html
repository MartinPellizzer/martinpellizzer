Ulantha

Ulantha, commonly known as the golden bell vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vigorous growth.

Ulantha is often cultivated for its beauty and can be used in vertical gardening or as a decorative element in landscapes.
