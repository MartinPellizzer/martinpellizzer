Urbanodendron verrucosum

Urbanodendron verrucosum, commonly known as the urban tree, is a unique species adapted to thrive in urban environments.

It possesses a robust root system that helps stabilize soil and absorb pollutants from the air and water. The plant's leaves are thick and waxy, reducing water loss in polluted and dry conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are primarily pollinated by wind.

This species is valued for its resilience and ability to contribute to urban green spaces.
