Ulostoma

Ulostoma, commonly known as the "water lily of the sea," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its aquatic or semi-aquatic habitat, typically found in freshwater environments. The plant features showy, often brightly colored spathes that enclose the true flowers.
