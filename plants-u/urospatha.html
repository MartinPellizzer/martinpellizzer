Urospatha

Urospatha, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often have a strikingly veined pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are typically followed by spadices, which are modified leaf-like structures. Urospatha thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
