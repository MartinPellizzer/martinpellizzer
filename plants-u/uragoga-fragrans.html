Uragoga fragrans

Uragoga fragrans, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its fragrance is often described as sweet and reminiscent of jasmine.

Uragoga fragrans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic properties.
