Urochloa jinshaicola

Urochloa jinshaicola, commonly known as the Jinshaicola grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with narrow, blade-like leaves. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in dry climates.

Urochloa jinshaicola is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
