Unona Ligularis

Ligularia, scientifically known as Unona ligularis, is a striking perennial plant native to eastern Asia. It is celebrated for its large, heart-shaped leaves that often display vibrant green or variegated patterns. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is commonly found in woodland gardens. Its bold foliage makes it a popular choice for adding texture and visual interest to landscapes. With its unique appearance and hardy nature, Ligularia is a favorite among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
