Urophyllum capitatum

Urophyllum capitatum, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow flowers in late spring. Its inflorescence consists of numerous small florets arranged in a dense, circular pattern.

Urophyllum capitatum is often found in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and prairies.
