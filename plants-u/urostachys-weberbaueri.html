Urostachys weberbaueri

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Urostachys weberbaueri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sunlight.
