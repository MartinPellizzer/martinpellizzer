Urceodiscus belensis var. conferta

Urceodiscus belensis var. conferta, commonly known as the dense-flowered urceodiscus, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, urn-shaped flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. The plant has small, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a dense inflorescence that gives it its varietal name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of atmospheric moisture.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
