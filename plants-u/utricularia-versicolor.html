Utricularia versicolor

Utricularia versicolor, commonly known as the variable bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, aquatic or semi-aquatic growth habit and the presence of specialized bladder-like structures used to trap prey. Utricularia versicolor is typically found in freshwater habitats such as ponds, ditches, and wetlands.
