Urostachys rubricaulis

Urostachys rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features fleshy, upright stems that are typically reddish in color, which help it store water and withstand harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Its leaves are thick and often covered in a waxy cuticle, reducing water loss.

Urostachys rubricaulis is well adapted to rocky and arid habitats, making it a resilient addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.
