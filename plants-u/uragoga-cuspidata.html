Uragoga cuspidata

Uragoga cuspidata, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, lanceolate fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist forests or along stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Uragoga cuspidata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
