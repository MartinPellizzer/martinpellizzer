Ubiaea

Ubiaea, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its pale, almost translucent leaves and its striking, white, flower-like structures that resemble ghostly apparitions. Ubiaea thrives in specific, moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them.

Due to its delicate nature and limited habitat, Ubiaea is considered a vulnerable species in many areas.
