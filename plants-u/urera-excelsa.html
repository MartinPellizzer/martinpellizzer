Urera excelsa

Urera excelsa, commonly known as the giant urera, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and can grow up to 30 meters tall. The plant has a smooth, greyish-brown bark and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry containing numerous seeds.

Urera excelsa is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local medicine.
