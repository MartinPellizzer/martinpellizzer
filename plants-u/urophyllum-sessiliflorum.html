Urophyllum sessiliflorum

Urophyllum sessiliflorum, commonly known as the sessile-flower urophyllum, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, sessile flowers that are often clustered together. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are usually small and may vary in color depending on the species.

Urophyllum sessiliflorum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky habitats.
