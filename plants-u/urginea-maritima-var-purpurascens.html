Urginea maritima var. purpurascens

Urginea maritima var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered African onion, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has a robust, fleshy bulb that stores water and nutrients, aiding its survival in arid environments. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often with a reddish tinge.

This variety is cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
