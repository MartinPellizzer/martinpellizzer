Uragoga tortilis

Uragoga tortilis, commonly known as the twisted leaf plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curling foliage that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in humid climates.

Uragoga tortilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
