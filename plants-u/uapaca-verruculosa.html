Uapaca verruculosa

Uapaca verruculosa, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a shrub native to eastern and southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by elliptical, dark blue to black fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence.

Uapaca verruculosa is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
