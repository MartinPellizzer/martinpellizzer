Urena tomentosa

Urena tomentosa, commonly known as hairy urena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are usually yellow or white, arranged in clusters. Urena tomentosa is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

It is often found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas where it can spread easily.
