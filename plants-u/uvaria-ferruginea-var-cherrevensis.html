Uvaria ferruginea var. cherrevensis

Uvaria ferruginea var. cherrevensis, commonly known as the red-veined uvaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with prominent red veins. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn from green to red when mature.
