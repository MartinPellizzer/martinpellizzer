Urceola tournieri

Urceola tournieri, commonly known as the Tournier's urn plant, is a unique member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive urn-shaped flowers, which are typically violet or purple in color. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture.

Urceola tournieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
