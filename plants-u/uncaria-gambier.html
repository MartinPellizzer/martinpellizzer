Uncaria gambier

Uncaria gambier, commonly known as gambier vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, elliptic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. Its roots and stems are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
