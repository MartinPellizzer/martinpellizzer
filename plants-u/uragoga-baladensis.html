Uragoga baladensis

Uragoga baladensis, commonly known as the Baladensis orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant blooms that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. Due to habitat destruction, Uragoga baladensis is considered endangered and is protected in many regions.
