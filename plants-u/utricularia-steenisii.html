Utricularia steenisii

Utricularia steenisii, commonly known as the African bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic species that thrives in shallow, nutrient-poor water bodies. The plant features delicate, floating leaves with specialized bladders that trap small aquatic organisms. Its roots are typically submerged, and it reproduces through both sexual and vegetative means.

Utricularia steenisii is known for its rapid growth and unique trapping mechanism, making it an intriguing subject in botanical studies.
