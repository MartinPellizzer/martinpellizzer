Uraria candida

Uraria candida, commonly known as white uraria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
