Urena australiensis

Urena australiensis, commonly known as the Australian urena, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in the northern regions of the country. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by flat, seed-filled pods. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Urena australiensis is adapted to withstand saline and arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
