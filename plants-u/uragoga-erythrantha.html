Uragoga erythrantha

Uragoga erythrantha, commonly known as the red-flowered uragoga, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and dense, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered fruits that are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
