Uragoga blumeana

Uragoga blumeana, commonly known as the Blumean orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically come in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Uragoga blumeana thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
