Uvaria hamiltonii var. fauveliana

Uvaria hamiltonii var. fauveliana, commonly known as the Fauvel's evergreen hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and as a houseplant in cooler regions.
