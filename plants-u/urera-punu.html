Urera punu

Urera punu, commonly known as the red sandalwood tree, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, reddish-brown heartwood that is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal. The plant is also noted for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional herbal remedies.
