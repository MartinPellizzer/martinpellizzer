Uropedium

Uropedium, commonly known as the "butterfly plant," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that resemble butterflies. Uropedium species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
