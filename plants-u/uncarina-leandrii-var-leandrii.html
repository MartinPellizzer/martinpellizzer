Uncarina leandrii var. leandrii

Uncarina leandrii var. leandrii, commonly known as the Leander's burr cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, sprawling vines and large, lobed leaves. The plant produces distinctive yellow flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant. Its fruit is a spiny, elongated berry that matures to a yellowish-orange color.

This species is native to parts of Africa and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
