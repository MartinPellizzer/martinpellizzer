Urostachys fontinaloides var. filiformis

Urostachys fontinaloides var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender thread moss, is a variant of the thread moss species native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, filamentous growth habit, with long, narrow leaves that form a dense, carpet-like mat. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous areas with high humidity. Its botanical structure is simple, consisting of protonemata, gametophytes, and sporophytes, typical of bryophyte morphology.

This variant is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphological traits and ecological adaptations.
