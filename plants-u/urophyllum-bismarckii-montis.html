Urophyllum bismarckii-montis

Urophyllum bismarckii-montis, commonly known as the Bismarck mountain pincushion, is a unique flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires a cool, temperate climate to flourish. Its botanical features make it a striking addition to rock gardens and alpine landscapes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare and often found in isolated mountainous areas.
