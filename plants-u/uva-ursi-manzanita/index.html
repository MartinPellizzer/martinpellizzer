Uva-Ursi Manzanita

The uva-ursi manzanita is a hardy shrub native to the western united states and northern mexico. it thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. its leaves are small, oval, and covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. the plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the spring. it is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
