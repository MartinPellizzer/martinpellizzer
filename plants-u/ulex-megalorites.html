Ulex megalorites

Ulex megalorites, commonly known as the greater sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, and its stems are often spiny.

Ulex megalorites is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
