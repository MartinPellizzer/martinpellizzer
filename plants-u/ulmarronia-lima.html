Ulmarronia lima

Ulmarronia lima, commonly known as the Lima tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ulmaceae, which includes elm and plane trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically arranged in pendulous clusters, while the fruit is a small, woody capsule.
