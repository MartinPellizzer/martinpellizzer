Urochloa lorentziana

Urochloa lorentziana, commonly known as silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in loose panicles, typical of the grass family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Urochloa lorentziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its unique foliage.
