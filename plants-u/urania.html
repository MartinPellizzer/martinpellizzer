Urania

Urania, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue and purple flowers, which are highly prized for their beauty. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are known for their intricate structure and long-lasting bloom, making it a popular choice in horticulture.

However, due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is also considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
