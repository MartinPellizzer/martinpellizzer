Uvifera plumieri

Uvifera plumieri, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces fleshy, orange fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Uvifera plumieri plays an important role in the local ecosystem, providing food for native birds and insects.
