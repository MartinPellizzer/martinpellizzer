Ulmus kunmingensis

Ulmus kunmingensis, commonly known as the Yunnan elm, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, spherical fruits that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and parks.
