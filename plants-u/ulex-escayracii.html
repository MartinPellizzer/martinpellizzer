Ulex escayracii

Ulex escayracii, commonly known as the Spanish heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula, particularly found in rocky and coastal areas. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Ulex escayracii is adapted to coastal environments, showing resilience to salt spray and strong winds.
