Urtica berteroana

Urtica berteroana, commonly known as the Bertero's nettle, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various stinging nettles. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and fine, stinging hairs on both sides. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its flowering stems bear small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters.
