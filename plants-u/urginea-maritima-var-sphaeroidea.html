Urginea maritima var. sphaeroidea

Urginea maritima var. sphaeroidea, commonly known as the African narcissus, is a striking bulbous plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It features large, showy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has a thick, fleshy bulb that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Its leaves are typically broad and strap-like, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens with good drainage.
