Ursinia jacottetiana

Ursinia jacottetiana, commonly known as the Cape snowdrop, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, white to pale blue flowers that resemble snowdrops. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, hairy leaves and has a compact growth habit.

Ursinia jacottetiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
