Uncasia torreyana

Uncasia torreyana, commonly known as the Torrey's lily, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and streambanks. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and bloom in the spring.

Uncasia torreyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
