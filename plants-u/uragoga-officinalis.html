Uragoga officinalis

Uragoga officinalis, commonly known as the medicinal orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its aromatic properties. Its roots and leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing benefits.

However, due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is now considered a vulnerable species.
