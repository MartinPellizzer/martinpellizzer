Urostachys hippurideus

Urostachys hippurideus, commonly known as the desert lily, is a perennial herb native to arid regions of North America.

It features a rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, typically blooming in spring.
