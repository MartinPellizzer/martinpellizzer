Utricularia humboldtii

Utricularia humboldtii, commonly known as the Humboldt bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to South America.

It is a small, aquatic or semi-aquatic plant that thrives in shallow, nutrient-poor water bodies. The plant features tiny, bladder-like traps that capture and digest small aquatic organisms. Its leaves are typically whorled and have a delicate, translucent appearance.

Utricularia humboldtii is notable for its rapid growth and ability to adapt to various water conditions.
