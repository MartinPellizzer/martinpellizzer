Unona mindorensis

Unona mindorensis, commonly known as the Mindanao orchid, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Unona mindorensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
