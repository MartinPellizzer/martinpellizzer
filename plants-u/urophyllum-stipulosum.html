Urophyllum stipulosum

Urophyllum stipulosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved urophyllum, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Urophyllum stipulosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
