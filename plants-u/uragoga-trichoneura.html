Uragoga trichoneura

Uragoga trichoneura, commonly known as the hairy-winged orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive hairy wings on the sepals and petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Uragoga trichoneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems.
