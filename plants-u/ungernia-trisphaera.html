Ungernia trisphaera

Ungernia trisphaera, commonly known as the three-sphere bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive three-layered floral structure, which gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually blue or purple and bloom in late summer.

Ungernia trisphaera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
