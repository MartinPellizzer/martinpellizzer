Utricularia × pollichii

Utricularia × pollichii, commonly known as the hybrid bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Utricularia aurea and Utricularia gibba, showcasing a combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its small, transparent bladders that trap and digest small aquatic organisms. It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and ditches.

Its unique botanical features make it a fascinating subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
