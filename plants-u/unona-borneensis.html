Unona borneensis

Unona borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean soursop, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces a fleshy, spiny fruit that is typically green when mature. Its botanical features include a smooth, greyish bark and a strong, aromatic scent.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
