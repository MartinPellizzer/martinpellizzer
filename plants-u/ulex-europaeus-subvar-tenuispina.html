Ulex europaeus subvar. tenuispina

Ulex europaeus subvar. tenuispina, commonly known as the narrow-spined hedgehog.

It is a perennial shrub native to Europe, characterized by its spiny, bristly stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in hedgerows and woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and resilient member of the Fabaceae family.
