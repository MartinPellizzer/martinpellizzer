Urginea maritima var. anthericoides

Urginea maritima var. anthericoides, commonly known as the Canary Island star of Africa, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy, night-blooming flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has a robust, fleshy rhizome and produces large, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal environments.
