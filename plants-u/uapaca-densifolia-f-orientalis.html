Uapaca densifolia f. orientalis

Uapaca densifolia f. orientalis, commonly known as the East African velvet bean, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, yellowish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces elongated, bean-like fruits that contain edible seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Due to its hardy nature and nutritional value, it is sometimes cultivated for both ornamental and practical uses.
