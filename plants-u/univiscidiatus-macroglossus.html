Univiscidiatus macroglossus

Univiscidiatus macroglossus, commonly known as the giant swallowtail orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a new shoot at the base. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
