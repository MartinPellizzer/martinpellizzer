Uvaria multiflora

Uvaria multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered uvaria, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

The fruit is a small, smooth, fleshy drupe that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.
