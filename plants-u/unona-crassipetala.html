Unona crassipetala

Unona crassipetala, commonly known as the broad-petaled unona, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, with broad, overlapping petals. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface.

Unona crassipetala is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its traditional use in local herbal medicine.
