Utricularia dunlopii

Utricularia dunlopii, commonly known as the Dunlop's bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus Utricularia, which is characterized by its unique trapping structures called bladders. These bladders are used to capture small aquatic organisms, providing the plant with essential nutrients. The plant typically grows in shallow water and is known for its small, delicate leaves.

Utricularia dunlopii is an epiphytic species, meaning it can grow on the surface of other plants or submerged in water.
