Urochloa pullulans

Urochloa pullulans, commonly known as silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly rolled, with a soft texture.

Urochloa pullulans is valued for its ornamental use in landscaping and as a forage plant for livestock.
