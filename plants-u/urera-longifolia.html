Urera longifolia

Urera longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf urera, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly found in humid lowland forests. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. Urera longifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.
