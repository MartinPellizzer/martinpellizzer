Unona buchananii

Unona buchananii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Unona buchananii is also known for its ability to produce offsets, which can be propagated to grow new plants.
