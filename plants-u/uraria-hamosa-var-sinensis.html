Uraria hamosa var. sinensis

Uraria hamosa var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese uraria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or white. The plant produces cylindrical, dehiscent pods that contain several elliptical seeds. Native to regions of East Asia, it thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
