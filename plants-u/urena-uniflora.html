Urena uniflora

Urena uniflora, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a tropical annual herb native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Urena uniflora is known for its rapid growth and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
