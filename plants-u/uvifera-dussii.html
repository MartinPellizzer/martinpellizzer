Uvifera dussii

Uvifera dussii, commonly known as the Dussis rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Uvifera dussii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
