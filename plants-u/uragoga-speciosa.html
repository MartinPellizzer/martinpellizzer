Uragoga speciosa

Uragoga speciosa, commonly known as the showy uragoga, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to dark purple. The plant produces vibrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the wet season. Uragoga speciosa thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant in horticultural collections.
